Privacy Policy

Version 2.0 · Last updated: July 17, 2026 · Effective: July 17, 2026

Not legal advice. This document explains our practices in plain English. It is not a substitute for legal counsel. If you are relying on this policy for compliance with laws in your jurisdiction, please consult a licensed attorney.
Short version: We collect the minimum data needed to run the tool. Photos in Profile Studio never leave your device. We do not sell your data. We honor GDPR, CCPA, and PIPA data-subject rights. To exercise any right, email evanjones@mdr.net.

1. Who we are (Data Controller)

Evan Jones, operating r5tools.io as an independent developer.

  • Contact: evanjones@mdr.net
  • Location: Alabama, United States
  • Site: https://r5tools.io

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), Evan Jones is the "data controller" for personal data processed through r5tools.io. Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), Evan Jones is the "business." Under Korea's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA), Evan Jones is the "personal information handler."

We do not currently have an EU-based representative under GDPR Article 27 because our processing does not meet the threshold that requires one. If our EU user base grows past that threshold we will appoint one and update this page.

2. What data we collect

Data you give us directly

  • Email address (opt-in) — only if you join the waitlist, subscribe to updates, or use the email magic-link login path.
  • Discord handle (opt-in) — only if you provide it on the waitlist form so we can invite you to the community server.
  • Roster CSVs and coordinates — if you upload alliance rosters or base coordinates to the tools that need them. Stored per alliance signature (SHA-256 hash of your R5 name + top 8 members), separate from your IP address.
  • Discord webhook URLs — if you paste one so a tool can auto-post exports to your alliance chat.
  • Support request contents — anything you type in the Feature Request or Contact widgets.

Data collected automatically

  • IP address — used briefly for rate limiting (retained up to 1 hour); anonymized to the /24 network for longer analytics use.
  • Browser language, country (from IP), user agent — for UI localization and aggregate analytics.
  • Page views and event actions — through Plausible (cookieless) and Google Analytics 4 (with IP anonymization and Google Signals disabled).
  • Referral attribution — if you arrived through a `?ref=CODE` link, the code is stored in a first-party cookie (see Section 5).

Data collected by our payment processor

  • Stripe payment metadata — email, name on card, billing country, last-4 of card, and transaction amount. We never see or store your full card number. Stripe processes this data under its own privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy.

4. How long we keep it (Retention)

  • Newsletter subscribers: retained until you unsubscribe, then a further 2 years for suppression-list purposes (so we do not accidentally re-email you).
  • Stripe payment records: 7 years, to satisfy U.S. tax law.
  • Analytics data: 26 months (Google Analytics 4 default). Plausible aggregates only, no raw retention.
  • Roster / coordinates uploaded to tools: 90 days after last use, then automatically purged.
  • IP address: 1 hour for rate limiting; anonymized after that.
  • Support tickets: 3 years, then archived and stripped of identifiers.

6. Who we share data with (Third parties)

We share the minimum data required for each vendor to do its job. We do not sell data. We do not share for advertising.

  • Stripe — payment processing. Privacy Policy.
  • Plausible Analytics — cookieless site analytics. EU-based, GDPR-compliant. Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics 4 — pageview counts. IP anonymization is on and Google Signals is off. Privacy Policy.
  • SMTP provider — sends transactional and newsletter email (currently Postmark / Amazon SES depending on volume). Data shared: your email address and the message content.
  • Railway — hosts our backend API. U.S.-based infrastructure. Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts our static site. Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare — DNS and edge caching. Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — powers the KB Chat and Roster Extractor. Only your question or roster frame is sent, never your account info. Privacy Policy.

We have Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) with Stripe, Plausible, and Anthropic. We are working on formal DPAs with the remaining vendors and will publish links here as they are executed.

7. International data transfers

Our hosting is primarily in the United States. If you are located in the EEA, UK, or South Korea, your data will be transferred to and processed in the U.S. We rely on the following safeguards:

  • EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework where the vendor is self-certified (Stripe, Google, Cloudflare, GitHub).
  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) under Commission Decision 2021/914 for other transfers.
  • Additional technical measures: encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+), encryption at rest, and minimization of the personal data actually sent abroad.

8. Your rights

Everyone

Regardless of where you live, you can email evanjones@mdr.net to ask us to access, correct, delete, or export your data. We respond within 30 days.

If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland (GDPR / UK GDPR)

  • Right of access — ask what data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten") — ask us to delete your data.
  • Right to restrict processing.
  • Right to data portability — get a JSON export of your data.
  • Right to object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time (does not affect processing already done).
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

If you are in California (CCPA / CPRA)

  • Right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and (if applicable) sell.
  • Right to delete personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to opt out of "sale" or "sharing" — we do not sell or share for cross-context behavioral advertising, so there is nothing to opt out of, but the right exists.
  • Right to limit use of sensitive personal information — we do not collect sensitive personal information as defined by the CPRA.
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising these rights.

If you are in South Korea (PIPA)

  • Right to be informed about processing of your personal information.
  • Right to access your personal information.
  • Right to correct or delete.
  • Right to suspend processing.
  • Right to compensation for damages resulting from a violation.
  • Our designated personal information protection officer is Evan Jones (contact above).

To exercise any right, email evanjones@mdr.net. We may ask you to verify your identity before we act (typically by confirming the email address linked to your account or the access code you used).

9. Children

r5tools.io is a companion tool for a mobile military strategy game. It is not directed at children under 13 (or under 14 in Korea, or under 16 in some EEA member states). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, email evanjones@mdr.net and we will delete it.

10. Security

  • All traffic between your browser and our servers uses TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Access codes are stored as hashes, not plaintext.
  • Roster uploads are stored with per-alliance signatures separate from your IP address.
  • Photos processed in Profile Studio never leave your device.
  • We publish a bug bounty policy at /trust.html and welcome responsible disclosure.

No system is perfectly secure. If we discover a breach involving your personal data, we will notify you within 72 hours as required by GDPR Article 33 (and equivalent laws in your jurisdiction).

11. Automated decision-making

We do not use your personal data for automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects. The AI features (KB Chat, Roster Extractor) generate text or extract data but do not make decisions about you.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. For material changes we will notify you at least 30 days in advance by email (if we have your address) and by an in-tool banner. The current version is always at r5tools.io/privacy.html. Older versions are kept in our Git history and available on request.

13. Contact

Questions, requests, or complaints: evanjones@mdr.net. Usual response time: within a few days.
