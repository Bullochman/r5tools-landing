메타 현황 (2026 출처 발췌)

히어로 in 라스트 워: 서바이벌 are the single largest lever on squad power. There are three combat types — 탱크 (탱크), 항공기 (전투기), and 미사일 차량 (미사일) — in a rock-paper-scissors: 탱크 > 미사일, 미사일 > 항공기, 항공기 > 탱크, at roughly ±20% damage (see 04-squads-combat for the damage-formula math). 히어로 exist at four rarities — R, SR, SSR, UR — with SSR→UR promotion available for one designated hero per season (메이슨 S1, 바이올렛 S2, 스칼렛 S3, 사라 S4, 베놈 S5, 브라즈 S6). Every hero has four skills (자동 공격, 전술/전술, 패시브, 특기), reaches 5★ with 975 shards, and unlocks progression tiers at 1★/2★/3★/5★. 시즌 6 (Shadow/잃어버린 열대우림, launched 2026-04-13) introduced 히어로 각성, which replaces the "전용 무기" system for new UR seasonal releases and adds a 4th-skill overlay to legacy heroes (킴벌리 Week 1, DVA Week 3, 테슬라 Week 5). The current meta is 탱크-dominant, built around 킴벌리 (UR AoE carry) with 윌리엄스/머피 as anchors and 마샬 as the only UR support in the game.

Slot positioning matters: 전열 (slots 1–2) takes physical damage first and needs tanks; 후열 (slots 3–5) should hold DPS and support. 윌리엄스 and 머피 are the two mandatory front-row tanks in nearly every meta comp. 킴벌리, DVA, 테슬라, and 마샬 sit in the 후열.

The dominant 2026 competitive 랠리 formation across every major server cluster is a 탱크-lead hybrid built around the 킴벌리 / DVA burst window. Community consensus from four independent 2026 guides (LW Spy, Packsify, Last War Handbook, LDShop) converges on the following five-hero cores:

S-tier / meta 랠리 comp (탱크 formation, 4+1 hybrid):

  • 윌리엄스 (front-center) — 윌리엄스's damage-reduction passive only applies to the whole formation when he is in the front-center slot; placed elsewhere it applies only to adjacent heroes. This positioning rule is the #1 pilot error in the meta comp per LW Spy [lw-spy.com/blog/last-war-best-hero-formation-2026, 2026].
  • 킴벌리 (후열) — main AoE-DPS carry.
  • DVA (후열, opposite side from 킴벌리) — maximizes the aircraft-pair stacking interaction; DVA and 킴벌리's active-skill windows overlap for burst that "frequently exceeds what any other DPS pair in the game can produce" [lw-spy.com, 2026].
  • 스카일러 (SR) — passive accelerates the aircraft squad's skill cooldown by 18%, meaning the burst window comes around ~1.3× per fight instead of once [lw-spy.com, 2026].
  • 머피 or 피오나 (flex slot) — 머피 for 랠리-survivability, 피오나 for 랠리-DPS. 얼라이언스 meta dictates which.

S-tier all-purpose (pure 탱크 mono, 마샬 support):

  • 윌리엄스 / 킴벌리 / 마샬 / DVA / 스테트만 — hits the 4-탱크 formation bonus while using 마샬 to amplify. "The 4 same-type 탱크s plus 마샬 produces both the formation buff (+20% ATK/HP) and support amplification — this is the single highest-output composition a competitive account can field" [ldshop.gg 킴벌리 build guide, 2026; packsify.com best-squad-lineup, 2026].

항공기 mono (pure formation, S6 late-game):

  • 루시우스 / 칼리 / 모리슨 / 스카일러 / DVA — 칼리 is "the only 항공기-type tank and makes the formation viable"; DVA "runs at full 항공기 formation bonus in this lineup, producing her highest effective output" [ldshop.gg aircraft-hero-skills, 2026]. Note: 킴벌리 is NOT part of this comp — she's a 탱크. Common newbie confusion because her Barrage Strike visual looks aerial.

Canonical opener sequences [lastwarhandbook.com, 2026]:

  • 탱크 squad: 킴벌리 Barrage Strike → 머피 철clad 방벽 → 마샬 Command Strategy.
  • 항공기 squad: DVA Steel Barrage → Vortex 미사일 → 칼리 Energy Adaptation.

무과금 hero investment priority order (Wardawgg, Packsify, LDShop 2026 consensus):

  1. 킴벌리 to 5★ — "she's your main carry and nothing else matters until she's maxed" [ldshop.gg best-f2p-tank-teams, 2026]. Also the safest investment in the game (useful across every mode).
  2. 마샬 alongside 킴벌리 — global ATK amplifier. 마샬's Command Strategy (1군 skill) boosts entire squad ATK for 8s.
  3. DVA — second hero built by most whale accounts; single-target burst partner to 킴벌리.
  4. 머피 — 무과금 starter UR, frontline anchor. Every account gets him free.
  5. 메이슨 (SSR→UR promotion) — 무과금 탱크 DPS with the highest promotion ROI (shards drop from long-standing farm sources at Normal Stage 4-3).
  6. Everyone else is optional until 1–5 are 5★.
