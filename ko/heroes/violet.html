메타 현황 (2026 출처 발췌)

히어로 in 라스트 워: 서바이벌 are the single largest lever on squad power. There are three combat types — 탱크 (탱크), 항공기 (전투기), and 미사일 차량 (미사일) — in a rock-paper-scissors: 탱크 > 미사일, 미사일 > 항공기, 항공기 > 탱크, at roughly ±20% damage (see 04-squads-combat for the damage-formula math). 히어로 exist at four rarities — R, SR, SSR, UR — with SSR→UR promotion available for one designated hero per season (메이슨 S1, 바이올렛 S2, 스칼렛 S3, 사라 S4, 베놈 S5, 브라즈 S6). Every hero has four skills (자동 공격, 전술/전술, 패시브, 특기), reaches 5★ with 975 shards, and unlocks progression tiers at 1★/2★/3★/5★. 시즌 6 (Shadow/잃어버린 열대우림, launched 2026-04-13) introduced 히어로 각성, which replaces the "전용 무기" system for new UR seasonal releases and adds a 4th-skill overlay to legacy heroes (킴벌리 Week 1, DVA Week 3, 테슬라 Week 5). The current meta is 탱크-dominant, built around 킴벌리 (UR AoE carry) with 윌리엄스/머피 as anchors and 마샬 as the only UR support in the game.

  • R (레어, common) — starter units. Barely used past the tutorial. Nomenclature varies; some sources call them "Common."
  • SR (Super 레어, blue) — four heroes total, all damage dealers: 앰볼트, 검프, 케인, 로키. 로키 is the strongest of the group thanks to a shotgun burst kit; 앰볼트 is usually the first 항공기 hero every account touches. 레어ly competitive past HQ 20 but useful for filler slots, event side-teams, and early campaign clears. Levelable with 레어 히어로 만능조각s, which are the cheapest and most plentiful shard type.
  • SSR (에픽, purple) — the mid-tier bulk of the roster (13+ heroes). Includes 메이슨, 바이올렛, 스칼렛, 사라, 베놈, 브라즈, 케이지, 엘사, 맥스웰, 모니카, 리처드, Farhad, 케인 (SR), and others. Six of these are "SSR→UR promotion" candidates (see below). A 5★ SSR gene랠리 out-performs a 3★ UR, so SSRs are the 무과금 bridge until UR heroes cross 4★.
  • UR (전설, orange/red) — the endgame roster. 15+ heroes as of 시즌 6, one added per season on average. Divided across 탱크, 항공기, and 미사일. 머피 is the only free-to-play UR who begins in every account's inventory; every other UR requires either shards from 전설 모집, seasonal battle passes, or SSR→UR promotion.

조각 sources (per Theria Games, LDShop, Cpt Hedge):

  • 술집 — daily free recruits (mostly SR fodder, occasional SSR).
  • 엘리트 모집 — SSR-guaranteed with SSR + UR shard mix.
  • Advanced (전설) 모집 — UR-focused pool with ~1% UR rate, ~4% SSR rate.
  • 소원 — spend 200 wish tickets → 10 shards of a specific named hero. Primary way to farm a target UR without RNG.
  • Campaign farming — targeted SSR shards drop from specific stages. 메이슨 (S1 promotion candidate) drops at Normal Stage 4-3 (1–2 per clear); 바이올렛 (S2) drops at Normal Stage 8-5 (1–3 per clear).
  • Trade & Plunder — attacking trucks yields ~2 shards each.
  • Honor Store, Campaign Store, 연맹 상점 — periodic hero shard rotations.
  • Battle Pass — every season includes a Growth / 히어로 pass with named UR shards on tracks.
  • 레이더 & event stores — 무기 경쟁, 얼라이언스 듀얼s, 수도 War all pay out shard bundles as milestones.
  • Black Market (post-season) — currency accumulated over the season is spent here for shard choice boxes; the primary 무과금 long-tail source.

The only universally-endorsed promotion is 메이슨 (his 탱크 DPS is genuinely competitive in mono-탱크 comps and shards drop from long-standing farm sources). 바이올렛 is conditional — worthwhile if she's already in your active squad, wasteful otherwise. Community consensus (Packsify, Handbook, Tap-가이드) is to skip 스칼렛, 사라, 베놈, and 브라즈 promotions unless you specifically need that seasonal comp.

Per Lootbar's 명예의 벽 guide, published bonus types per hero:

  • 메이슨: +0.50% ATK + HP (UR-eligible)
  • 케이지: +0.50% Defense
  • 루시우스: +0.50% Defense
  • 칼리: +0.50% Health
  • 테슬라: +0.25% Attack
  • DVA: +0.50% Defense
  • 모리슨: +0.50% Attack
  • 스카일러: +0.50% Attack
  • 바이올렛: +0.50% ATK + HP (UR-eligible)
  • 맥스웰: +0.25% Attack
  • 피오나: +0.50% Attack
  • 스위프트: +0.50% Attack
  • 킴벌리: +0.50% Attack [Last War Vault 명예의 벽 가이드, 2026-07-15; corroborated by lastwar.wiki 명예의 벽 page, 2026-07-15 — corrects a prior KB entry that read "+0.50% Defense", which was a KB transcription error; 킴벌리 is the flagship AoE-DPS 탱크 and grants the strongest tank Attack passive in the 명예의 벽]
  • 스칼렛: +0.50% ATK + HP (UR-eligible)

2026-07-15 cross-check of 탱크-hero bonuses — Last War Vault's 명예의 벽 guide (티어 B) publishes the full 탱크 block as: 킴벌리 / 메이슨 (UR) → +0.50% Attack; 마샬 / 스테트만 → +0.50% Defense; 머피 / 윌리엄스 / 바이올렛 (UR) → +0.50% Health; 로키 / 검프 → +1.00% Troop Load. lastwar.wiki confirms 킴벌리 = Attack. This resolves the "킴벌리 = Defense" question flagged in the KB's Contradictions section.

훈장 refund reminder: SSR → UR promotion refunds 100% of pre-promotion medals. This is what makes early SSR-medal investment safe on promotion-eligible heroes (메이슨, 바이올렛, 스칼렛, 사라, 베놈, 브라즈) — but ONLY if you actually plan to execute the promotion.

  • No level cap — every extra shard past 5★ compounds indefinitely.
  • Account-wide passive — bonuses apply whether the hero is fielded or benched.
  • Refund on promotion — SSR → UR promotion refunds shards spent on 방벽 entries for that hero, so investing 방벽 levels into 메이슨/바이올렛/etc. is zero-risk if you plan to promote them [lootbar.com wall-of-honor guide, 2026].
  • Every 50 방벽 levels the per-tier bonus increases by +0.25% to +1.0% — meaning a hero at 방벽 Lv.100 has a materially stronger bonus than at Lv.50, and Lv.250 stronger still [lootbar.com, 2026; heaven-guardian.com wall-of-honor guide, 2026].

Banner rotation: rate-ups are announced ~1 week ahead in the in-game news. Rotation is season-dependent — the "히어로's Return" pool re-features promotion-eligible SSRs matching each season (메이슨 S1, 바이올렛 S2, 스칼렛 S3, etc.). Some passes were removed in a 2026 monetisation update ("각성 Battle Pass is gone" per Last War Vault).
