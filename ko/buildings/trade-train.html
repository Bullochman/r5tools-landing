라스트 워: 서바이벌 runs on an 8-week seasonal treadmill: every server rotates through a fresh themed map with new resources, buildings, PvP events, and hero/weapon rollouts. Since 시즌 2 (폴라 스톰) ended, 퍼스트펀 has shipped four full seasons — S3 골든 킹덤 (Egyptian desert / 향신료 전쟁 / Curse Resistance), S4 에버나이트 아일 (Sakura island / 등대 / 블러드 나이트), S5 와일드 웨스트 (시즌 5 / Banks / 무역 열차 / merged 8-워존 map), and S6 그림자 열대우림 (faction warfare / permanent city destruction / 히어로 각성). 시즌 7 is confirmed by community creators as launching approximately August 2026 but no official theme/mechanic details have leaked publicly at the time of this research (2026-07-14). Each season introduces a new seasonal resource loop, a new PvP conflict pattern, and a designated hero for UR ascension or 각성 — and the trend from S5→S6 is toward fewer, longer war windows and permanent, high-stakes territorial outcomes rather than continuous grind (documented in 13-meta-competitive).

무역 열차: A train continuously travels across the continent, stopping at multiple stations. Players consign 위스키 for 크리스탈골드 profit — or raid competitors' trades for rewards. Introduces cross-얼라이언스 economic warfare not previously in the game.

  • 02-heroes — Every season promotes one SSR to UR. Since S6, three heroes per season also gain 히어로 각성. Named vs Universal 각성 조각s are the new currency; hero tier-list volatility now depends on which heroes are in the 각성 rotation.
  • 03-chief — 치프 profession cap extended from 60 → 100 in S3, retained through S6. 외교관 unavailable in S3/S4 (unconfirmed if unlocked later). New seasonal skills (Sandworm Traps, Trap Landmines, Nature's Touch, etc.) tie to season-specific PvE.
  • 04-squads-combat — Tactics Cards debuted in S4; card meta shifted hard in S6 with the removal of the 4+1 hybrid squad strategy from 글로벌 익스페디션, forcing pure 탱크/미사일/항공기 formations. Curse Resistance / 바이러스 저항 (VR) stats are new gate mechanics for PvE damage that stack over standard stats.
  • 05-season-1-crimson-plague — S1 크림슨 플레이그 introduced T10 troops. 시즌 structure since S3 has become more elaborate but retains the S1 template of themed map + seasonal resources + 수도/faction endgame.
  • 06-season-2-frozen — S2 폴라 스톰 was the first true themed environmental hazard (cold/heat). S3+ retained the "seasonal building trinity" model (resource-producer + shop-currency-producer + resistance-lab). 얼라이언스 화로 pattern from S2 evolves into 축복의 샘 (S3) / 등대 (S4) / 은행 요새 (S5) / 제단s (S6). See STYLE_GUIDE for how the 라워_얼라이언스_Ops_Center suite handles seasonal building overlays.
  • 08-얼라이언스-systems — 얼라이언스 협약s (S6), 얼라이언스 세이프 타임 (S5+), and 파벌 기술 (S6) reshape how 얼라이언스s coordinate at the meta level. 얼라이언스 city/stronghold caps evolve per season (S3 = 8/8; S4 = 6/6; S5 Bank = 4 default up to 12; S6 factional).
  • 09-events — 블러드 나이트 강림 (S4, 02:30/10:30/18:30 server), 모래벌레 위기 (S3, 14 days), 향신료 전쟁 (S3, biweekly), 구리 전쟁 (S4, Wed/Sat), 무역 열차 (S5), 제단 Tuesdays (S6), 파벌 대전 (season-ending). Each season adds 2–3 new recurring events.
  • 10-economy — 시즌 Battle Pass (~48 days, ~€11.99 Advanced / ~€23.99 Luxury) is the shipped pricing anchor since S3. Weekly Pass adds a temporary seasonal building + resources. Three exclusive weapon passes per season (Weeks 1/3/6). 히어로 각성 shards are now a paid axis on top of exclusive weapons.
  • 14-ui-reference — 시즌 transitions require map re-render, seasonal building rendering, and faction color coding (S6 Deepwood green / Wetland blue / Great River neutral). 얼라이언스 세이프 타임 UI added in S5.
  • 15-glossary — Every season has full KR terminology; namu.wiki maintains a running 한국어-language reference. Faction names (카게노사토, 코부타이 in S4; Deepwood/Wetland in S6) are transliterated from EN in KR sources rather than re-localized.
  • 02-heroes — Certain SSR/UR heroes carry gathering-speed, load-capacity, or resource-production bonuses on their base skills or exclusive weapons. 킴벌리, 사라, Molly, and various season-featured heroes are commonly cited as "farming leads" whose exclusive-weapon L20 team-boost provides economy gain in addition to combat. The 치프 profession's Economy tree parallels this on the player side.
  • 03-chief — VIP levels, profession XP, and exclusive weapon Lv 1–30 all sit in the 치프 system and directly modify economy production, gathering speed, and load capacity. VIP 타임 consumption pattern and VIP-8/VIP-11 milestone hunt are 치프-progression decisions.
  • 05-season-1-crimson-plague — 시즌 1 does not introduce new base resources; economy runs on 식량/철/골드 only. 시즌 medals and 시즌 1-specific event currencies are separate loops. 워존 결투 adds a plunder cadence but not a new resource type.
  • 06-season-2-frozen — The economy expands to include 석탄, 티타늄 합금, 희토 for the duration of the season. 고열 화로 and 얼라이언스 화로 are new 석탄 sinks. 희토 ranks 얼라이언스s at season end. Off-season, only the base four resources persist.
  • 04-squads-combat — Troop tier is the primary determinant of both load capacity (for gathering and plundering) and combat casualties (which is a resource sink via healing costs). T10/T11 unlocks are gated on 오일 availability from HQ 31+ and 골드 availability throughout. 병원 + 응급 센터 (ICU) sizes cap the food/iron/coin loss per major PvP encounter.
  • 08-얼라이언스-systems — 연맹 기술, 연맹 상점, 돕기, 얼라이언스 화로, 얼라이언스 Train, 집결지 are all economy-adjacent. 권장 tech +20% bonus and 30 daily donations are the daily economy anchor.
  • 09-events — 무기 경쟁, 얼라이언스 듀얼, Duel VS, Mutant Spawn, 파멸의 정예, and monthly events are the primary speedup and diamond inflow. Speedup-hoard-then-spend-during-Arms-Race is the single largest 무과금 economy multiplier.
  • 11-monetization — Diamond and 골드 Brick spending decisions all reduce to "how many resources / speedups does this pack buy?" — the economy is the denominator for every ROI calc.
  • 12-progression — 나사s, Blueprints, and 광석 are the crafting-side resources sitting alongside the four permanent resources; 12-progression documents the crafting economy proper.
  • 07-future-seasons — Every season adds one new economy loop (S3 향신료, S4 코퍼 + Sakura, S5 골드en + 무역 열차, S6 Faction currency). Off-season, only the base four resources persist.
