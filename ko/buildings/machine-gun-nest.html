The overlap with game titles is common — e.g. the 워로드 title (+5% MG 랠리, S6 세르펜트 브레스) is usually assigned to the same person who acts as War Coordinator. But they don't have to match: an R5 might title one R4 as "워로드" for the S6 skill activation timing while giving the War Coordinator hat to a different R4 who is online during Enemy Buster. The 라워_얼라이언스_Ops_Center Ministry Assigner tool (planned per TOOLS_ROADMAP) separates the two dimensions explicitly.

Auto-fill / 자동 집결 interaction. 방어군 Defenders effectively double as "auto-fill donors" for Marshal's Guard — the 30-second auto-fill kicks in on offline teammates' garrisoned troops, so a Defender who keeps a full reinforcement stack in Ring 1 leaders' 연맹 센터s is contributing to every MG 랠리 launched from those bases even while asleep [lastwargame.online/en/얼라이언스-exercise-2/ 티어 B].
