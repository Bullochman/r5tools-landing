"God-tier" 워로드 skills (per community consensus across LDShop/Joytify/LastWarTutorial):

  • 팀 스트라이크 — 랠리 damage multiplier. Non-negotiable if you 랠리.
  • 부대 동원 — free tier-max squad daily.
  • 급성 치료 — massive hospital heal on 71.5-h cooldown.
  • 공성 강화 — flat +% siege damage.
  • 행군 in Badlands — critical for 시즌 5 와일드 웨스트 and 수도 Conquest.

워로드 phased build (Joytify Phase-1/2/3 model):

  • Phase 1 (Lv 1–20, "get playing") — 신속 귀환 → 부대 동원 → Drill 지면 온도 Prep → 병원 Prep → Craze. Goal: daily free tier-max squad + hospital + capacity.
  • Phase 2 (Lv 20–35, "get killing") — 팀 스트라이크 (max first — 랠리-damage multiplier is the highest single-skill combat multiplier in the tree) → Winning Pursuit → Speedup 증원.
  • Phase 3 (Lv 35+, "get sieging") — 공성 강화 (max) → 급성 치료 → Fortify Defense → Charge Banner. For Wild-West/수도-Conquest seasons add 행군 in Badlands.

Healing is a resource conversion that most players underestimate. Every wounded troop the 병원 heals costs 식량 + 철 + 골드 (per-troop-tier costs; higher tiers cost dramatically more). Full formula:

  • 부상 병력 = min(Casualties, 병원 Capacity)
  • Dead 병력 = Casualties − 병원 Capacity (only if Casualties > Capacity)

The 응급 센터 (응급 센터 (ICU)) unlocks at HQ 22 and catches the would-be-dead overflow at a starting 중상 Unit Ratio of 21% — meaning if your 병원 caps out, 21% of the overflow is saved (still requires healing) and 79% is permanently lost. 응급 센터 중상 Ratio scales with level. 6회 갱신 규칙Six-Refresh RuleUR truck-plunder heuristic
  • 02-heroes — Certain SSR/UR heroes carry gathering-speed, load-capacity, or resource-production bonuses on their base skills or exclusive weapons. 킴벌리, 사라, Molly, and various season-featured heroes are commonly cited as "farming leads" whose exclusive-weapon L20 team-boost provides economy gain in addition to combat. The 치프 profession's Economy tree parallels this on the player side.
  • 03-chief — VIP levels, profession XP, and exclusive weapon Lv 1–30 all sit in the 치프 system and directly modify economy production, gathering speed, and load capacity. VIP 타임 consumption pattern and VIP-8/VIP-11 milestone hunt are 치프-progression decisions.
  • 05-season-1-crimson-plague — 시즌 1 does not introduce new base resources; economy runs on 식량/철/골드 only. 시즌 medals and 시즌 1-specific event currencies are separate loops. 워존 결투 adds a plunder cadence but not a new resource type.
  • 06-season-2-frozen — The economy expands to include 석탄, 티타늄 합금, 희토 for the duration of the season. 고열 화로 and 얼라이언스 화로 are new 석탄 sinks. 희토 ranks 얼라이언스s at season end. Off-season, only the base four resources persist.
  • 04-squads-combat — Troop tier is the primary determinant of both load capacity (for gathering and plundering) and combat casualties (which is a resource sink via healing costs). T10/T11 unlocks are gated on 오일 availability from HQ 31+ and 골드 availability throughout. 병원 + 응급 센터 (ICU) sizes cap the food/iron/coin loss per major PvP encounter.
  • 08-얼라이언스-systems — 연맹 기술, 연맹 상점, 돕기, 얼라이언스 화로, 얼라이언스 Train, 집결지 are all economy-adjacent. 권장 tech +20% bonus and 30 daily donations are the daily economy anchor.
  • 09-events — 무기 경쟁, 얼라이언스 듀얼, Duel VS, Mutant Spawn, 파멸의 정예, and monthly events are the primary speedup and diamond inflow. Speedup-hoard-then-spend-during-Arms-Race is the single largest 무과금 economy multiplier.
  • 11-monetization — Diamond and 골드 Brick spending decisions all reduce to "how many resources / speedups does this pack buy?" — the economy is the denominator for every ROI calc.
  • 12-progression — 나사s, Blueprints, and 광석 are the crafting-side resources sitting alongside the four permanent resources; 12-progression documents the crafting economy proper.
  • 07-future-seasons — Every season adds one new economy loop (S3 향신료, S4 코퍼 + Sakura, S5 골드en + 무역 열차, S6 Faction currency). Off-season, only the base four resources persist.
