우리 얼라이언스 잠금 해제 →

라스트 워: 서바이벌's economy runs on four permanent resources — 식량, 철, 골드 (gold), and 오일 — plus two season-locked resources (석탄 + 티타늄 합금 in 시즌 2 폴라 스톰) and a ranking resource per season (희토 in S2). The economy is not "electricity" or "bread/water" style; those are community coinages for 식량 and 골드. 골드 is the hidden late-game bottleneck: it becomes required from 골드 광산 L9 and 오일 becomes required for every HQ upgrade from HQ 31–35. 자원s are produced by dedicated buildings (농장land, 철광, 골드 광산, 오일 Well, Smelter) that scale from ~1,440/hr at L1 to ~33,840/hr at L30 for basic resources and cap at 12 h of unclaimed production (39,600/hr at L35 for 농장land and 철광, 21,600/hr at L30 for 골드 광산, 1,800/hr at L30 for 오일 Well). Warehouses (식량 창고, 철 창고, 골드 창고) define a protected threshold — anything at or below is unraidable; anything above is exposed to plunder proportional to the attacker's troop load capacity and the target's stored ratio (see 01-core-gameplay for warehouse building basics and 04-squads-combat for load-capacity mechanics). On top of raw resources sit 6 speedup types (universal, construction, research, training, healing, hero-medal), the Diamond premium currency (used for VIP time, stamina, shields, VIP-shop hero shards), and 골드 Bricks — a super-premium tier above diamonds gated behind top-up. Every serious player runs continuous Development + Economy research, does 30 daily 20-min coin donations to the 권장 얼라이언스 tech, dispatches 4 Intercity Trade Trucks/day, and hoards speedups for 무기 경쟁 + 얼라이언스 듀얼 phase overlap so a single build completion double-scores.

  1. 식량 (식량 / sikryang) — produced by 농장land. Consumed by every building upgrade, every troop training queue, and every healing action. Community shorthand "bread" or "water" is nonstandard — the in-game 영어 label is simply 식량. [lastwar.wiki, lastwargame.online, lastwarhub.com]
  2. 철 (강철 / gangcheol, sometimes 철 / cheol) — produced by 철광. Same universal consumer profile as 식량; usually the second-largest expense after 식량 for building upgrades. [lastwar.wiki iron-mine page]
  3. 골드 / 골드 (금화 / geumhwa) — produced by 골드 광산. First becomes required at 골드 광산 building level 9, and is the primary bottleneck for T10/T11 troop training in late game because the coin cost per elite recruit dwarfs the food+iron cost. [lastwar.wiki gold-mine, lastwarhandbook.com resource-management] 골드 is the sink most veteran R5s report as their limiting reagent — food/iron overflow, coin runs dry.
  4. 오일 (석유 / seogyu) — introduced with the Age of 오일 research tree post-시즌-2, produced by 오일 Wells (up to 3 concurrent). Becomes mandatory as a tertiary resource for HQ 31→35 upgrades. Even a coin-and-food-rich account is stalled at HQ 30→31 without accumulated oil. [lastwartutorial.com Age of 오일, lastwarhandbook.com Age of 오일, cpt-hedge.com 오일 Well]

Levels 31–35 for 농장land and 철광 unlock only after the HQ Expansion research in the Age of 오일 tree. 골드 광산's max is L30 in every published table; there is no confirmed L31–35 for the 골드 광산.

  1. Assigned 생존자 — every production building has a survivor slot. A purple (에픽) survivor gives roughly +32% output; 전설 survivors more; star-level upgrades amplify further. A well-tuned 철광 L23 with a strong 전설 survivor "can produce over twice" its 16,560/hr base rate. [tap-guides.com, lootbar.com]
  2. VIP 타임 boosts — while VIP 타임 is active, VIP-level-based percent bonuses apply. VIP 8 unlocks +20% 식량 / 철 / 골드 production; higher levels stack more. VIP 타임 is a consumable buff, not a permanent unlock — the level is permanent but the bonus only applies while VIP 타임 is running. [ldshop.gg VIP guide, packsify.com]
  3. Economy 연맹 기술 nodes — the 식량 Production, 철 Production, 골드 Production, and later 오일 Production research nodes stack additive % boosts. Individually small (couple percent per rank) but the sum across all economy nodes plus survivor plus VIP can more than triple base output at endgame. [packsify.com research-guide, ldshop.gg tech-tree-guide]
  • 오일 Well unlocks first via the "해금 오일 Well" research. L1 → L30 spans ~49 days total build time at base values (before Development speed buffs), consuming ~212M 식량, 638M 철, 137M 골드 at max.
  • 오일 becomes required for HQ 31, HQ 32, HQ 33, HQ 34, HQ 35 upgrades. Without oil, everything else is stalled.
  • 농장land and 철광 unlock L31–35 only after "HQ Expansion" completes in the Age of 오일 tree.
  • 1군 and 3rd 오일 Wells unlock via mid/late Age of 오일 research.
  • Reaching HQ 35 무과금 is late 시즌 4 at the earliest — 6–8 months of focused progression.
