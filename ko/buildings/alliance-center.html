  • Level 5: 미스릴 티타늄 합금 공장 (34,560/hour)
  • Level 10: Exploratory Well (600 오일/hour)

모래벌레 위기 event (14 days): Combat between 얼라이언스s generates "shock waves"; accumulated shock waves spawn:

  • 대형 모래벌레s — knock bases outside 연맹 센터 range to distant map locations (forced teleport)
  • Giant Sandworms — map-wide hazards, trapped via the Trap Landmine profession skill

향신료 전쟁 (Weeks 4–8): Two factions battle twice weekly across stages — Waiting → Declaration → Invitation → Preparation → War → End. Attackers can steal up to 30% of enemy 향신료 by destroying secondary buildings (5% each) and the 연맹 센터 (10%).

구리 전쟁 — the main PvP event. 얼라이언스s declare on enemy 연맹 센터s. Key rules change from S3: only 3 secondary buildings per 연맹 센터; declaration range narrows weekly; minimum 11-tile spacing between 연맹 센터s.

구리 전쟁 — full mechanics [lastwartutorial (strategy guide), 2026-07-15]:

  • 8 total rounds across W4–W7 (2/week, Wed + Sat).
  • 카게노사토 starts on attack, 코부타이 starts on defense. Sides alternate round-to-round — each faction ends with 4 attacking + 4 defending battles.
  • Declaration window: 24 hours in the Declaration stage. R4+ in the attacking faction can declare on 1 target 얼라이언스 in the same 코퍼 ranking group. Up to 3 attacking 얼라이언스s may declare on the same target; target changeable until stage ends.
  • 코퍼 ranking groups: 얼라이언스s sorted by copper holdings into 4 tiers (1–10, 11–20, 21–30, 31–50). The Group 1 range narrows across the season: 10 얼라이언스s (W4) → 6 (W5) → 3 (W6–W7). Concentrates matchmaking among top 얼라이언스s late-season.
  • 코퍼 distribution formula: attackers plunder a percentage per building destroyed. Secondary buildings (코퍼 / 오일 / 코퍼 Warehouse) = 3% each; 연맹 센터 = additional 6%. Max plunder per round: 15% of defender's 코퍼. Multiple attackers share the plunder proportionally by damage inflicted.
  • 여신 role redesign: the 여신 얼라이언스-skill was removed and replaced by the 테슬라 Coil 집사 스킬 — defensive ability, deployable only by the 집사 during preparation and war phases.
  • NOTE: Earlier KB draft cited "30% steal" (S3 향신료 전쟁 carryover). 시즌 4 구리 전쟁 cap is 15%, not 30% — a meaningful reduction from S3.

연맹 센터 placement — exact rule [lastwartutorial (strategy guide), 2026-07-15]: minimum 11-tile spacing measured center-to-center between 연맹 센터s. 재배치 spots are attackable during the pre-100%-completion window — a known 코퍼-War exploit for aggressive positioning.

Week 1 — build-out & first PvE. [lastwartutorial S3 W1, 2026-07-15]

  • Day 1: Protector's 농장, 저주 연구실, and 축복의 샘 unlock. Purge Action (7-day zombie-killing leaderboard) and Digging Stronghold Clash (13-day) launch. 모래벌레 위기 14-day event launches. Recommendation from guides: level Protector's 농장 only to 10 initially — levels 11+ demand 미스릴 and 신성수 needed elsewhere.
  • Day 2: 연맹 센터 unlocks; R4/R5 initiate repairs.
  • Day 3 + 12h: Level 1 cities unlock for 도시 Clash.
  • Day 4: 마샬 전용 무기 7-day battle pass opens (티어-5 마샬 required to equip; 70 shards in pass, 50 needed for activation).
  • Day 3: Build an Oasis event begins — a 48-day long-term event running to end of season.
  • Day 6 + 12h: Level 2 cities unlock. [lastwartutorial S3 W1, 2026-07-15]
  1. Declaration Stage — attacking 얼라이언스 R4s select defending opponents in their ranking group. Each defender faces up to 3 attackers; defender may invite up to 2 allied reinforcements.
  2. Invitation Stage — defending 얼라이언스 recruits backup; reinforcement 얼라이언스s vote to accept via R3 members.
  3. Preparation Stage — final organization; no strategy changes permitted afterwards.
  4. War Stage — combat. Attackers must destroy the defending 연맹 센터 before timer expires to plunder 향신료. Defenders win if AC survives.

향신료 War economics:

  • 연맹 센터 destroyed = attacker takes 10% of defender's 향신료.
  • Each secondary building destroyed = attacker takes 5% (4 pillars: 신성수 Hut, Exploratory Well, 미스릴 티타늄 합금 공장, Obelisk).
  • Cap: 30% maximum per 향신료 War (destroy AC + 4 pillars = 10 + 4×5 = 30%). This is the origin of the "30%" number quoted in the main S3 section above.
  • Defending 얼라이언스s with fewer participants receive up to 3× reward multiplier — this incentivizes defending against overwhelming odds. [medievalfun 향신료 War, 2026-07-15]
