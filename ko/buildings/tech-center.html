우리 얼라이언스 잠금 해제 →
  • Avatar frames — Cosmetic frames on the player avatar. Sources include 시즌 events, Console Contest (S6 champion frames), Faction War ranks, monthly pass, and the 골드 Brick Radiance event. Frames are largely time-limited (often 7-day expiries on competition rewards).
  • Titles / 치프 tags — Awarded through event rankings (e.g. 얼라이언스 듀얼 VS top rankers), faction leadership positions, and top-100 킬 이벤트 rankers on a server. No published "collector list."
  • 용맹 배지s — Not a title. This is a tech-research currency for the 과학 센터's 얼라이언스 듀얼, 특수부대 (T10 path), Intercity Truck, Siege Warfare, and Defense Fortification research trees. Sources: 얼라이언스 듀얼 VS event chests (50→400 badges), 워존 VS victories (~200), 반짝이는 시장. Costs range from 50 badges (early nodes) to 20,000+ (late 특수부대). This is what most EN "chief badge" search results actually surface.
  • 스킬 훈장s — Not chief-owned; hero-owned currency used to level hero skills. Sourced primarily from 무기 경쟁 (up to 8,000/day at gold-chest ceiling). 스킬 medals refund fully when a hero is UR-promoted (SSR → UR), enabling free re-allocation.
