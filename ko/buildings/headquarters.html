  • Purpose: raises your own base temperature and grants 바이러스 저항 (VR) (VR) used against 파멸의 정예s, 폴라 비스트s, and 둠 워커s.
  • 해금: 본부 level 30 (some sources report available at season start; treat HQ30 as the prerequisite). [theriagames.com High Heat 화로 가이드]
  • Max level: 30, upgraded independently of HQ level.
  • Fuel: consumes 석탄 continuously while active.
  • Modes:
  • Off — no heat, no coal.
  • Normal (Activate) — steady coal burn, moderate heat.
  • 오버드라이브 — ~4× coal burn, much higher heat output. Use during blizzards; return to Normal when the storm ends or coal reserves will be gone within hours. [ldshop.gg / lastwarguide.net]
  • Heat output curve:
  • L1: +5.5 °C activation / +8 °C overdrive
  • L19 (community example): +14.5 °C / +21.5 °C
  • L30: +20 °C / +30 °C
  • 바이러스 저항 (VR) curve: L1 = 100 VR, L30 = 10,000 VR. VR is required to attack higher-level 폴라 비스트s and 파멸의 정예s; when 랠리ing, the 랠리 leader's VR applies to the whole 랠리 unless a member has higher. [lastwartutorial.com]
