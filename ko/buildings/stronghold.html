얼라이언스 capture limits: 2 city war declarations/day, max 8 captured cities; 2 digging stronghold captures/day, max 8 captured.

Digging Stronghold 코퍼 production by level [lastwartutorial, 2026-07-15]: L1 100/h → L2 120/h → L3 140/h → L4 160/h → L5 180/h → L6 200/h.

Additional S6 mechanics:

  • 얼라이언스 협약s — 7-day non-aggression treaties between same-faction 얼라이언스s. Prevent 도시 파괴. Share adjacency benefits and open joint communication channels for up to 200 members. Make faction selection load-bearing.
  • 파벌 기술 — faction-wide research tree unlocked via Stronghold donations, applies buffs to all faction members after individual contribution thresholds met.
  • 워 메리트 economy — earned via kills, captures, reinforcements, donations, rankings. 티어s Bronze → 엘리트 with corresponding daily stipends and stat bonuses. 워 메리트 Shop trades.
  • 균류 연구소 (max ~L60) — enhances 바이러스 저항 (VR) (analog to 광전기 실험실 / 저주 연구실).
  • 포자 공장 (max L30) — produces 포자s.
  • 성역 timer buff: doubled from 10 → 20 minutes (occupies 1/3 of the 60-minute attack window). Sources call this a "stealth change" that significantly restricts strategic flexibility.
  • 얼라이언스 스킬 cooldowns: 3–6 days.
  • Card meta shift: the popular "4+1 hybrid squad strategy" was removed from 글로벌 익스페디션, forcing pure 탱크 / 미사일 / 항공기 formations backed by attribute-specific Tactics Cards.
  • Beneath the Ruins (added 2026-07-15) — 시즌 6's dedicated minigame, launched in TWO phases [Cpt Hedgehog Beneath the Ruins, 2026-07-15; TheGamesEdge Beneath the Ruins 가이드, 2026-07-15]:
  • Solo Mode (Week 1 Day 2+): descending-platformer challenge. 가이드 your character downward through an ever-deepening stage, avoiding hazards. 1 new stage unlocks per day. Missed days stack — play back-to-back to catch up. Platform types: Regular (safe), Crumbly (breaks after ~1 sec), Spike (1 HP damage on landing — avoid at low HP), Travelators (moving belts — go with the flow, fighting them costs time). HP regenerates when standing on safe platforms. Only left/right movement. Reward: Rainforest Mushrooms (larger on later stages), plus season-exclusive gifts.
  • PvP Duel Mode (Week 2+): competitive descent against another commander, same stage simultaneously, first to bottom wins. No HP regeneration. Entry paid in 워 메리트 deposit up to 500 (or 1,500 with 시즌 스킬). Rewards up to 3,000 워 메리트 per match [TheGamesEdge, 2026-07-15]. Wager spirit similar to S5's 하이눈 Duel.
  • 글로벌 익스페디션 — new dedicated building & schedule (added 2026-07-15) — moved out of the Alert 미사일 발사대 into a new dedicated 원정 Base building [LDShop S6 글로벌 익스페디션 가이드, 2026-07-15]. Three GE rounds per season, each 14 days long (roughly Weeks 2, 4, 6 of the main season). Named 각성 조각s drop at rank thresholds — one of the best regular free shard sources in the game.

  • 02-heroes — Every season promotes one SSR to UR. Since S6, three heroes per season also gain 히어로 각성. Named vs Universal 각성 조각s are the new currency; hero tier-list volatility now depends on which heroes are in the 각성 rotation.
  • 03-chief — 치프 profession cap extended from 60 → 100 in S3, retained through S6. 외교관 unavailable in S3/S4 (unconfirmed if unlocked later). New seasonal skills (Sandworm Traps, Trap Landmines, Nature's Touch, etc.) tie to season-specific PvE.
  • 04-squads-combat — Tactics Cards debuted in S4; card meta shifted hard in S6 with the removal of the 4+1 hybrid squad strategy from 글로벌 익스페디션, forcing pure 탱크/미사일/항공기 formations. Curse Resistance / 바이러스 저항 (VR) stats are new gate mechanics for PvE damage that stack over standard stats.
  • 05-season-1-crimson-plague — S1 크림슨 플레이그 introduced T10 troops. 시즌 structure since S3 has become more elaborate but retains the S1 template of themed map + seasonal resources + 수도/faction endgame.
  • 06-season-2-frozen — S2 폴라 스톰 was the first true themed environmental hazard (cold/heat). S3+ retained the "seasonal building trinity" model (resource-producer + shop-currency-producer + resistance-lab). 얼라이언스 화로 pattern from S2 evolves into 축복의 샘 (S3) / 등대 (S4) / 은행 요새 (S5) / 제단s (S6). See STYLE_GUIDE for how the 라워_얼라이언스_Ops_Center suite handles seasonal building overlays.
  • 08-얼라이언스-systems — 얼라이언스 협약s (S6), 얼라이언스 세이프 타임 (S5+), and 파벌 기술 (S6) reshape how 얼라이언스s coordinate at the meta level. 얼라이언스 city/stronghold caps evolve per season (S3 = 8/8; S4 = 6/6; S5 Bank = 4 default up to 12; S6 factional).
  • 09-events — 블러드 나이트 강림 (S4, 02:30/10:30/18:30 server), 모래벌레 위기 (S3, 14 days), 향신료 전쟁 (S3, biweekly), 구리 전쟁 (S4, Wed/Sat), 무역 열차 (S5), 제단 Tuesdays (S6), 파벌 대전 (season-ending). Each season adds 2–3 new recurring events.
  • 10-economy — 시즌 Battle Pass (~48 days, ~€11.99 Advanced / ~€23.99 Luxury) is the shipped pricing anchor since S3. Weekly Pass adds a temporary seasonal building + resources. Three exclusive weapon passes per season (Weeks 1/3/6). 히어로 각성 shards are now a paid axis on top of exclusive weapons.
  • 14-ui-reference — 시즌 transitions require map re-render, seasonal building rendering, and faction color coding (S6 Deepwood green / Wetland blue / Great River neutral). 얼라이언스 세이프 타임 UI added in S5.
  • 15-glossary — Every season has full KR terminology; namu.wiki maintains a running 한국어-language reference. Faction names (카게노사토, 코부타이 in S4; Deepwood/Wetland in S6) are transliterated from EN in KR sources rather than re-localized.
