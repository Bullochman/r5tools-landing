Launch: 뽑기ing per-server, ~6–7 weeks after S3 ends. Duration: 2-week pre-season + 8-week main + celebration week. Theme: 시즌 4, a Japanese-inspired island paradise, plunged into endless darkness by the Nightfall Device and the release of the 오니 바이러스 (ONV) (ONV). Players must restore light.

Factions (introduced in S4 as a formal system):

  • 카게노사토 (Ninja Clan) — dark green aesthetic
  • 코부타이 (Samurai Clan) — imperial red aesthetic

워존 are grouped and choose sides in the Faction Award 이벤트 in Week 3; 구리 전쟁 (Wed/Sat) occur across Weeks 4–7; Faction 수도 + War's Eve in Week 7.

Map: 13×13 grid (same as S3), 75×75 pixel squares.

  • 24 Level 1–6 cities (down from 40 in S3 — meaningfully denser competition)
  • 6 Digging Strongholds (코퍼 production 100–200/hour)
  • Trade Posts (Levels 1–4, unlock Week 3 Day 2)
  • 10 스시 레스토랑s (unlock progressively Weeks 1–4)

Signature building: The 등대. Central power system storing electricity and pushing back darkness. Requires 4 zombie-powered generators (광전기 실험실 levels 1, 3, 10, 15), each operated by an 전기공. Features:

  • 4 brightness levels (L1–L4)
  • Power management system
  • Auto-adjustment toggle for conservation

광전기 실험실 (max L35) — increases 바이러스 저항 (VR) and unlocks generator repairs at specific thresholds. Analogous to S3's 저주 연구실.

블러드 나이트 강림 — the S4 flagship recurring PvE-into-PvP event. Occurs 3 times daily at 02:30, 10:30, and 18:30 server time, each lasting 30 minutes. Power consumption jumps to +18,000/minute during 블러드 나이트. Four stages:

  • Stage 1: 오니 장군s spawn; defeating them unlocks city buffs
  • Stage 2: Wandering Oniwagons appear (max 5 consecutive battle rounds)
  • Stage 3: Escalated difficulty
  • Stage 4: Holy Mountain event

구리 전쟁 — the main PvP event. 얼라이언스s declare on enemy 연맹 센터s. Key rules change from S3: only 3 secondary buildings per 연맹 센터; declaration range narrows weekly; minimum 11-tile spacing between 연맹 센터s.

마네키네코 discoveries: Similar to S3 desert artifacts; discovered using flares/base lights (Large 마네키네코) or 전기공s on nighttime expeditions (Mini 마네키네코 — 1 per day max). 3 daily claims (max 15).

Trade Posts contests run Tuesdays and Fridays at 12:00 server time; during 블러드 나이트, governors can call additional caravans with 코퍼-exclusive items.

도시 capture: 2 declarations/day, max 6 captured cities + 6 Digging Strongholds. Buffs unlock via 오니 장군 defeats.

히어로 / weapons: 사라 upgrades to UR (Week 3 Day 1). Exclusive weapons: 루시우스 (Week 1), 아담 (Week 3), 윌리엄스 (Week 6).

직업s: Level cap remains 100. 외교관 unavailable. Tactics Cards debut here as a new equipable layer.

T11 troops unlock at season end during the celebration/off-season week.

블러드 나이트 강림 — 4 stages mapped to weeks [lastwartutorial (game-modes), 2026-07-15]:

  • Stage 1 "Night Parade of One Hundred Oni" (W1): zombies + 파멸의 정예s roam; first-kill rewards on world zombies reset daily and drop Tactics Cards; 오니 장군s inside your cities restore city buffs + drop oil when killed.
  • Stage 2 "Wandering Oniwagon Invasion" (W2): Oniwagons appear during 블러드 나이트; attack yields Parts Chests; destroying one spawns a Kappa dropping epic Tactics Cards.
  • Stage 3 "둠스데이 Crisis / Oni Legions" (W3–W7): 둠 워커s spawn; seasonal profession skill Oni Summon attracts legions to your base; surviving grants rewards but applies "Base corroded. 총사령관 Durability Damage +500" debuff.
  • Stage 4 "Towards the Light" (W8): charge the Holy Mountain by killing 블러드 나이트 Zombies; full charge dispels darkness and permanently ends 블러드 나이트 for the 워존.

Related 블러드 나이트 side-modes [lastwartutorial (game-modes), 2026-07-15]:

  • 블러드 나이트 Hunter (W2 onward): PvP-during-Blood-Night mode. 직업 skill transforms you into a hunter for buffs; attack rival hunters for points; 3 breach opportunities before elimination; teleport items and shields disabled while hunting.
  • Divine Tree (W2+): send 전기공s to charge; once active, draw a Fortune Slip Tue–Sat from 5 element types; completing a full draw grants +250 바이러스 저항 (VR) during 블러드 나이트.
  • Ryōtei Restaurant (W2+): daily Mahjong-solitaire minigame; perfect clears (no items used) yield bonus rewards; visitor gifts refill hint items.
  • Silverclaw 항공 Boost (W1): "like" bases wearing the Silverclaw skin (click the moon symbol) for Night Gift speed-ups.

구리 전쟁 — full mechanics [lastwartutorial (strategy guide), 2026-07-15]:

  • 8 total rounds across W4–W7 (2/week, Wed + Sat).
  • 카게노사토 starts on attack, 코부타이 starts on defense. Sides alternate round-to-round — each faction ends with 4 attacking + 4 defending battles.
  • Declaration window: 24 hours in the Declaration stage. R4+ in the attacking faction can declare on 1 target 얼라이언스 in the same 코퍼 ranking group. Up to 3 attacking 얼라이언스s may declare on the same target; target changeable until stage ends.
  • 코퍼 ranking groups: 얼라이언스s sorted by copper holdings into 4 tiers (1–10, 11–20, 21–30, 31–50). The Group 1 range narrows across the season: 10 얼라이언스s (W4) → 6 (W5) → 3 (W6–W7). Concentrates matchmaking among top 얼라이언스s late-season.
  • 코퍼 distribution formula: attackers plunder a percentage per building destroyed. Secondary buildings (코퍼 / 오일 / 코퍼 Warehouse) = 3% each; 연맹 센터 = additional 6%. Max plunder per round: 15% of defender's 코퍼. Multiple attackers share the plunder proportionally by damage inflicted.
  • 여신 role redesign: the 여신 얼라이언스-skill was removed and replaced by the 테슬라 Coil 집사 스킬 — defensive ability, deployable only by the 집사 during preparation and war phases.
  • NOTE: Earlier KB draft cited "30% steal" (S3 향신료 전쟁 carryover). 시즌 4 구리 전쟁 cap is 15%, not 30% — a meaningful reduction from S3.

사라 UR promotion — costs and refunds [lastwartutorial (Week 3), 2026-07-15]:

  • Requires 전설 히어로 Badge item + 사라 at 5★.
  • After UR upgrade, hero resets to 3★ with skills at Level 1.
  • Cost to return to 5★ post-UR: 1,600 total shards (doubled from the standard SSR path).
  • Refund policy: shards spent on prior star-ups not refunded; 명예의 벽 shards fully refunded (converted to hero-specific shards); skill medals fully refunded.

아담 exclusive weapon activation [lastwartutorial (Week 3), 2026-07-15]: rolls out W3 D4, 7-day battle pass, activation cost 50 아담's 전용 무기 named shards, prerequisite 아담 at 5★.

Trade Post contest specifics [lastwartutorial, 2026-07-15]: contests run Tue + Fri at 12:00 server time for 1-hour windows. No connected 얼라이언스 territory required to join — unique among S4 capturable objectives.

도시 level unlock timing (W3) [lastwartutorial (Week 3), 2026-07-15]: L5 cities unlock W3 D3 (mission goal delayed 12h); L6 cities unlock W3 D6 (mission goal delayed 12h).

Hunting Oni event [lastwartutorial (Week 3), 2026-07-15]: available from 블러드 나이트 강림 Stage 3 onward; 워존-wide goal to eliminate 100 Oni collectively for shared rewards.

연맹 센터 placement — exact rule [lastwartutorial (strategy guide), 2026-07-15]: minimum 11-tile spacing measured center-to-center between 연맹 센터s. 재배치 spots are attackable during the pre-100%-completion window — a known 코퍼-War exploit for aggressive positioning.

축하 주간 — full lineup [lastwartutorial (celebration), 2026-07-15]:

  • Transfer Surge: server-transfer feature unlocks W8 D1, runs 14 days into off-season.
  • Canyon Storm Battlefield: new weekly battlefield event (partial Desert Storm resemblance + new features). Registration in off-season W1; first match off-season W2.
  • Bingo Task: 7-day 15-tile bingo grid, off-season W1. Grand prize: 1,500 Black Market Cash + Overlord items.
  • Black Market: 28-day currency store (Black Market Cash). Daily refresh with free/discounted/regular items. Runs across all celebration weeks with Challenges I / II / III.
  • Champion Duel: starts Tuesday of off-season W1 in the 아레나 building.
  • Full celebration span: ~4 weeks after 시즌 4 W8.

T11 troop unlock — exact prerequisites [cpt-hedge via search snippet, 2026-07-15]: T11 unlocks in S4 off-season. Requires (a) HQ Level 27+, (b) at least one Barrack at Level 35, (c) built 무기 연구소, (d) all 4 Armament 연맹 기술es at 100%.
