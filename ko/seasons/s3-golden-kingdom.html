Core mechanics

The seasonal cadence — what's fixed across every season

Since 시즌 3, every 라워 season has followed the same structural template, with only theme and specific mechanics varying:

  • 2-week pre-season (introduced in S4, retained through S6): countdown, faction/워존 grouping, rule preview, reward preview, 시즌 Boost, capitol conquest without adjacency, 얼라이언스 세이프 타임 configuration.
  • 7-day 시즌 Preview — daily challenges unlock the season's mechanics one at a time before main season begins.
  • 8-week main season — the actual war phase, divided into weekly milestones (city unlocks, faction contests, war windows).
  • Celebration / off-season week — final rewards, T-troop unlocks (T11 introduced in S4), migration prep to next season.
  • 서버 migration — at season end, players' accounts, HQ level, heroes, and tech carry forward; the map and all seasonal buildings/resources reset. 서버s merge across 워존s as populations decay (08-얼라이언스-systems cross-ref).

The 8-week main season subdivides consistently: Weeks 1–3 are build-out (buildings unlocked progressively, seasonal resource stockpiling), Weeks 4–6 are the main PvP conflict (향신료 전쟁 / 구리 전쟁 / 도시 Clash / 파벌 대전 prelims), and Weeks 7–8 are the season-defining endgame (수도 War, 조상 사원, 파벌 대전 final).

Length has crept upward: S3 was documented as ~8 weeks; S4 as "roughly 8–10 weeks" including celebration; total cycle including pre-season, main, and post-season is ~14 weeks per KR namu-wiki interpretation. Sources disagree on whether S4/S5 celebration weeks count as "the season" or as inter-season buffer.

Curse Resistance — new stat that gates PvE content. If your Curse Resistance is below a monster's requirement, your damage drops sharply (up to 99.9% reduction). 랠리 leader's resistance applies to all participants unless individual members have higher levels. This is why the 저주 연구실 is the single most important S3 building to keep upgraded.

Curse Resistance formula precision (open question status 2026-07-16): Multiple sources confirm the qualitative shape — "damage drops to as much as 99.9%" when CR is under monster requirement — but the exact CR-shortfall → damage-multiplier table remains gated behind Cpt Hedgehog's paywalled 시즌 3 pages and the Last War Vault Curse Resistance guide's supporter-only content [lastwarvault.com/guides/season-3/curse-resistance-guide/ WebFetch 2026-07-16 confirmed paywall]. What the KB does know from published summaries: (a) the penalty is a smooth-ish curve, not a hard cliff — small CR shortfalls yield small damage cuts, large shortfalls yield the 99.9% wipe; (b) the 랠리 leader's CR overrides participant CR only if the leader's is higher; (c) sandworm content is exempt from CR entirely. Downstream 시즌-3 tools should model CR as a 랠리-leader input with a "damage effectiveness" output of 100% if CR ≥ requirement, else scaled down toward 0.1% — the exact interpolation curve should be labeled TBD, from-in-game until a screenshot pull captures the S3 monster-CR-requirement table.

Recurring cross-season patterns worth noting

시즌al Resistance Stat rotation — every season since S3 has introduced a season-specific resistance stat that gates PvE damage:

시즌-specific building trinity — every season ships (a) a "research lab" for the resistance stat, (b) a producer for the shop currency, and (c) a producer for the ranking currency. Learn the pattern once, apply to any season.

시즌-specific troop tier gate — T-troop upgrades unlock at season-end celebration weeks. T10 unlocked in S1, T11 in S4, T12 anticipated but unsourced.

시즌-specific hero UR ascension — one designated SSR hero per season gets promoted to UR:

  • S3 = 스칼렛
  • S4 = 사라
  • S5 = 베놈 (plus retro UR paths for 메이슨/바이올렛/스칼렛/사라)

전용 무기s rollout — every season ships 3 exclusive weapons on Weeks 1/3/6 via dedicated 7-day battle passes. Named-shard-only for activation, universal shards for upgrades. Since S6, this coexists with the 히어로 각성 system.

Interaction with other systems

  • 02-heroes — Every season promotes one SSR to UR. Since S6, three heroes per season also gain 히어로 각성. Named vs Universal 각성 조각s are the new currency; hero tier-list volatility now depends on which heroes are in the 각성 rotation.
  • 03-chief — 치프 profession cap extended from 60 → 100 in S3, retained through S6. 외교관 unavailable in S3/S4 (unconfirmed if unlocked later). New seasonal skills (Sandworm Traps, Trap Landmines, Nature's Touch, etc.) tie to season-specific PvE.
  • 04-squads-combat — Tactics Cards debuted in S4; card meta shifted hard in S6 with the removal of the 4+1 hybrid squad strategy from 글로벌 익스페디션, forcing pure 탱크/미사일/항공기 formations. Curse Resistance / 바이러스 저항 (VR) stats are new gate mechanics for PvE damage that stack over standard stats.
  • 05-season-1-crimson-plague — S1 크림슨 플레이그 introduced T10 troops. 시즌 structure since S3 has become more elaborate but retains the S1 template of themed map + seasonal resources + 수도/faction endgame.
  • 06-season-2-frozen — S2 폴라 스톰 was the first true themed environmental hazard (cold/heat). S3+ retained the "seasonal building trinity" model (resource-producer + shop-currency-producer + resistance-lab). 얼라이언스 화로 pattern from S2 evolves into 축복의 샘 (S3) / 등대 (S4) / 은행 요새 (S5) / 제단s (S6). See STYLE_GUIDE for how the 라워_얼라이언스_Ops_Center suite handles seasonal building overlays.
  • 08-얼라이언스-systems — 얼라이언스 협약s (S6), 얼라이언스 세이프 타임 (S5+), and 파벌 기술 (S6) reshape how 얼라이언스s coordinate at the meta level. 얼라이언스 city/stronghold caps evolve per season (S3 = 8/8; S4 = 6/6; S5 Bank = 4 default up to 12; S6 factional).
  • 09-events — 블러드 나이트 강림 (S4, 02:30/10:30/18:30 server), 모래벌레 위기 (S3, 14 days), 향신료 전쟁 (S3, biweekly), 구리 전쟁 (S4, Wed/Sat), 무역 열차 (S5), 제단 Tuesdays (S6), 파벌 대전 (season-ending). Each season adds 2–3 new recurring events.
  • 10-economy — 시즌 Battle Pass (~48 days, ~€11.99 Advanced / ~€23.99 Luxury) is the shipped pricing anchor since S3. Weekly Pass adds a temporary seasonal building + resources. Three exclusive weapon passes per season (Weeks 1/3/6). 히어로 각성 shards are now a paid axis on top of exclusive weapons.
  • 14-ui-reference — 시즌 transitions require map re-render, seasonal building rendering, and faction color coding (S6 Deepwood green / Wetland blue / Great River neutral). 얼라이언스 세이프 타임 UI added in S5.
  • 15-glossary — Every season has full KR terminology; namu.wiki maintains a running 한국어-language reference. Faction names (카게노사토, 코부타이 in S4; Deepwood/Wetland in S6) are transliterated from EN in KR sources rather than re-localized.
