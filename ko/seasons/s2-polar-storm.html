TL;DR

시즌 2 (marketed as 폴라 스톰, sometimes 시즌 2 or 시즌 2) is 라스트 워: 서바이벌's second seasonal ruleset, replacing 시즌 1's virus-stack mechanic with an active per-tile temperature system. The map is a frozen concentric grid running from -15 °C at the outer corners to -80 °C at the 수도, with per-band drops of roughly 15 °C per zone; if a base's temperature drops below -20 °C for 5 minutes it enters the 시즌 2 state, which disables 랠리, teleport, and relocation (see 04-squads-combat for 랠리 mechanics and 08-얼라이언스-systems for 하이브 coordination implications). Survival is driven by three new buildings — the personal 고열 화로 (level 30 max, coal-burning, adds +5.5 °C to +20 °C activation / +8 °C to +30 °C overdrive), the shared 얼라이언스 화로 (level 20 max, R4/R5 deployed on Day 1, produces +3 °C to +12.5 °C normal / up to +25 °C overdrive across a shared radius), and the 티타늄 합금 티타늄 합금 공장 (level 30 max, +720 titanium/hr per level, up to 4 factories organically + 1 from weekly pass). Territory is captured for 희토 (the seasonal ranking currency) — max 6 cities + 4 dig sites per 얼라이언스, 2 captures per day, 36-hour post-capture protection, city temperature bonus scaling +5 °C at L1 → +60 °C at L6 — culminating in the 희귀 토양 전쟁 in Week 4 where factions can plunder 20–30 % of enemy soil stocks under a -70 °C blizzard.

Core mechanics

The temperature system

시즌 2 abandons the flat "virus stack" debuff of 시즌 1 in favor of a coordinate-based ambient temperature. Every tile on the map has an inherent temperature that is a function of its distance from the map center (the 수도 / 핵 용광로). A 열지도 toggle in the UI paints the base 지면 온도 as a heatmap overlay — critical for pre-planning 하이브 placement because that overlay does not account for city furnaces, the 핵 용광로, blizzards, or any dynamic heat modifier; it reveals only the raw geographic baseline. [Handbook 온도 가이드, lastwartutorial.com]

The map is divided into seven concentric zones, each a fixed ambient band: -15, -30, -40, -50, -60, -70, -80 °C, moving inward. The corners of the map are the warmest and safest ground; the very center where the 수도 sits is the coldest and most unlivable position without stacked heat sources. [lastwar-tutorial.com, lastwartutorial.com]

Each base has its own current base temperature, computed as ambient_tile_temperature + sum(active_heat_bonuses). Heat sources stack additively. When the resulting number drops below the freeze threshold, the base enters a countdown.

Freezing threshold and 얼음 상태

The universal freeze threshold is -20 °C. If a base's current temperature stays below -20 °C for a continuous 5-minute countdown, the base transitions into the 시즌 2 state. [multiple sources: theriagames.com, lastwar-guide.org, medievalfun.com, lastwartutorial.com]

While 시즌 2, the following actions become unavailable:

  • 랠리 — cannot start rallies, cannot join in-flight rallies. If a base freezes mid-랠리-sequence during a capture attempt, it stops contributing until it thaws.
  • 텔레포트 — no random, targeted, 얼라이언스-formation, or cross-server teleports.
  • 재배치 — the base cannot move on the map.
  • 방어막 — inside missile-created "검은땅" zones, shields cannot be activated (see 워로드 미사일 below).

Production, healing, and troop-morale debuffs are also applied while cold (bluestacks.com), though the exact scaling has not been surfaced in a single authoritative source.

Defrost / thawing

To escape 얼음 상태, the base's total temperature must rise above the freeze threshold and hold for 5 minutes (mirroring the freeze countdown). The primary defrost methods are:

  1. Fire your own 고열 화로 (or flip it to overdrive) so your local heat output overrides the ambient chill.
  2. Ally spy plane — an 얼라이언스-mate can fly a spy plane over your base with heating enabled to raise your local temperature and initiate defrost. This is the primary rescue method when a member's own coal has run out. [lastwartutorial.com search snippet, lastwar-tutorial.com]
  3. Move within range of an active 얼라이언스 화로 or captured city — passive but slow because relocation itself is disabled while 시즌 2. In practice this only works if the base was pre-positioned before it froze.

A "safe ally heating limit" of roughly +38 °C is referenced as the cutoff before fire damage risk begins around +40 °C, meaning heating too aggressively can flip from freeze protection into over-heat penalty. [lastwar-guide.org Week 4 guide]

눈보라s

눈보라s are temporary map-wide temperature drops that apply on top of the baseline gradient, announced via a top-left notification hours in advance so 얼라이언스s can prepare. Each blizzard event lasts 3 hours. The per-week severity climbs sharply:

The Week 4 -70 °C blizzard runs concurrently with the opening of the 희귀 토양 전쟁 — this is the most dangerous single event in the season and dictates most 얼라이언스s' furnace budgets. [lastwar-guide.org]

The 고열 화로 exposes an "auto-activate in blizzard" toggle that automatically switches the furnace on (and to overdrive if configured) when a blizzard begins. 권장 to enable Day 1. [lastwarguide.net]

Day-1 landing mechanics

When 시즌 2 begins, every 얼라이언스 is teleported as a 하이브 to a corner (or edge) of the map in a pre-arranged formation. The map transition happens on a scheduled server rotation (community reports place this around 13:00 server time for many 워존s); resources dropped from 둠 워커 "first kill" bonuses only reward seasonal 석탄 after the map has transitioned to winter. [dcinside minor gallery, medievalfun.com]

The critical Day-1 priority list is essentially universal across guides:

  1. Reposition the 하이브 if the drop location is too crowded or too close to hostile 얼라이언스s. The whole 하이브 can be moved using the free 얼라이언스-relocation trick (leaders shift the 집결지; members teleport back and forth without spending personal teleports). [lastwar-tutorial.com teleporter guide]
  2. Place the 얼라이언스 화로 in the center of the 하이브 on Level-1 territory.
  3. 방어군 the 얼라이언스 화로 with as many players as possible to burn through the ~20 reinforcements gate quickly.
  4. Every member builds their own 고열 화로 and 티타늄 합금 공장 #1 and enables the auto-blizzard toggle.
  5. Weaker players park closer to the 얼라이언스 화로; stronger players anchor the outer ring so they can absorb hits and provide reinforcement.
  6. Do not kill the highest-level Wanderer / 둠 워커 before the map transition — the coal reward only pays out post-transition. Community reports a ~200,000 coal payout for a level 180 Wanderer first-kill after transition. [dcinside gall/12212]
  7. Stack radar missions before the season opens so they can be cleared on Day 1 for guaranteed coal rewards. [lastwartutorial.com Ultimate Strategy 가이드]
Cities and dig sites

The map is dotted with two capturable territory types, both of which are essential:

Cities (도시 / 마을 / 城)

  • Provide passive heat bonus to their owner and any base parked within their heat radius: L1 = +5 °C → L6 = +60 °C in +11 °C increments. [lastwartutorial.com]
  • Produce 희토 at rates from 350/hr (L1) to 1,000/hr (L6).
  • Newly captured cities snap their tile temperature up to -10 °C (a warm island in the frost). [WebSearch: 시즌 2 city capture rules]
  • Max 6 cities per 얼라이언스, max 2 captured per day, ~6 days protection after capture. [lastwarguide.net]
  • 해금 schedule:
  • L1 (Village): Week 1 day 3 + 12h
  • L2 (Town): Week 1 day 6 + 12h
  • L3: Week 2 day 3 + 12h
  • L4: Week 2 day 6 + 12h
  • L5: Week 3 day 3 + 12h
  • L6: Week 3 day 6 + 12h
  • L7: Week 4 day 7 + 12h (수도-tier / rare)

채굴장s (채굴장 / 광산 / mining sites)

  • Produce 석탄 and 희토. 석탄 output: L1 = 2,736/hr → L6 = 3,456/hr. 희토 output: 100–150/hr at L1–6. [lastwartutorial.com]
  • Max 4 dig sites per 얼라이언스 (independent of city count).
  • Max 2 captured per day, 36-hour protection post-capture.
  • Guarded by mutant 폴라 비스트s that must be defeated first. Beast types have rock-paper-scissors weaknesses:
  • Gorilla (항공기 type) → weak to 미사일 차량s
  • Polar Bear (미사일 type) → weak to 탱크s
  • Mammoth (탱크 type) → weak to 항공기

  • 바이러스 저항 (VR) minimums by dig-site level: L1 = 4,000, L2 = 6,500, L3 = 8,500, L4 = 9,500, L5 = 10,000, L6 = 10,500.
  • 편성 strength recommendation: 25 M+ for main assault.
  • Capture is competitive: the first 얼라이언스 to reach 100 % occupation wins. If no 얼라이언스 reaches 100 % within 4 hours, the attempt fails and everyone is refunded. [lootbar.com]

Adjacency rule: an 얼라이언스's first territory captured in the season must be a Level 1 채굴장. From there, every subsequent capture must be adjacent to existing 얼라이언스 territory — this drives the "expanding claim" pattern outward from the corner. [lastwartutorial.com Ultimate Strategy 가이드]

희토 — the season ranking currency

희토 (희토 / 稀土 / "희토") replaces the 시즌-1 model where city control directly = ranking. In 시즌 2, control matters only insofar as it produces 희토, which stockpiles in the 얼라이언스 수도 and drives both:

  1. 얼라이언스 화로 auto-upgrades (stockpile thresholds trigger level-ups).
  2. 시즌-end 얼라이언스 rank (which 얼라이언스 has the most stockpiled soil).

Cities and dig sites are consequently valued by their soil production rate, not their raw coordinates.

Hidden supply caches: each city has hidden supply caches that appear when the local ground temperature exceeds 0 °C. Players use incineration items (throwable heat bombs) to melt the ice and reveal them. 시즌 2 supplies require spy-plane heating with 5 stamina per hit for 1,000 durability to defrost. Daily reclaim limit: 5 attempts. [lastwartutorial.com, dcinside gall/12212]

희귀 토양 전쟁 (Week 4+)

해금ed in Week 4, the 희귀 토양 전쟁 is a scheduled cross-얼라이언스 combat event whose objective is to destroy the enemy 얼라이언스 화로 and plunder up to 20 % of their 희토 stockpile (30 % after the Showdown event later in the season). [lastwarguide.net, lastwartutorial.com]

20 % / 30 % plunder confirmed 2026-07-15 — third-source cross-check via lastwarguide.net/en/seasons/season-2 search excerpt ("The 얼라이언스 can steal up to 20% of the enemy's 희토 stock, or up to 30% during the Showdown"). Previously KB-flagged as single-sourced (티어 C only); now upgraded to two 티어 B/C sources agreeing on identical percentages. The 30 % applies specifically during the Showdown week (Week 6 per lastwartutorial.com/season-2-week-6/), not the entire post-Week-4 window — an 얼라이언스 mid-시즌-2 랠리 planner should treat 20 % as the default expected transfer and 30 % as a Week-6 pulse-event modifier.

Faction system: at the "Faction Awards" event in Week 3, all 워존s vote themselves into one of two factions — Rebels or Gendarmerie — with 8 워존s split 4-and-4. Only inter-faction attacks are allowed.

Ranking groups: 얼라이언스s are split into 4 groups by 희토 stockpile — ranks 1–10, 11–20, 21–30, 31–50. Attacks are only allowed within the same group.

  • 3-minute (180-second) preparation window after launch during which the 워로드's own base is revealed to the entire server via world-chat broadcast and the 워로드 enters "전쟁 열풍" status. [lastwar-tutorial.com 희토 War, 2026-07-15; lastwar-tutorial.com Ultimate Strategy 가이드, 2026-07-15]

Close-perimeter defender ping — the mechanic the KB was missing. On successful impact, every base inside the 25×25 blast area is forcibly relocated to a random position on the map — except the 워로드's own base if it happens to be inside the radius. This is the "close-perimeter ping" observed in community reports: defenders reinforcing the 얼라이언스 화로 do not merely lose their shield — the game physically evicts them from their reinforcement position, forcing the furnace to be re-reinforced under fire while defenders find their new (often deep-frozen) landing tiles, shield up, and teleport back. 증원ment squads inside the furnace itself are not evicted (they are inside the building, not on a tile), but any base staged next to the furnace to launch counter-rallies is displaced. 방어막s are also blocked inside the 검은땅 for the full 1-hour duration, so re-shielding at the random landing tile is possible but re-shielding at any tile inside the 25×25 zone is not. [lastwar-tutorial.com 희토 War, 2026-07-15; lastwarguide.net 시즌 2, 2026-07-15]

눈보라 interaction with the ping. If the missile lands during an active blizzard, random-teleported bases can arrive on frozen tiles with insufficient personal heat and start the 5-minute freeze countdown immediately at their new location — a compounding penalty that removes them from the war entirely. [lastwar-guide.org 시즌 2 Week 4, 2026-07-15]

Intercept play — cancelling the missile before it fires. During the 3-minute prep window the 워로드's coordinates are public and defenders on the enemy server can 랠리-attack the 워로드's base. The cancel condition is precisely: the 워로드's base moves (relocates OR is breached during the 3-minute window) → missile launch fails → 72-hour cooldown on the 워로드's missile ability. A "breach" appears to mean any successful hit that forces base displacement or damages it enough that the 워로드 relocates defensively; the surveyed sources do not publish a fixed damage number to trigger the cancel. [lastwar-tutorial.com 희토 War, 2026-07-15; lastwar-tutorial.com Ultimate Strategy 가이드, 2026-07-15]

"Failed launch" definition (specific to the 72-hour cooldown). A failed launch is any of:

  1. 워로드's base relocates during the 3-minute prep window (whether voluntarily to dodge or involuntarily via breach).
  2. 워로드 moves off the target server before impact (워로드 must remain on the target furnace's server and within its catchment area). [lastwar-tutorial.com 희토 War, 2026-07-15]

A cancel triggered by 랠리-breach counts as a failed launch and applies the 72-hour cooldown. The 72-hour cooldown is on the missile skill, not the 워로드 role — the 얼라이언스's 워로드 officer can still be replaced by another R4 with the 워로드 title, but that alternate must not have burned their own missile skill recently. This creates the standard "shadow 워로드" pattern where 얼라이언스s rotate 워로드 titles between multiple R4s to keep at least one missile skill available per war round. [inferred from lastwar-tutorial.com 희토 War + 08-얼라이언스-systems R4 직책 mechanics; needs in-game confirmation]

Defensive positioning consequence. Because the 25×25 ping evicts everyone in the blast area, the standard defense guidance is: park every base at least 5 tiles from the 얼라이언스 화로 tile (outside the 25×25 blast when centered on the furnace, which corresponds to roughly a ring 5-12 tiles out depending on furnace footprint). The 집결지 should be placed near the furnace but outside the missile blast radius so evicted defenders have a reliable return tile to shield up and teleport back from. [lastwar-guide.org 시즌 2 Week 4, 2026-07-15]

Plunder distribution: multi-attacker splits are proportional to furnace-durability damage dealt.

희귀 토양 전쟁 — timing conventions (when does the war round actually start?)

The Stage-3 (30-min Prep) and Stage-4 (20-40-min Combat) windows always fire on Wednesday and Saturday of the war week, but the exact clock hour at which they open is not a universal 시즌 2 constant — it is a per-워존 value driven by that 워존's assigned server-time offset. What surveyed sources confirm:

  1. Combat opens 12:30 server time on each war day (Wednesday round, Saturday round). The 30-minute Prep stage runs 12:00-12:30 server time, and Combat runs 12:30-13:10 server time (40 min max, less in imbalanced brackets). [lastwar-tutorial.com Ultimate Strategy 가이드, 2026-07-15]
  2. "서버 time" = the 워존's fixed regional server-time offset, set when the 워존 was created. It does not auto-adjust to member time zones. A community observation of "for us it was 16:00 UK time" reflects the fact that that 워존's server-time 12:30 mapped to 16:00 UTC+0 — i.e., server-time was UTC-3.5 or similar. Different 워존s fire at different UK/PST/KST clock hours because different 워존s sit on different server-time offsets. [community observation via r/LastWarSurvivalGame 시즌 2 threads — filed as 티어 C]
  3. 워존 server-time offset is not R5-configurable in 시즌 2. 시즌 2 predates the 시즌 5 "얼라이언스 세이프 타임" feature that lets R5s choose one of three attack windows per war day. In 시즌 2, every 얼라이언스 on a 워존 fights on the same fixed 12:30-server-time schedule; there is no per-얼라이언스 safe window. The three-windows-per-war-day system is a 시즌 5+ mechanic and does not retroactively apply to 시즌 2 rules. [cpt-hedge.com 얼라이언스 세이프 타임 가이드 (S5/S6), 2026-07-15; distinction is explicit in the source]
  4. Full round cadence per war week (repeated Weeks 4-end):
  • Monday — Stage 0 (Declaration Wait, 24h): rankings visible
  • Tuesday — Stage 1 (선포권, 24h): attackers pick targets
  • Wednesday 00:00-12:00 server time — Stage 2 (Invitation, 12h): defenders invite allied support
  • Wednesday 12:00-12:30 server time — Stage 3 (Preparation, 30 min): matchups locked, 워로드 미사일s can be launched
  • Wednesday 12:30-13:10 server time (max) — Stage 4 (Combat): actual furnace assault
  • Wednesday 13:10 through Thursday reset — Stage 5 (End): rewards distributed
  • Thursday-Saturday: identical 5-stage cycle for the Saturday round

[lastwar-tutorial.com Ultimate Strategy 가이드, 2026-07-15; lastwarguide.net 시즌 2, 2026-07-15]

  1. Regional server-time conventions. No surveyed source publishes a canonical 워존 → server-time-offset table. Community-observed offsets in circulation (티어 C, uncorroborated):
  • KR 워존s often server-time = KST (UTC+9), so Combat 12:30 = 12:30 KST = 03:30 UTC
  • NA 워존s often server-time = EST/PST-adjacent, Combat 12:30 ≈ 17:30-20:30 UTC
  • EU 워존s often server-time = CET-adjacent, Combat 12:30 ≈ 11:30-12:30 UTC
  • The one UK-reporting community example (Combat 12:30 server = 16:00 UK) is consistent with a UTC-3.5 워존 (LATAM or Atlantic). Do NOT publish these as authoritative — they need per-워존 verification.

[multiple r/LastWarSurvivalGame comments; filed as weakly-sourced in open-questions]

Full round duration. From the top of Stage 0 (Monday reset) to the end of Stage 5 (Thursday reset) is ~72 hours per round, of which the actual combat window is at most 40 minutes. Two rounds per week = ~144 hours of war-cycle scheduling with at most 80 minutes of live combat, which is why coordination and 랠리-timing external tooling matters far more than raw combat hours.

희귀 토양 전쟁 — faction rotation schedule across the 4 war weeks

Rebels and Gendarmerie swap attacker/defender roles between Wednesday and Saturday of each war week, and the assignment flips again the following week. The rotation is not a single-얼라이언스 choice — it is set by the season for the entire faction:

[lastwarhandbook.com 희귀 토양 전쟁 시즌 2 가이드, 2026-07-15 — search excerpt: "The gendarmerie is in the fourth week of the season on Wednesday Defender and on Saturday Attacker. In the fifth week of the season is the gendarmerie on Wednesday Attacker and on Saturday Defender."]

Implication for the 얼라이언스 planner: every 얼라이언스 fights exactly one attack round and one defense round per week in Weeks 4-7. The 워로드 미사일 budget must be planned across 4 attack days total (roughly 1 워로드 missile per week for the 얼라이언스's 워로드 officer, respecting the 72-hour skill cooldown). 임원-title rotation to a shadow 워로드 is required if the same officer wants both Wednesday and Saturday launches in a two-week span.

희토 ranking — cumulative accumulation, not end-state hold

Confirmed 2026-07-15: 시즌-end 얼라이언스 ranking is based on total 희토 stockpiled across the entire season, not on which 얼라이언스 controls the most territory when the season ends. This is a fundamental change from 시즌 1 (where end-of-season city control = rank) and drives the "capture early, hold forever" 무과금 strategy:

"The ranking system rewards sustained accumulation, not peak military power. Capture territory early and hold it — a Level 2 dig site held from Week 1 accumulates more 희토 over 48 days than a high-level city captured in Week 6."
[lastwarhandbook.com 시즌 2 가이드, 2026-07-15 (via WebSearch excerpt)]

Consequences for 하이브 planning:

  1. Week-1 speed matters more than late-season power spikes. An 얼라이언스 that deploys its 얼라이언스 화로 and captures its first L1 채굴장 within Hour 12 of Day 1 will out-accumulate an 얼라이언스 that spends Week 1 optimizing before its first capture — even if the second 얼라이언스 ends up with more combat power in Week 6.
  2. 희귀 토양 전쟁 plunder is worth less than steady-state production. A successful 20% plunder of an enemy's stockpile shifts soil between 얼라이언스s but does not create new soil; over 8 weeks, the 얼라이언스 that spends less time attacking and more time farming captured territory typically wins.
  3. Do not over-capture into a higher bracket you cannot defend. 희귀 토양 전쟁 brackets (Ranks 1-10, 11-20, 21-30, 31-50) are set by stockpile — capturing extra cities can promote you into a tougher bracket where you get plundered more than you can produce. [lastwarhandbook.com 시즌 2 가이드, 2026-07-15]
Week 5 endgame — 핵 용광로 reactivation and 글레이시아라돈 boss cycle

The season's endgame is not the 희귀 토양 전쟁 — it is the 핵 용광로 reactivation event that opens in Week 5. The 핵 용광로 sits at the map's absolute center (Zone 7, ambient -80 °C) and is a 워존-scale cooperation event that runs concurrently with the -90 °C blizzard cycle. Every 얼라이언스 on the 워존 contributes to a shared 100% activation bar, and four 글레이시아라돈 bosses (one per map corner) march inward to sabotage that progress.

한파 Alert -90 (Week 5, Day 1, 7 days). 눈보라s intensify from Week 4's -70 °C to -90 °C. 워존-collective goal: warm up allies 10,000 times to unlock the reward chest. [lastwartutorial.com 시즌 2 Week 5, 2026-07-15]

핵 용광로 activation (Week 5, Day 1, 7 days — but event start day varies by server: Monday, Tuesday, or Thursday observed). The mission:

  • Per-player contribution: send troops to the 핵 용광로 via the green-truck icon on the furnace tile
  • 스태미나 cost: 5 stamina per contribution
  • Daily cap: 10 contributions per day per player
  • 워존 goal: reach 100% activation on the shared bar before season end
  • Progress erosion: each 글레이시아라돈 step lowers activation progress by ~1% every 4 hours while walking
  • 워존-collective side goal: donate 1,000,000 coal to 얼라이언스 화로s across the 워존

[lastwartutorial.com 시즌 2 Week 5, 2026-07-15]

글레이시아라돈 boss (4 spawns, 1 per map corner, over 4 consecutive days). Cannot be attacked while walking on open map — attackable only when passing through a city. This makes city-ownership timing a strategic asset separate from 희토 production. Mechanics:

  • Attack cost: 10 stamina per attack
  • Daily cap: 5 attacks per day per player per 워존
  • Contribution: 얼라이언스 reinforcements to attacked cities reduce 글레이시아라돈 HP
  • Defense payoff: successful defense preserves city ownership (a 글레이시아라돈 walking through your city normally would push you off)

[lastwartutorial.com 시즌 2 Week 5, 2026-07-15]

Rewards distribution: Final rewards are based on how well your 워존 progresses vs. other 워존s — a cross-워존 leaderboard is implicit. 서버s with poor 핵 용광로 activation reap smaller rewards than servers that hit 100% early.

핵 용광로 as a Week-4 capturable objective. The 핵 용광로 itself is unlocked for capture on Week 4, Day 6 + 12h (per Week 4 event schedule). This is separate from the Week 5 reactivation event — Week 4 is territory-claim, Week 5 is 워존-collective activation. [lastwartutorial.com 시즌 2 Week 4, 2026-07-15]

시즌 length reconciled — 8 weeks total, with 48 "core" days across weeks 1-7

The prior contradiction between the "48 days / 5 weeks" (theriagames.com) and "8 weeks" (lootbar.com) sources is resolved as of 2026-07-15: the season runs approximately 8 weeks total (some sources say 8-10 with 워존-specific tail variation), of which:

  • Weeks 1-3: ~21 days of setup, capture unlocks, faction voting
  • Weeks 4-7: ~28 days of active 희귀 토양 전쟁 (Wednesday + Saturday combat, 8 total war rounds), 핵 용광로 reactivation event begins Week 5
  • Week 8 (and possibly 9-10): wind-down, final 글레이시아라돈 defeats, season-end rewards distribution

[lastwarvault.com 시즌 2 Timeline 가이드, 2026-07-15 — search excerpt: "시즌 2 runs roughly 8-10 weeks"; lastwar-guide.org 시즌 2 가이드, 2026-07-15]

The "48 days" figure corresponds to the first 7 weeks of core content (Wk 1-7 × 7 days ≈ 49); the "5 weeks" figure likely refers to the weeks with unique unlock content (Wk 1-5, before content settles into the repeating Wed/Sat war cycle). Both are correct measures of different things.

텔레포트ation rules in 시즌 2

Standard cross-server and in-map teleports work in 시즌 2 with three modifiers:

  1. 얼음 상태 disables ALL teleport options. No random, targeted, cross-server, or 얼라이언스-formation teleport is possible while a base is 시즌 2. [multiple sources]
  2. Cross-server teleport is unlimited during combat events (Duel VS on Saturday, 수도 War, 시즌 battles). [lastwar-tutorial.com]
  3. Free 시즌 편성 순간이동 is available from Week 3 — accessed via 시즌 2 → Grouping 텔레포트 in the season menu, this lets an entire 얼라이언스 move to another server (and back) free of personal teleport charges. This is the vehicle for merged 얼라이언스s and coordinated attacks. [lastwar-tutorial.com]
  4. Cooldown: returning from an enemy server triggers a 5-minute cooldown before the next hop.

The free 얼라이언스-relocation trick works in 시즌 2 exactly as in 시즌 1: the R5 shifts the 얼라이언스's 집결지, and members teleport back and forth without personal teleport cost, allowing the entire 하이브 to move.
