Launch: 뽑기ing per-server. Community timeline places 시즌 5 launch in early 2026 (before S6 launched April 13, 2026). MedievalFun's preview article (Nov 7, 2025) said 시즌 5 arrives "in 11 days," implying ~November 18, 2025 as first server rollout. Duration: 2-week pre-season + 8 weeks main. Retains celebration week pattern. Theme: 와일드 웨스트 / 시즌 5 / 시즌 5. 베놈 is the featured hero.

The structural break: single-워존 → 8-워존 shared map. S5 introduced "옥타곤 클래시": 8 워존s from 8 different servers converge onto a single battlefield. Map is 9× larger than previous seasons, plus a central area. This foreshadows and enables the S6 faction model.

New seasonal resources: | 자원 | Purpose | Source | |---|---|---| | 크리스탈골드 | Primary ranking currency, deposited in Banks for interest | 도시 passive income, Bank deposits, 파멸의 정예 first-kills, 하이눈 (max 15,000/day from Week 3), 무역 열차 whiskey trades (Week 2+) | | 커피 / 커피 Beans | Fuels 커피 Factory and 카페인 학원 upgrades | 커피 Factory production | | 위스키 | 무역 열차 consignment (converts to 크리스탈골드) | 무역 열차 |

Signature mechanic: 은행 요새s.

  • 얼라이언스s capture 2 은행 요새s/day
  • Default max holding: 4 banks; increases +1 per city controlled up to 12 max
  • 3 크리스탈골드 deposits/day, up to 15 simultaneous 5-day deposits (interest-bearing)
  • Levels 1–6 available Week 1; Levels 7–10 open Week 4 with 바이러스 저항 (VR) requirements reaching 39,900
  • Sundays are Truce Day — no 은행 요새s can be taken

무역 열차: A train continuously travels across the continent, stopping at multiple stations. Players consign 위스키 for 크리스탈골드 profit — or raid competitors' trades for rewards. Introduces cross-얼라이언스 economic warfare not previously in the game.

War timing overhaul — the biggest quality-of-life change since S2. Previous seasons ran 24/7 combat. S5 introduced:

  • 3 fixed war time slots per day (1 hour each, spaced 7 hours apart)
  • 얼라이언스 세이프 타임 — 얼라이언스s disable 1 of the 3 daily war slots for 7–15 consecutive hours of immunity (president-managed)
  • Cities can only be captured on war days: Wednesdays and Saturdays (2 captures per war day = 4/week max)

New seasonal buildings:

  • 카페인 학원 — 바이러스 저항 (VR) + coffee recipes
  • 커피 Factories — 커피 production
  • Protector's 농장 — houses special combat units (continues from S3)
  • Bars — 탱크/Air/미사일 hero damage buffs

Faction system: eliminated. Unlike S4 and (later) S6, S5 has no factions. 영향력 포인트 competition is free-for-all between all 얼라이언스s across the 8 워존s.

히어로 / weapons:

  • 베놈 upgrades to UR
  • 메이슨, 바이올렛, 스칼렛, 사라 can also upgrade to UR with available items (returning heroes get "second-chance" ascension in S5)
  • Exclusive weapons: 피오나 (Week 1), 스테트만 (Week 3), 모리슨 (Week 6)
  • 4 heroes gain +10% monster damage: 베놈, 피오나, 스테트만, 모리슨

사전 시즌 structure (retained from S4):

  • Week 1: 시즌 Preview (7 daily reveals), 시즌 Boost preview, Faction Awards
  • Week 2: 골드 프로스펙팅 mini-game (Mon–Fri) → top 100 players/워존 determine 지역 선택 priority → 지역 선택 Tue–Sat (워존s choose spawn locations, higher rank = better area, presidents can swap positions until Saturday lock) → 수도 Conquest Day 6 (all 얼라이언스s compete for 수도 without adjacency)

시즌 pass — Tycoon's Mansion HQ skin grants +5% Attack, plus HP/Defense bonuses.
