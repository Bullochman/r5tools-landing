시즌 1 — The 크림슨 플레이그 (a.k.a. "둠스데이")

Last researched: 2026-07-15 Sources consulted: 28 (22 original + 6 added in 2026-07-15 pass; see sources) KR sources included: ✓ Completeness: comprehensive

Release history (real-world dates)

시즌 1 first went live on May 13, 2025 on server range S101-S196, per 퍼스트펀 Group's official Facebook announcement (referenced in a "running for" community post at facebook.com/lastwarsurvival) [facebook.com/lastwarsurvival post 399609049726381, 2025-05-13, via search 2026-07-15 — 티어 A official social post]. Subsequent server groups roll into 시즌 1 on a staggered schedule as newer 워존s age past the ~90-100-day pre-season eligibility threshold; no consolidated public rollout calendar exists (filed to open-questions). The KB-derivable rule of thumb: any 워존 whose founding date is >~120 days old is either in-시즌-1 or has already progressed past it. See LW Atlas (lwatlas.com) and 농장Ops (lastwar.farm) for live per-server 시즌 status queries.

---

Terminology alert. Community shorthand — especially inside 얼라이언스 chat and on YouTube — often calls 시즌 1 "둠스데이." That is misleading. The official season title is "The 크림슨 플레이그." In-game, "둠스데이" is a separate, permanent, bi-weekly event (둠 워커 랠리) that has nothing to do with the 시즌 1 map or ministries. The two overlap thematically (post-apocalyptic, viruses, "doom" enemies), which is why the term stuck. This article covers the season. See 09-events for the standalone 둠스데이 event.

TL;DR

시즌 1 (officially "The 크림슨 플레이그") is 라스트 워: 서바이벌's first competitive season — a 48-day virus-themed campaign preceded by a 2-week Pre-시즌 and followed by a ~4-5 week Celebration phase. The map, called the Land of Fog and Rain, is grouped into 8 워존s/servers competing for a central prize called the 종말의 도시 (a level-7 super-capitol) which unlocks on Day 28. All player power is gated behind a new stat — 바이러스 저항 (VR) — which is grown almost exclusively by upgrading the 바이러스 연구소 (max +10,000 across 30 levels). Without enough 바이러스 저항 (VR), damage output can drop by 60-99% and incoming damage can be inflated by up to 900%, making the stat mandatory for city/stronghold captures and 파멸의 정예 kills. 얼라이언스 ranking is decided by 얼라이언스 영향력 포인트 accumulated from held cities (100k–1M each depending on level 1-6), culminating in the cross-워존 8강 토너먼트 tournament in weeks 5-7 (see 13-meta-competitive for the tournament tier structure). Small 얼라이언스s that cannot compete for city ranks may permanently switch to 건설자 연맹 mode in weeks 4-7 to trade cities-and-glory for stable construction rewards.

The term "둠스데이" that 얼라이언스 chats use for 시즌 1 is a misnomer inherited from the "파멸의 정예" / "둠 워커" enemy family. Official name: The 크림슨 플레이그. Known KR name: 크림슨 플레이그 (시즌 1).

Core mechanics

1. 시즌 structure and duration

시즌 1 runs on this cadence, in order:

  1. Pre-시즌 — 2 weeks. Preview mechanics, 시즌 Boost bonus display, 시즌 Preview (unlock story tiles by hitting daily goals), and a Pre-시즌 수도 Conquest on Day 6 of Week 2. During pre-season 수도 Conquest, any 얼라이언스 can join without needing adjacent land — this is intentionally more open than the mid-season 수도 rules.
  2. 전술 시즌 — 8 weekly cycles (~56 days; theriagames reports 48 days — a ~1-week disagreement possibly reflecting a patch or "active content" vs "wind-down" terminology drift; see also 06-season-2-frozen for the same S2 disagreement). All seasonal buildings, resources, virus mechanics and territory-capture rules are live.
  3. Celebration Phase — ~4-5 weeks. Black Market 이벤트 (28 days), Bingo Task (Week 1), Champion Duel tournament, and Black Market Cash weekly challenges (Weeks 2-4). 시즌al buildings and their power bonuses are removed at the end of Celebration; permanent rewards (medals, hero shards, exclusive weapon activations) stay.

The season repeats mission "weeks" internally: each mission has a start day and a mission goal that unlocks a delayed number of days later. The whole 8-week schedule is server-time synchronised across all 8 워존s in a group so that 워존-vs-워존 events line up.

2. The map — Land of Fog and Rain

The map is a virus-infested world where every city, stronghold, and strategic tile begins under the control of the Crimson Legion (the NPC faction). 얼라이언스s spawn at map edges in 하이브s; the center of the map is the endgame prize (종말의 도시, level 7). Everything must be re-captured from the Legion.

Territory types:

  • 요새s (level 1 only) — the entry-level territory. Guarded by a 오염자 (a new NPC mini-boss). Adjacency to at least one Stronghold you own is required to declare on Cities.
  • Cities, levels 1 through 6. Levels unlock on a schedule (see numbers table). Each city has a "durability" that scales with level and generates 얼라이언스 영향력 포인트 while held.
  • 종말의 도시 (level 7, map centre) — the former capital of the world, now Legion-held. 해금s on Day 28. Same rules as 수도 Capture but 8-hour contest.
  • 검은땅 (검은땅 in KR). PvP zones where cross-워존 attack is permitted (on foreign 워존s every day; on your own 워존 only on Saturdays).
  • 얼라이언스 Outposts. Placed on foreign 워존s during 서버 원정 (Week 3+). Max 4 outposts per 얼라이언스, one per foreign 워존. Construction-protected on placement.
3. 수도 도시 — 종말의 도시

The 수도 from base-game (the 얼라이언스-controlled capital where ministries and hats are assigned) rolls forward into 시즌 1: the 얼라이언스 holding the 수도 before 시즌 1 starts retains it and keeps all Ministry access for the first 28 days. After Day 28 the 수도 becomes contestable again under a modified ruleset that requires holding an adjacent level-6 city or a bordering stronghold.

At map centre sits the 종말의 도시, a level-7 super-capitol. Rules for contesting it:

  • 해금s Day 28
  • 3-day protection removal delay (Week 4 mission goal)
  • Contest window: 8 hours
  • Victory: first 얼라이언스 to hold 4 cumulative hours wins, or highest cumulative hold-time at the buzzer
  • Winner gains access to bordering territory chains
  • Only 얼라이언스s that hold level-6 cities may participate (adjacency requirement)
4. Strongholds, Cities and capture rules

Daily capture limits (own 워존, before 서버 원정):

  • Strongholds: 2/day, across all 워존s combined
  • Cities: 2/day (on your home 워존 in week 1-3)

Once 서버 원정 opens (Week 3, Day 16):

  • Cities may be declared only on Thursdays and Saturdays ("declaration days") — this applies to both home 워존 and foreign 워존s alike
  • 랠리-based attacks still consume the daily limit; unsuccessful attacks do not consume a capture slot

Maximum territory held:

  • Strongholds base cap: 3
  • +1 stronghold slot per level-1 through level-5 city captured
  • +2 stronghold slots per level-6 city captured
  • Cities: 4 max on home 워존 + 4 across foreign 워존s = 8 total

Example: an 얼라이언스 holding 3× lvl-4 cities + 1× lvl-6 city can hold 3 + 3 + 2 = 8 strongholds.

Protection status:

  • Strongholds: 36 hours after successful capture
  • Cities: 7 days after successful capture — protection expires at 12:00 server time on the 6th day. Protection persists even if the city is abandoned mid-window.
  • 스왑 tactic (KR discovery): swap a city on Thursday → protection expires next Thursday; swap Saturday → next Saturday. Used for locking down protection windows the week before season end.

Adjacency and declaration:

  • Your 얼라이언스 territory must be connected via owned Strongholds to the target 도시
  • Exception: the 수도 and 종말의 도시 can bridge to adjacent territories without a Stronghold chain
  • War declaration duration: 1 hour from posting

Casualties / virus damage:

  • 병력 are infected during city sieges regardless of resistance
  • If your 바이러스 저항 (VR) is below the city's virus strength, damage is reduced 60% (mild gap) to 99% (critical gap)
  • Enemy damage to you scales up: +375% (mild gap) up to +900% (critical gap)
5. 바이러스 저항 (VR) — the master stat

바이러스 저항 (VR) is the single most important stat in 시즌 1. Every other seasonal system — 직업, 유전자 재조합, territory captures — exists to build 바이러스 저항 (VR) faster.

  • Only source of 바이러스 저항 (VR): 바이러스 연구소 (VRI), max level 30, total contribution ranging +100 to +500 per level, cap +10,000.
  • 시즌 Pass adds +250 바이러스 저항 (VR) (7-day buff, repeatable weekly).
  • Battle bonuses from 직업s and 유전자 재조합 add more resistance.

For 랠리 leaders attacking strongholds: only the leader's resistance is checked. If the leader is under-resistant, the entire 랠리's damage drops to zero and casualties are massive. This is why R5/R4 얼라이언스s usually front-load VRI upgrades on their 랠리 leaders.

6. Virus / infection system

RA Virus Stacks are the infection meter: 0 to 100.

  • Thresholds at 1, 20, 40, 60, 80 stacks activate progressive debuffs (reduced damage, increased casualties, morale penalties).
  • 100 stacks: base explodes after a countdown, damaging and infecting other bases in the immediate blast radius.
  • Infection sources: attacking infected zombies above your resistance, attacking 오염자s (auto-infect regardless of resistance), attacking 파멸의 정예 (giant scorpions), being attacked by other infected bases, weather.
  • Treatment: allied players use 면역 단백질 to cure you (airplane mechanic). Curing another player earns gene fragments.

  • 전쟁 리더: combat/랠리 bonuses. Preferred by main-account 랠리 leaders.

  • Right (green) — seasonal skills that reset at season end

시즌 스킬s unlock at levels 1, 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, 35, 40 (9 tiers). Includes 농장 베테랑 (raises daily 직업-XP-from-zombies cap from 75k → 150k) and the Viral Offense function (allows spreading virus offensively).

10. 시즌al resources

Three currencies (they don't survive the season):

  • 면역 단백질 — from 단백질 농장s → upgrades VRI + Military Bases + used to cure allies
  • 돌연변이 결정 — from zombie kills + events → upgrades 단백질 농장s + Military Bases
  • 유전자 조각s (유전자 조각) — 6 types (I–VI); dropped by 파멸의 정예, from curing allies, and from 유전자 조각 Collector event → used in 유전자 재조합

유전자 재조합 event:

  • Duration 47 days (starts Day 3 of Week 1)
  • 6 fragment types, exchanged with allies to complete sets
  • Recombination pool: starts at 10, regenerates +1 every 2 hours, cap 30
  • Payout: 돌연변이 결정s + buffs
11. 서버 원정 & cross-server war

워존 grouping: 8 servers per 시즌 1 group, competing simultaneously.

Timeline:

  • Week 3, Day 16 — 서버 원정 opens; 얼라이언스s can place outposts on foreign 워존s (max 4 per 얼라이언스, 1 per foreign 워존). Outposts open at Stronghold levels 2, 4, 6 or 8.
  • Week 4, Day 1 — 워존 Declaration mission starts; first 수도 Declaration of War happens end of Week 4.
  • Week 5-7 — 8강 토너먼트, the flagship cross-server tournament (see below).

Kill rules by day of week:

  • Every day: attack anyone in contaminated land of foreign 워존s
  • Saturdays: always PvP-open across all 워존s (bases and squads attackable everywhere) with cross-워존 teleportation
  • Thursdays and Saturdays: 도시 war declarations allowed (both home and foreign 워존s once 원정 opens)
12. 8강 토너먼트 (S1 tournament, Weeks 5-7)

The 시즌 1 endgame. Officially documented on the Last War support site (퍼스트펀 Group).

  • 3 rounds spanning Weeks 5-7
  • 8 워존s per bracket — single-elimination, no consolation round for losing 워존s [confirmed 2026-07-15 via firstfungroup.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/44659758784147-S1-Infinite-Octagon search excerpt (티어 A official — direct fetch 403); corroborated by theriagames.com 시즌 1 calendar via search 2026-07-15]. The "Octagon" name is not decorative — it lite랠리 refers to the 8-워존 bracket. Previously KB-flagged as "inferred from bracket size" — now upgraded to 티어 A confirmation from the official 퍼스트펀 support article.
  • 서버s are grouped by 워존 영향력 포인트 (highest-ranked group is the "골드en Bracket")
  • Each round: two servers duel; winner is decided by Bonus 연맹 코인 accumulated over the round
  • Winning 워존 earns bulk 워존 영향력 포인트
  • 얼라이언스-side bonus (if 얼라이언스 total 영향력 포인트 > 10,000,000,000 = 10 billion):
  • 1 round won: +250,000 얼라이언스 influence pts
  • 2 rounds won: +500,000
  • 3 rounds won: +1,500,000

14. 건설자 연맹 (opt-out mode, Weeks 4-7)

For 얼라이언스s that realistically cannot compete for city rankings, 건설자 연맹 mode opens Week 4. Switching to 건설자 연맹 is irreversible for the rest of the season.

  • Trade: give up 도시 Race + Influence ranking + top decoration (God of Judgment) + 시즌 Merit 훈장s
  • Gain: stable donation-driven construction rewards (buildings you donate materials to → 얼라이언스-wide hero buffs + shard rewards)
  • Audience: small 얼라이언스s, mid-tier 얼라이언스s, casual/off-timezone 얼라이언스s, migrant 얼라이언스s
  • Choose window: Weeks 4-7 (can switch anytime in that range)
15. Landing patterns & 하이브 placement (시즌 1 practice)

시즌 1 하이브 placement is the single highest-leverage pre-season decision by R5. Formal community consensus:

  1. Land near the 얼라이언스's target level-6 city — the endgame trophy for tier 1-3 rewards. Ideally within 1-2 stronghold-hops.
  2. Space bases tightly — 돕기 reaches every base within a small radius; 좀비 시즈 reinforcements arrive in time; Desert Storm participation is available to the full roster; 워존 결투 defensive perimeter is cohesive.
  3. Avoid landing directly adjacent to the strongest hostile 얼라이언스 — 시즌 1 daily-kill windows on Saturdays make 하이브-on-하이브 borders violent.
  4. Reserve interior slots for R5/R4 랠리 leaders — they should be flanked by farm accounts so incoming attackers hit farms first.
  5. 하이브 compliance: members leaving the 하이브 without R5 approval get one warning then removal. Compliance is tracked like VS-day participation.

No published "canonical coordinate" list exists — the map is randomised per 워존 group. 얼라이언스 R5s pre-plan 하이브 shape (see the 얼라이언스 하이브 Grid Manager tool the user has built) then anchor on the corresponding coordinates once the map is revealed.

16. 베테랑 / Prestige / Eternal War

연맹 기술 finding: the terms "veteran mode," "prestige mode," and "eternal war" do not appear in any official 라스트 워: 서바이벌 documentation for 시즌 1. They may be community-invented shorthand from other games (Last Shelter: Survival's "둠스데이" event has an "eternal war" mode; Last War is a different game).

  • 농장 베테랑 exists but is a 직업 skill (raises the daily 직업 XP cap from zombies from 75k to 150k), not a game mode.
  • No 시즌 1 replay / prestige / veteran-tier progression was found in any tier-A or tier-B source. Once 시즌 1 ends and Celebration passes, the world transitions to 시즌 2 (시즌 2 / 폴라 스톰). 시즌 then run 1 → 2 → 3 (골든 킹덤) → 4 (에버나이트 아일) → 5 (와일드 웨스트) → 6 (잃어버린 열대우림) chronologically per server.
  • Whether servers replay 시즌 1 after 시즌 6, or whether "eternal war" refers to a post-S6 endgame: unconfirmed — filed to open questions.

Some sources hint that once a 워존 hits the top season, it repeats the current season on a rolling basis, but this was not verifiable from primary sources.

16.11 시즌 1 formation meta (community consensus, 2025-2026)

The KB previously listed a formation-meta gap (open-question #8). Now backed by cross-source community consensus [packsify.com/blogs/best-squad-lineup-in-last-war-survival-game, 2026 update, 티어 C; allclash.com/best-team-formations-lineups-in-last-war-survival-game, 2025 update, 티어 C; ldshop.gg/blog/last-war-survival/season-1-hero-recommendations.html, 티어 B; all via search 2026-07-15].

Master rule — RPS triangle 항공기 > 탱크 > 미사일 > 항공기 per 01-core-gameplay. Full 5-hero same-type squad grants +20% squad-wide stat bonus, which is why mono-type formations dominate over mixed.

Early-game S1 meta — 탱크 squad (~Week 1 through Day ~60-70):

  • 머피 + 윌리엄스 + 킴벌리 + 스테트만 + 마샬
  • Rationale: 킴벌리 is the highest-DPS early-game hero (S1 미사일 Shadow debut Wk1 D4 makes her even stronger from day 4). 머피 + 윌리엄스 absorb hits so 킴벌리 + 스테트만 DPS survives. 마샬 adds attack-speed buffs.
  • Fits S1's early-game because virus resistance is still low across the server → tank frontline soaks 오염자 damage during Stronghold captures.

Mid-to-late-game S1 meta — 항공기 squad (~Day 70+):

  • 루시우스 + 칼리 + DVA + 모리슨 + 스카일러
  • Rationale: as the server matures and most 얼라이언스s default to tanks (because tanks dominate early PvE too), the RPS counter is aircraft. Full-aircraft squad picks up +20% mono-bonus AND the +20% RPS advantage vs tanks = ~44% multiplicative damage edge.
  • DVA is called out as S-tier "fits every meta." 테슬라 is the equivalent for the 미사일 line.

Counter-pick — 미사일 squad (situational, ~Day 60+ if server is aircraft-dominated):

  • Anchored by 테슬라; specific 5-hero list varies by roster availability.
  • Only run this if scouting confirms your 워존's top 얼라이언스s have gone all-aircraft; otherwise the meta stays tank/aircraft.

S1-specific twist not present in the general formation guides: 랠리 leaders in S1 must also meet the Stronghold/도시's 바이러스 저항 (VR) requirement or the 랠리 deals ~0 damage (see §5). So the "best formation" for the leader isn't purely DPS-optimal — it's DPS-optimal given the leader has stacked enough VRI to clear the resistance gate. 농장 accounts often run the "correct" formation for pure PvE, while 랠리 leaders bias toward whichever hero-comp lets them survive the resistance-gap damage penalty.
