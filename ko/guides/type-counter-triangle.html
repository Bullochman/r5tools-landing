히어로 in 라스트 워: 서바이벌 are the single largest lever on squad power. There are three combat types — 탱크 (탱크), 항공기 (전투기), and 미사일 차량 (미사일) — in a rock-paper-scissors: 탱크 > 미사일, 미사일 > 항공기, 항공기 > 탱크, at roughly ±20% damage (see 04-squads-combat for the damage-formula math). 히어로 exist at four rarities — R, SR, SSR, UR — with SSR→UR promotion available for one designated hero per season (메이슨 S1, 바이올렛 S2, 스칼렛 S3, 사라 S4, 베놈 S5, 브라즈 S6). Every hero has four skills (자동 공격, 전술/전술, 패시브, 특기), reaches 5★ with 975 shards, and unlocks progression tiers at 1★/2★/3★/5★. 시즌 6 (Shadow/잃어버린 열대우림, launched 2026-04-13) introduced 히어로 각성, which replaces the "전용 무기" system for new UR seasonal releases and adds a 4th-skill overlay to legacy heroes (킴벌리 Week 1, DVA Week 3, 테슬라 Week 5). The current meta is 탱크-dominant, built around 킴벌리 (UR AoE carry) with 윌리엄스/머피 as anchors and 마샬 as the only UR support in the game.

  • R (레어, common) — starter units. Barely used past the tutorial. Nomenclature varies; some sources call them "Common."
  • SR (Super 레어, blue) — four heroes total, all damage dealers: 앰볼트, 검프, 케인, 로키. 로키 is the strongest of the group thanks to a shotgun burst kit; 앰볼트 is usually the first 항공기 hero every account touches. 레어ly competitive past HQ 20 but useful for filler slots, event side-teams, and early campaign clears. Levelable with 레어 히어로 만능조각s, which are the cheapest and most plentiful shard type.
  • SSR (에픽, purple) — the mid-tier bulk of the roster (13+ heroes). Includes 메이슨, 바이올렛, 스칼렛, 사라, 베놈, 브라즈, 케이지, 엘사, 맥스웰, 모니카, 리처드, Farhad, 케인 (SR), and others. Six of these are "SSR→UR promotion" candidates (see below). A 5★ SSR gene랠리 out-performs a 3★ UR, so SSRs are the 무과금 bridge until UR heroes cross 4★.
  • UR (전설, orange/red) — the endgame roster. 15+ heroes as of 시즌 6, one added per season on average. Divided across 탱크, 항공기, and 미사일. 머피 is the only free-to-play UR who begins in every account's inventory; every other UR requires either shards from 전설 모집, seasonal battle passes, or SSR→UR promotion.

Nomenclature check: the community calls the three types 탱크 / 항공기 / 미사일. Some early guides use "미사일 차량" (the full in-game label) and rarely "Lancer" — the latter is a translation artifact from a different game and is not standard 라워 용어inology. 한국어 uses 탱크 (tank), 전투기 (fighter/aircraft), 미사일 (missile); there is no equivalent to "Lancer" in the 한국어 UI.

  1. 자동 공격 (top-left) — fires automatically each time the attack cooldown resolves. Physical or Energy damage class depending on hero. Priority to upgrade only for pure DPS carries; tank 자동 공격s are decorative and should be skipped on the medal queue.
  2. 전술 (전술) skill (top-right) — the hero's signature move. Fires on a cooldown after battle start (usually 10–20s), then repeats. This is where the biggest single-skill power spikes live. First priority on medals for damage dealers.
  3. 패시브 (bottom-left) — always-on aura or self-buff. Priority for tanks (survivability) and supports (team buffs).
  4. 특기 (bottom-right) — unlocks at 4★. For nearly every UR hero this is "Super Sensing," a flat +20% self HP/ATK/DEF plus +10% skill cooldown speed. 시즌 6 각성 replaces the 특기 skill with a hero-specific 4th skill that inherits the +20/+10 bonuses.

There is no separate "expedition skill" system in 라워 despite that wording appearing in tier-list articles. The four skills above apply in both the main map (둠스데이 / 시즌 2 / Rainforest zombie waves), 얼라이언스 듀얼, 수도 War, 시즌 War, 글로벌 익스페디션, and PvE campaigns. What differs by mode is the squad-level buff overlays (formation type bonus, tech-center research, 얼라이언스 emblems, tactical drone) — those are separate from the hero's own skills.

Every hero levels through five stars using hero shards. Costs are identical across rarities in shard count; only the type of shard differs.

편성 formation and type bonus

편성s hold 5 heroes, arranged 2 front-row / 3 back-row. 편성 rules:

  • Same-type bonus (모노 스쿼드) — a squad where all 5 heroes share a type gets up to ~20% to primary stats per the KR BlueStacks guide; the Packsify guide phrases this as "up to 44% effective power" once tech and gear overlays are counted. The specific stat multiplier at max stack is disputed across sources but the mechanic is uncontroversial: mono-type squads dominate.
  • 상성 — 탱크 deals +20% damage to 미사일 and takes –20% from 미사일; 미사일 similarly counters 항공기; 항공기 similarly counters 탱크. Confirmed in both EN (Pocket Gamer, Packsify) and KR (BlueStacks KR, LDPlayer KR) sources. This is the rock-paper-scissors backbone of all PvP formation decisions.
  • Hybrid "4+1" 마샬 setup — 마샬 is the only UR support in the game, and pairing four same-type heroes with 마샬 retains ~35% of the pure-type formation bonus while gaining 마샬's global ATK amplification. Wardawgg and Packsify call this "the most efficient setup for 90% of players."

Slot positioning matters: 전열 (slots 1–2) takes physical damage first and needs tanks; 후열 (slots 3–5) should hold DPS and support. 윌리엄스 and 머피 are the two mandatory front-row tanks in nearly every meta comp. 킴벌리, DVA, 테슬라, and 마샬 sit in the 후열.
