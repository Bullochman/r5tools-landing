부상 vs killed vs fainted — 라워 troop-loss rules

우리 얼라이언스 잠금 해제 →

라스트 워: 서바이벌 runs a strict three-way rock-paper-scissors combat system: 항공기 beats 탱크, 탱크 beats 미사일, 미사일 beats 항공기, with each counter granting +20% damage dealt and −20% damage taken. 병력 come in the same three "vehicle" classes (탱크 / 항공기 / 미사일 차량 — no infantry/lancer/marksman split, that's Whiteout Survival), organized in tiers T1–T11 gated by 병영 level (T10 at 병영 30, T11 at 병영 35 plus 무기 연구소 research). Each troop has four battle stats — Attack, Defense, Health, Lethality — and is deployed as part of a 5-hero squad (2 front / 3 back) that gains stacking mono-type formation bonuses up to +20% Attack/Defense/HP for a full-type stack. Combat losses are triaged into three buckets: lightly wounded (auto-heal), wounded (병원), fainted / severely wounded (응급 센터 at fainted-unit ratio 21%→40%); anything past both caps is permanently killed. Rallies combine up to 5 얼라이언스 joiners under the leader's stats — the leader's research, gear, hero levels, and drone bonuses apply to every joiner's troops, so leader selection is the single largest lever in 얼라이언스 combat (see 08-얼라이언스-systems for 랠리-coordination context).

Why it works. First wave clears garrison. Follow-up waves land while defender's troops are marching home or sitting wounded — no re-garrisoning happens in the seconds between waves, so waves 2–N attack an empty or under-defended base and do durability damage to structures. "Even the strongest 얼라이언스 struggles to stay afloat under the sheer volume."

부상 → 중상 → 사망 math (explicit)

Beyond the qualitative cascade above, the explicit rule per-battle (Last War Handbook — Healing System Master 가이드 search snippet 2026-07-15; Last War Handbook — Troop Combat Math search 2026-07-15):

casualties = <side loses N troops this battle>

wounded  = min(casualties, hospital_free_capacity)
overflow = casualties - wounded

fainted  = floor(overflow × fainted_unit_ratio)
                         (Emergency Center L1 = 0.21, L20+ = 0.40)
fainted  = min(fainted, emergency_center_free_capacity)

killed   = casualties - wounded - fainted    (permanent, unrecoverable)

Worked example. 응급 센터 L20 (0.40 ratio). 병원 has 500 free slots. 응급 센터 has 1,000 free slots. Battle results in 3,000 casualties:

  • wounded = min(3000, 500) = 500 → 병원
  • overflow = 3000 − 500 = 2500
  • fainted = min(floor(2500 × 0.40), 1000) = min(1000, 1000) = 1000 → 응급 센터
  • killed = 3000 − 500 − 1000 = 1500 → permanent loss

Critical: 병원 and 응급 센터 caps are independent. Even if 병원 still had free slots, once 응급 센터 overflows the excess is killed — not backfilled to 병원. Corroborated by namu.wiki KR: "병원자리가 남아도 응급환자수가 초과되면 전사 판정됩니다" — "even if there are 병원 slots free, if the 응급 센터 is over cap, [the excess] is judged killed in action."

Ratio economics — the fainted-unit-ratio jump from L1 (0.21) to L20 (0.40) is roughly 90% more rescued troops per overflowing battle. In a saturated defensive stand this is the single largest permanent-death-mitigation lever after the actual 병원/EC capacity itself. Combined with T11 장갑 보병's −5% severe-wound rate, the same 3,000-casualty battle above drops permadeath count by another ~150 troops on top of the ratio effect.

Healing cost ratio. Healing wounded/fainted troops costs ~50% of their training cost (Handbook healing summary) — one source (search excerpt via lastwargame.online, 2026-07-15) puts the effective savings even higher: "Healing is always 60–70% cheaper than retraining" once you account for the resource + time cost of full re-training vs. the flat heal price. Either way, maximizing wounded/fainted recovery is a bigger economic win than minimizing casualties in an unavoidable engagement.

부상 → 중상 → 사망 cascade

The single most important defensive system after combat resolution. When a battle ends, every casualty rolls through this cascade (lastwar.wiki hospital; lastwar.wiki emergency-center; Handbook healing guide summary; theriagames emergency center; namu.wiki 라스트워 서바이벌):

  1. 부상 troops go to the 병원, up to its capacity (병원 L1 = 650; L35 during Age of 오일 = 2,100 with room via multiple hospitals to combine). While in 병원 they aren't in your active army; they cost resources + time to heal.
  1. Overflow past 병원 cap would normally be dead — but a 중상 Unit Ratio rescues a percentage of that overflow into the 응급 센터 (응급 센터 (ICU)). The ratio starts at 21% at 응급 센터 Level 1 and increases +1% per level, capping at 40% at Level 20+. The 응급 센터 has its own capacity limit (starting 450 troops L1, up to 2,100 troops L35).
  1. Overflow past both 병원 + 응급 센터 capacity = permanently killed. These troops are gone forever, not merely wounded.

Additional 응급 센터 specifics:

  • Requires HQ 22 to construct
  • Rescue operations have a 2-day cooldown between heals (with slight variations at higher levels)
  • 병원 and 응급 센터 capacities are independent — full 응급 센터 = death even if 병원 still has slots (per namu.wiki KR source: "병원자리가 남아도 응급환자수가 초과되면 전사 판정됩니다")

T11 장갑 보병's 5% severe-wound reduction modifies the initial wounded distribution — 5% fewer of your casualties are classified as severe (which then land in 응급 센터), meaning more of them are merely wounded (병원). The dev-official position is this is a minor advantage in fast-healing scenarios and matters most in overwhelming defensive stands where you're saturating both buildings.
