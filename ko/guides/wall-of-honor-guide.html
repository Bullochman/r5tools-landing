명예의 벽 (permanent hero-account passive)

The 명예의 벽 is the account-level "extra shards keep helping" system. 해금 requires:

  • 히어로 at 5★.
  • Corresponding troop building (탱크 센터 / 항공기 센터 / 미사일 발사대) at Level 20.

Every extra shard beyond 5★ raises the hero's 방벽 entry level. Each 방벽 entry gives a specific stat bonus that applies to your account permanently regardless of whether that hero is fielded. Every 50 levels the per-tier bonus increases; there is no level cap on 명예의 벽 entries.

Per Lootbar's 명예의 벽 guide, published bonus types per hero:

  • 메이슨: +0.50% ATK + HP (UR-eligible)
  • 케이지: +0.50% Defense
  • 루시우스: +0.50% Defense
  • 칼리: +0.50% Health
  • 테슬라: +0.25% Attack
  • DVA: +0.50% Defense
  • 모리슨: +0.50% Attack
  • 스카일러: +0.50% Attack
  • 바이올렛: +0.50% ATK + HP (UR-eligible)
  • 맥스웰: +0.25% Attack
  • 피오나: +0.50% Attack
  • 스위프트: +0.50% Attack
  • 킴벌리: +0.50% Attack [Last War Vault 명예의 벽 가이드, 2026-07-15; corroborated by lastwar.wiki 명예의 벽 page, 2026-07-15 — corrects a prior KB entry that read "+0.50% Defense", which was a KB transcription error; 킴벌리 is the flagship AoE-DPS 탱크 and grants the strongest tank Attack passive in the 명예의 벽]
  • 스칼렛: +0.50% ATK + HP (UR-eligible)

UR heroes typically give the +0.50% class of bonuses; lower-rarity gives +0.25% class bonuses. The 방벽 entry compounds every 50 levels — 5-star + long-tail shard investment can push individual heroes to 300+ 방벽 level over a year of play. This is the primary long-term reason to keep dumping SSR shards into a fully-starred hero.

2026-07-15 cross-check of 탱크-hero bonuses — Last War Vault's 명예의 벽 guide (티어 B) publishes the full 탱크 block as: 킴벌리 / 메이슨 (UR) → +0.50% Attack; 마샬 / 스테트만 → +0.50% Defense; 머피 / 윌리엄스 / 바이올렛 (UR) → +0.50% Health; 로키 / 검프 → +1.00% Troop Load. lastwar.wiki confirms 킴벌리 = Attack. This resolves the "킴벌리 = Defense" question flagged in the KB's Contradictions section.

명예의 벽 ROI (long-term 무과금 investment)

The 명예의 벽 is described across multiple 2026 sources as the single best long-term progression system in the game for 무과금 and low-spend accounts. The value proposition:

  • No level cap — every extra shard past 5★ compounds indefinitely.
  • Account-wide passive — bonuses apply whether the hero is fielded or benched.
  • Refund on promotion — SSR → UR promotion refunds shards spent on 방벽 entries for that hero, so investing 방벽 levels into 메이슨/바이올렛/etc. is zero-risk if you plan to promote them [lootbar.com wall-of-honor guide, 2026].
  • Every 50 방벽 levels the per-tier bonus increases by +0.25% to +1.0% — meaning a hero at 방벽 Lv.100 has a materially stronger bonus than at Lv.50, and Lv.250 stronger still [lootbar.com, 2026; heaven-guardian.com wall-of-honor guide, 2026].

Optimal 무과금 방벽 strategy (Lootbar / Handbook consensus):

  1. Accumulate shards, don't dump them. Save excess shards for Versus Day (VS Day) events — the shard-dump earns free VS Day event points on top of the 방벽 progression.
  2. Prioritize Attack bonuses over Defense/HP bonuses for damage-dealer heroes on your 랠리 comp — Attack compounds harder with existing gear/tech/formation multipliers.
  3. Promote to UR when eligible — this refunds the SSR shard investment, letting you reallocate to the next 방벽 target.
  4. Focus on 3–5 priority heroes, not all 30+. Community consensus: "Identify your three to five priority 히어로 before spending any shards. Do not invest in heroes outside this shortlist" [lastwarhandbook.com hero-recruitment-tickets, 2026].

Exact shard costs per 방벽 level are not published in any 영어 guide reviewed — Lootbar, Handbook, LastWarVault all describe the +50-level breakpoint pattern without publishing per-level shard curves. This is flagged in Open Questions.
