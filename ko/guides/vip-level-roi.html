라스트 워: 서바이벌 monetizes through three stackable revenue rails: a tiered permanent-currency shop (다이아 and the premium 골드 Bricks (금괴) currency, $0.99–$999.90 per pack), an 18-tier lifetime VIP program driven by cumulative VIP 포인트 (1 Diamond = 1 VIP Point, capped at 2,500 per purchase, threshold 15,000 to unlock the pivotal second builder at VIP 6 (see 03-chief for the full VIP 1–18 tier map) and 50,000,000 for VIP 18), and a rotating catalog of passes and battle passes — the Super Monthly Pass ($24.99, 30 days), seasonal Battle Pass (Advanced 500 GB / Premium 2,000 GB), Custom Weekly / Special 훈련 / Drone 훈련 / 히어로 Growth passes, plus the Growth Fund and milestone-gated Dawn Fund. 히어로 recruitment is a gacha with published UR rates around 0.14% on the standard pool (킴벌리 example: 0.1436%); pity and guaranteed-UR thresholds exist only inside limited-time rate-up events, and the only reliable single-hero pity outside gacha is the 명예의 벽 shard-refund on SSR→UR promotion. Expected spend to reach the top of any leaderboard is empirically $1,000+/month for 18–24 months to close in on VIP 18 and max UR heroes; 무과금 earns roughly 55–210 다이아/day and can still reach HQ 30 in 6–8 months by leaning on 얼라이언스 leech, hoarding, and the 명예의 벽. The 2026 monetization overhaul removed the standalone 각성 Battle Pass and moved 각성 조각s to 무과금 sources (히어로 각성 Trial + 글로벌 익스페디션), shifting revenue toward Weekly Passes, 반짝이는 시장 coin packs, and top-up events.

  • 다이아 (다이아, "gems") — the day-to-day soft-premium currency. Earned from daily missions, events, 얼라이언스 help, 무기 경쟁, VIP chest, mail rewards, Duel VS, arena, and every celebration event. Priced against real money only in bulk Diamond packs (100 다이아 at $0.99 up through 10,000 at $99.99 per most guides; see numbers section). 다이아 pay for: 술집 hero-recruitment rolls, VIP Store items, VIP 타임 activation, teleporters, 생존자 뽑기 Tickets, 반짝이는 시장 daily coin exchange (500 다이아 → 60 골드s), and general in-game store purchases.
  • 골드 Bricks (금괴) — the true premium currency, only obtainable via real-money purchase or occasional gift-code / free-brick-event grants (e.g. 4,000 free bricks servers 3-2450, Jun 22 – Dec 19, 2026, for 60+ day-old accounts at Base ≥12). Every paid pass tier is priced in 골드 Bricks, not 다이아. Battle Pass Advanced = 500 GB (~$4.99); Battle Pass Premium = 2,000 GB (~$19.99); Super Monthly Pass = 2,500 GB (~$24.99); Custom Weekly 엘리트 = up to 10,000 GB. 골드 Bricks also unlock special bundles, rare items, and event exchanges that 다이아 cannot buy at all.

VIP program — 18 permanent tiers, one temporary state

Two distinct systems sit under the "VIP" label and they must not be conflated:

  1. VIP 등급 (1–18) — permanent. Determined by cumulative VIP 포인트 ever earned. Once you reach a level, that level and its structural unlocks stay forever, regardless of whether VIP 타임 is active. 누적-points thresholds documented across sources: VIP 1 (0), VIP 2 (500), VIP 3 (1,000), VIP 4 (5,000), VIP 6 (15,000), VIP 7 (30,000), VIP 8 (50,000), VIP 9 (100,000), VIP 11 (250,000), VIP 18 (50,000,000). The gaps between levels widen exponentially past VIP 11.
  2. VIP 타임 — temporary activation window during which percentage-based bonuses (construction/research/training speed, +% resource output) apply. Activation costs: 1,000 다이아 / 24 h, 3,000 다이아 / 7 days (~430/day), 10,000 다이아 / 30 days (~330/day). Without active VIP 타임, the permanent unlocks still work but the % speed bonuses turn off.

VIP Point sources. Free daily: ~200 VIP 포인트/day from the Daily VIP Chest and completing "Today's Theme" + Duel Progress tasks (some estimates 200–300/day). Purchase: 1 Diamond = 1 VIP Point, cap 2,500 per single purchase. 얼라이언스: when any 얼라이언스 member buys a premium pack, the 연맹 선물 Chest often includes VIP 포인트 for every member. Packs: dedicated VIP Point bundles frequently ship in ~4 × 2,500-point units. 랠리 rewards and event drops add smaller drips.

Practical thresholds every account cares about:

  • VIP 6 (second builder) — 15,000 cumulative points. Reachable in 30–50 days 무과금 with 얼라이언스 contributions and event VIP 포인트. Every guide unanimously ranks this as the single most valuable point in the VIP curve. The second builder queue is permanent and does not require VIP 타임 to operate; it doubles construction throughput for the account's entire life.
  • VIP 8 (Agent 셜리 + 명예로운 캠페인 auto-battle) — 50,000 points. 셜리 provides march-size and attack buffs that compound into every 랠리, VS 킬 데이 march, and 시즌 War engagement.
  • VIP 11 (+50% Construction + 15% 연맹 기술) — 250,000 points. First tier where the active-VIP-Time speed differential is visibly larger than pack-buyer benchmarks. Requires VIP 타임 to actually run.
  • VIP 15 — 20% unit healing + 10% training speed (VIP 타임-gated).
  • VIP 18 — 50,000,000 cumulative points. Grants +12.5% hero HP/ATK/DEF across all squads, exclusive developer base skin, and the cosmetic prestige tier. Real-world cost estimate from Vault: 18–24 months of $1,000+/month sustained spend even with 얼라이언스-gift VIP 포인트 and bundle efficiency; direct 1:1 Diamond conversion would theoretically be 50M 다이아 but nobody actually pays that.
