라스트 워: 서바이벌 runs on an 8-week seasonal treadmill: every server rotates through a fresh themed map with new resources, buildings, PvP events, and hero/weapon rollouts. Since 시즌 2 (폴라 스톰) ended, 퍼스트펀 has shipped four full seasons — S3 골든 킹덤 (Egyptian desert / 향신료 전쟁 / Curse Resistance), S4 에버나이트 아일 (Sakura island / 등대 / 블러드 나이트), S5 와일드 웨스트 (시즌 5 / Banks / 무역 열차 / merged 8-워존 map), and S6 그림자 열대우림 (faction warfare / permanent city destruction / 히어로 각성). 시즌 7 is confirmed by community creators as launching approximately August 2026 but no official theme/mechanic details have leaked publicly at the time of this research (2026-07-14). Each season introduces a new seasonal resource loop, a new PvP conflict pattern, and a designated hero for UR ascension or 각성 — and the trend from S5→S6 is toward fewer, longer war windows and permanent, high-stakes territorial outcomes rather than continuous grind (documented in 13-meta-competitive).

The structural break: single-워존 → 8-워존 shared map. S5 introduced "옥타곤 클래시": 8 워존s from 8 different servers converge onto a single battlefield. Map is 9× larger than previous seasons, plus a central area. This foreshadows and enables the S6 faction model.

시즌 6 — 그림자 열대우림 / 잃어버린 열대우림

Launch: April 13, 2026 (first server rollout; rolling thereafter). 사전 시즌 went live late 행군 2026. Duration: 2-week pre-season + 8 weeks main + celebration week. Theme: The 그림자 열대우림, home of three ancient clans — the Great River (their lifeline) has been poisoned by a spreading virus.

S6 is the biggest structural overhaul the game has shipped to date. Community sources uniformly rank it above any prior season in mechanical complexity.

Three clans (two playable + one neutral):

  • 딥우드 클랜 (green stag totem) — forest, "hunting and combat," defensive/resource-focused
  • 웨틀랜드 클랜 (blue crocodile totem) — swamp, "survival and resource control," aggressive/city-capturing
  • 그레이트 리버 클랜 (neutral) — controls the central zone including the 조상 사원 (the 수도)

Map: 그림자 열대우림 Mega Map = 8 워존s + 1 central area, 100% mirror-symmetrical for fair start. 워존 are split 4-vs-4 between factions. Environmental fog reduces visibility (shared among nearby allies, so clustered 얼라이언스s have an advantage over spread-out ones).

Faction assignment: 사전 시즌 Week 1 auto-groups 8 워존s into 2 groups of 4. Strongest 워존 in each group = Faction 연맹장. 골드 toss between leaders determines Deepwood vs Wetland — locked for the season.

Signature mechanic #1: Permanent 도시 파괴. In prior seasons, captured cities could be reclaimed. In S6, captured enemy-faction cities are automatically destroyed and cannot be rebuilt for the entire season. Impact on faction 영향력 포인트:

  • Attacking faction: +50% of city's IP value
  • Defending faction: –100% of city's IP value (net 150% swing per destruction)
  • Destroyed cities do not count toward the attacker's city cap

Signature mechanic #2: 제단s (Central Zone objectives). 5 제단s in the Great River central zone, each granting different 얼라이언스 스킬s tied to a leadership role:

제단Role스킬
Cobra 제단모집관코브라 결계 (얼라이언스 shield)
Echo 제단여신밤 군대 summon
Gust 제단워로드세르펜트 브레스 (contamination attack)
Feather 제단집사페더 라이트닝 strike
트리헤이븐 제단얼라이언스 연맹장Reset skill cooldowns

Additional 얼라이언스 스킬s referenced across sources: 워로드 미사일, Mummy Army Summon, 테슬라 Coil.

제단 rules: open every Tuesday. 얼라이언스s hold max 3 제단s simultaneously. Capturing unoccupied 제단s requires highest progress at timer end; occupied 제단s demand exactly 100% completion. 제단s cannot be used as stepping stones to seize neighboring territory — they exist as isolated capturable nodes.

Signature mechanic #3: 히어로 각성. New progression tier above UR ascension. Replaces a hero's 특기 스킬 (their 4th slot) with a more powerful 각성 스킬, plus major stat boosts (HP/ATK/DEF) and an updated model/effects.

해금 requirements:

  • 히어로 at 5 stars
  • 전용 무기 at Level 20+
  • 50 Named 각성 조각s

조각 sources: Battle Pass (70 named), paid packs (up to 300), 각성 Trial (10 free/hero), 글로벌 익스페디션 Weeks 2/4/6.

시즌 6 각성 rotation:

  • Week 1: 킴벌리
  • Week 3: D.Va
  • Week 6: 테슬라 — resolved 2026-07-15. Earlier sources cited Week 5, but the confirmed release cadence matches the 전용 무기 rhythm of Weeks 1/3/6 per Last War Handbook and LDShop [Last War Handbook 각성 가이드, 2026-07-15; LDShop 히어로 각성, 2026-07-15]. Week 5 sightings appear to have been early speculation rather than the shipped schedule.

MAJOR MONETIZATION SHIFT (mid-시즌 6): In an update published around late Q2 2026, 퍼스트펀 permanently removed all 각성 Bundles and 각성 Battle Passes — direct paid unlocks for Awakened heroes no longer exist. All 각성 조각s must now be obtained through in-game gameplay (Battle Pass rewards, 각성 Trial, 글로벌 익스페디션) [LDShop 히어로 각성 가이드, 2026-07-15]. The broader "pack removal" wave in the same window also stripped the $20/$50/$100 pop-up bundles and Custom Weekly Passes, replaced by a compensation package including 200 전용 무기 조각s + 1,000 용맹 배지s to every player's inbox [LDShop Update 가이드 via search snippet, 2026-07-15]. 왕국d goal per developer messaging: slow the hyper-competitive whale-driven power spike and shift toward season-pass style monetization.

Additional S6 mechanics:

  • 얼라이언스 협약s — 7-day non-aggression treaties between same-faction 얼라이언스s. Prevent 도시 파괴. Share adjacency benefits and open joint communication channels for up to 200 members. Make faction selection load-bearing.
  • 파벌 기술 — faction-wide research tree unlocked via Stronghold donations, applies buffs to all faction members after individual contribution thresholds met.
  • 워 메리트 economy — earned via kills, captures, reinforcements, donations, rankings. 티어s Bronze → 엘리트 with corresponding daily stipends and stat bonuses. 워 메리트 Shop trades.
  • 균류 연구소 (max ~L60) — enhances 바이러스 저항 (VR) (analog to 광전기 실험실 / 저주 연구실).
  • 포자 공장 (max L30) — produces 포자s.
  • 성역 timer buff: doubled from 10 → 20 minutes (occupies 1/3 of the 60-minute attack window). Sources call this a "stealth change" that significantly restricts strategic flexibility.
  • 얼라이언스 스킬 cooldowns: 3–6 days.
  • Card meta shift: the popular "4+1 hybrid squad strategy" was removed from 글로벌 익스페디션, forcing pure 탱크 / 미사일 / 항공기 formations backed by attribute-specific Tactics Cards.
  • Beneath the Ruins (added 2026-07-15) — 시즌 6's dedicated minigame, launched in TWO phases [Cpt Hedgehog Beneath the Ruins, 2026-07-15; TheGamesEdge Beneath the Ruins 가이드, 2026-07-15]:
  • Solo Mode (Week 1 Day 2+): descending-platformer challenge. 가이드 your character downward through an ever-deepening stage, avoiding hazards. 1 new stage unlocks per day. Missed days stack — play back-to-back to catch up. Platform types: Regular (safe), Crumbly (breaks after ~1 sec), Spike (1 HP damage on landing — avoid at low HP), Travelators (moving belts — go with the flow, fighting them costs time). HP regenerates when standing on safe platforms. Only left/right movement. Reward: Rainforest Mushrooms (larger on later stages), plus season-exclusive gifts.
  • PvP Duel Mode (Week 2+): competitive descent against another commander, same stage simultaneously, first to bottom wins. No HP regeneration. Entry paid in 워 메리트 deposit up to 500 (or 1,500 with 시즌 스킬). Rewards up to 3,000 워 메리트 per match [TheGamesEdge, 2026-07-15]. Wager spirit similar to S5's 하이눈 Duel.
  • 글로벌 익스페디션 — new dedicated building & schedule (added 2026-07-15) — moved out of the Alert 미사일 발사대 into a new dedicated 원정 Base building [LDShop S6 글로벌 익스페디션 가이드, 2026-07-15]. Three GE rounds per season, each 14 days long (roughly Weeks 2, 4, 6 of the main season). Named 각성 조각s drop at rank thresholds — one of the best regular free shard sources in the game.

조상 사원 / 파벌 대전 Finale (Week 7): All-out battle between Deepwood and Wetland for control of the 조상 사원 and Central Area. Winner takes 30,000,000 영향력 포인트 toward faction ranking (no individual 얼라이언스/commander points). All commanders in your 워존 are locked to the 조상 사원 and your designated Strategic Area once battle begins.

히어로: 브라즈 (SSR) receives UR upgrade capability in S6.

Cosmetics: "Enchanted Fungus" HQ base skin (~1,500 반짝이 코인), +5% damage to monsters/zombies.
