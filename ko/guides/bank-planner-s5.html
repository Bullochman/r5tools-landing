라스트 워: 서바이벌 runs on an 8-week seasonal treadmill: every server rotates through a fresh themed map with new resources, buildings, PvP events, and hero/weapon rollouts. Since 시즌 2 (폴라 스톰) ended, 퍼스트펀 has shipped four full seasons — S3 골든 킹덤 (Egyptian desert / 향신료 전쟁 / Curse Resistance), S4 에버나이트 아일 (Sakura island / 등대 / 블러드 나이트), S5 와일드 웨스트 (시즌 5 / Banks / 무역 열차 / merged 8-워존 map), and S6 그림자 열대우림 (faction warfare / permanent city destruction / 히어로 각성). 시즌 7 is confirmed by community creators as launching approximately August 2026 but no official theme/mechanic details have leaked publicly at the time of this research (2026-07-14). Each season introduces a new seasonal resource loop, a new PvP conflict pattern, and a designated hero for UR ascension or 각성 — and the trend from S5→S6 is toward fewer, longer war windows and permanent, high-stakes territorial outcomes rather than continuous grind (documented in 13-meta-competitive).

The seasonal cadence — what's fixed across every season

Since 시즌 3, every 라워 season has followed the same structural template, with only theme and specific mechanics varying:

  • 2-week pre-season (introduced in S4, retained through S6): countdown, faction/워존 grouping, rule preview, reward preview, 시즌 Boost, capitol conquest without adjacency, 얼라이언스 세이프 타임 configuration.
  • 7-day 시즌 Preview — daily challenges unlock the season's mechanics one at a time before main season begins.
  • 8-week main season — the actual war phase, divided into weekly milestones (city unlocks, faction contests, war windows).
  • Celebration / off-season week — final rewards, T-troop unlocks (T11 introduced in S4), migration prep to next season.
  • 서버 migration — at season end, players' accounts, HQ level, heroes, and tech carry forward; the map and all seasonal buildings/resources reset. 서버s merge across 워존s as populations decay (08-얼라이언스-systems cross-ref).

The 8-week main season subdivides consistently: Weeks 1–3 are build-out (buildings unlocked progressively, seasonal resource stockpiling), Weeks 4–6 are the main PvP conflict (향신료 전쟁 / 구리 전쟁 / 도시 Clash / 파벌 대전 prelims), and Weeks 7–8 are the season-defining endgame (수도 War, 조상 사원, 파벌 대전 final).

Length has crept upward: S3 was documented as ~8 weeks; S4 as "roughly 8–10 weeks" including celebration; total cycle including pre-season, main, and post-season is ~14 weeks per KR namu-wiki interpretation. Sources disagree on whether S4/S5 celebration weeks count as "the season" or as inter-season buffer.

시즌 3 — 골든 킹덤 (Egyptian desert)

Launch: ~6–7 weeks after 시즌 2 concludes on a given server (rolling per-server launch, not a global date). 사전 시즌 14-day countdown appears below the VS duel button. Duration: 8 weeks. Theme: Noah has crowned himself ruler of the "골든 킹덤" of ancient Egypt, unearthed the Monument of the Dead, and unleashed a mummy army and sandworms.

Map: 13×13 grid. Three building types populate the desert:

  • Cities (Levels 1–6, produce 100–600 향신료/hour)
  • Digging Strongholds (produce 미스릴)
  • Trade Posts (individually captured; Levels 1–5 unlock progressively weeks 2–3)

New seasonal resources: | 자원 | Purpose | Source | |---|---|---| | 향신료 | End-of-season ranking currency | 연맹 센터 production, 향신료 전쟁 victories, city control | | 미스릴 | Upgrades 축복의 샘s and Protector's 농장 | 둠 워커 first blood, Desert Ruins radar missions, 미스릴 티타늄 합금 공장 | | 신성수 | Upgrades 저주 연구실 | 축복의 샘s, first-time zombie kills | | Sandworm Scales | Desert Bazaar exchange currency (Protector Horns, Trap Landmine refills) | 모래벌레 위기 event participation |

New seasonal buildings: | 건물 | Max Level | Function | |---|---|---| | 저주 연구실 | 30 | Increases Curse Resistance; L30 unlocks 스칼렛 UR ascension | | 축복의 샘 (5 variants) | 30 | Produces 신성수; unlocked progressively | | 제단 | 30 | Buffs 사막 수호자 stats +0.5% per level | | Protector's 농장 | — | Houses 사막 수호자s (one-time-use elite units) | | 연맹 센터 | — | Produces 향신료, provides in-range buffs, prevents Sandworm knockback |

연맹 센터 secondary building unlocks:

  • Level 5: 미스릴 티타늄 합금 공장 (34,560/hour)
  • Level 10: Exploratory Well (600 오일/hour)
  • Level 15: 신성수 Hut (8,640/hour)
  • Level 20: Obelisk (6 Protector Horns/hour)

사막 수호자s — the signature S3 combat mechanic. Disposable elite units converted from battlefield casualties (10% of losses recovered via 정찰기 assist). Progressive squad buffs based on stationed count:

  • 500+ units: Power of Adaptation — +250 Curse Resistance, +25% ATK/DEF/HP
  • 750+ units: Death Shockwave — damages enemy units when squad defeated
  • 1000+ units: Blue Flame Spell — 1-hour debuff on defeated enemy base
  • 1250+ units: Protector 랠리 — 3× march speed bonus (Protector squads only)

Each hero carries 1/10 of stationed protector count. All participating Protectors vanish after combat — this is a burnable resource, not a persistent army.

Curse Resistance — new stat that gates PvE content. If your Curse Resistance is below a monster's requirement, your damage drops sharply (up to 99.9% reduction). 랠리 leader's resistance applies to all participants unless individual members have higher levels. This is why the 저주 연구실 is the single most important S3 building to keep upgraded.

Curse Resistance formula precision (open question status 2026-07-16): Multiple sources confirm the qualitative shape — "damage drops to as much as 99.9%" when CR is under monster requirement — but the exact CR-shortfall → damage-multiplier table remains gated behind Cpt Hedgehog's paywalled 시즌 3 pages and the Last War Vault Curse Resistance guide's supporter-only content [lastwarvault.com/guides/season-3/curse-resistance-guide/ WebFetch 2026-07-16 confirmed paywall]. What the KB does know from published summaries: (a) the penalty is a smooth-ish curve, not a hard cliff — small CR shortfalls yield small damage cuts, large shortfalls yield the 99.9% wipe; (b) the 랠리 leader's CR overrides participant CR only if the leader's is higher; (c) sandworm content is exempt from CR entirely. Downstream 시즌-3 tools should model CR as a 랠리-leader input with a "damage effectiveness" output of 100% if CR ≥ requirement, else scaled down toward 0.1% — the exact interpolation curve should be labeled TBD, from-in-game until a screenshot pull captures the S3 monster-CR-requirement table.

모래벌레 위기 event (14 days): Combat between 얼라이언스s generates "shock waves"; accumulated shock waves spawn:

  • 대형 모래벌레s — knock bases outside 연맹 센터 range to distant map locations (forced teleport)
  • Giant Sandworms — map-wide hazards, trapped via the Trap Landmine profession skill

향신료 전쟁 (Weeks 4–8): Two factions battle twice weekly across stages — Waiting → Declaration → Invitation → Preparation → War → End. Attackers can steal up to 30% of enemy 향신료 by destroying secondary buildings (5% each) and the 연맹 센터 (10%).

워로드 미사일 in 시즌 3 — larger blast than 시즌 2 (added 2026-07-16). The 워로드 미사일 mechanic ported from S2 fires a 35×35 tile 검은땅 blast in S3 (vs S2's 25×25). Same 3-min prep window, same public-워로드-base broadcast during prep, same 72-hour cooldown on skill after successful use or intercept-cancel. The wider blast means the S2 "park 5 tiles from the 화로" rule of thumb undershoots in S3 — plan 시즌-3 defense with a 10-tile clearance ring around any missile-targetable building. [lastwarhandbook.com 시즌 3 향신료 전쟁 & 파벌 대전 search-excerpt 2026-07-16; lastwargame.online 시즌 3 mechanics search-excerpt 2026-07-16 — both 티어 B; direct fetches 403]

파벌 대전 (Week 7): Days 1–6 = Invasion Right Contest; Day 7 = 수도 War (controlling 수도 yields +10M 향신료; each 무기 controlled = +5M 향신료).

Oasis Greenification event: Staged expansion of desertified land. Weeks 1–4 restrict greenification to city perimeters; weeks 5–7 allow free expansion once regions cross 80% thresholds, unlocking level progression toward capitol control.

얼라이언스 capture limits: 2 city war declarations/day, max 8 captured cities; 2 digging stronghold captures/day, max 8 captured.

직업s: Cap extended from 60 → 100. 외교관 unavailable. New seasonal skills: Sandworm Traps, Summon Mummies, Nature's Touch, Trap Landmines.

히어로 / weapons: 스칼렛 upgradeable to UR (week 3). Exclusive weapons unlock: 마샬 (Week 1), 스카일러 (Week 3), 맥그리거 (Week 6) via dedicated battle passes.

레이더 Missions — Desert Ruins: 7-level progression with 2.016% odds of reaching final stage. Yields Merit 훈장s, resources, escape mini-games. 얼라이언스 Compasses unlock at Level 7.
