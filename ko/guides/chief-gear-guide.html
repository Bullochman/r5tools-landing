The 라워 "치프" system is really four systems bolted together. (1) The profession skill tree, chosen at HQ 25 / 시즌 1 start, is the closest analogue to a talent tree: pick 엔지니어 (economy/development) or 전쟁 리더 / 워로드 (military/combat), spend skill points earned by kills + seasonal building upgrades on a shared-shape tree that unlocks skills every 5 levels up to a cap that rises across seasons (Lv 40 in S1, Lv 70 in S2, Lv 100 by S3+). (2) HQ level (1–35) is the master gate — hero level cap = HQ × 5 (max hero 175 at HQ 35), and every research node, troop tier, and building unlock sits behind it. (3) VIP level (1–18) is a permanent progression from spending or earning VIP points; big power tiers land at VIP 6 (second builder), VIP 8 (Agent 셜리), VIP 11 (+50% construction / +15% research during VIP 타임), and VIP 18 (+12.5% hero HP/ATK/DEF plus a designer-custom base skin). (4) 히어로 gear is 4 slots per hero (Gun / 칩 / 장갑 / 레이더), five rarities (Common → 레어 → 에픽 → 전설 → Mythic), Lv 1–40 with a stat pulse every 10 levels, then up to 5 stars post-40, then Mythic promotion at 장비 공장 20 (see 12-progression for the material-cost pipeline). Respec is available for the profession tree via 직업 스킬 초기화 서적; profession change requires a 전직 증명서; seasonal skill points refund automatically at season end.

1. The profession skill tree (라워's "chief talent tree")

The profession system unlocks at HQ 25 when 시즌 1 starts and is the primary "chief-side" character-progression system in the game.

Two active professions (a third, 외교관, is present in the game files but has no skill tree and cannot be selected as of 시즌 6):

  • 엔지니어 — economy/development focus. Cheaper builds, cheaper research, resource generation, welfare bonuses, defensive posture. 권장 default for most players; 엔지니어's compounding cost/speed discounts accelerate every other progression system.
  • 전쟁 리더 (aka 워로드) — combat/military focus. 랠리 damage, defensive multipliers, troop training speed, daily free tier-max squads. 권장 for 얼라이언스 leaders and active PvP participants once their base and troop stack are already mature.

스킬 tree layout. The tree is three columns wide:

  • Left column — economy skills (엔지니어) or combat-utility skills (워로드).
  • Middle column — construction/research speed (엔지니어) or training/troop-capacity (워로드).
  • Right column — seasonal exclusive skills — shared shape between both professions, reset automatically at season end.

Both professions technically share the same underlying tree shape and the seasonal (right) column is identical between them; the left and middle columns swap contents based on which profession you picked. That means a 워로드 and an 엔지니어 see mostly the same UI structure, but their skill effects differ.

스킬 points. Each profession level grants 1 skill point. Additional points come from three sources:

  1. Killing monsters on the world map (main early feed).
  2. Upgrading or activating seasonal buildings on the map.
  3. Participating in the island light/electricity system (S5+ mini-game feed).

You can also consume up to 10 "S1/S2/S3 스킬 포인트s" items per season (bounty-pack drops). Any 11th+ skill-point item consumed auto-converts to 10,000 profession XP instead of granting a point.

스킬 scaling. Individual skills upgrade Lv 1 → Lv 5, with higher levels either shortening the cooldown or strengthening the effect (or both). Not enough points exist to unlock every skill — prioritisation is required.

Respec / reset.

  • 직업 스킬 초기화 서적 — full tree wipe, refunds all spent points. Distributed sparingly through Light Hours event rewards, season pass, and paid packs.
  • 전직 증명서 — switches 엔지니어 ↔ 워로드 and refunds all points at the same time. Same distribution channel; scarcer than reset books.
  • 시즌al (right column) points are refunded automatically when the season ends — you never lose those.

엔지니어 early-season point-dump order (first ~30 skill points):

  1. 건물 Inspiration I (Lv 5) — snowballs profession XP off seasonal building upgrades, which then feeds every other skill purchase. Max first.
  2. Build for Free + 연맹 기술 for Free (Lv 10) — free speedups drip daily forever.
  3. 빠른 생산 (Lv 1, active) — fills resource gaps for the next building sprint.
  4. Build Now + 연맹 기술 Now (Lv 15, actives) — 10-h queue skips paired with the free-drip.
  5. Cooperative Construction + Cooperative 연맹 기술 (Lv 30) — team play multiplier once you have an active 얼라이언스 target.
  6. 자원-Saving + Recycling (Lv 35) — 5% cost cut on everything, compounding.
