라스트 워: 서바이벌's calendar is a stack of overlapping loops: a daily loop (무기 경쟁, six 4-hour phases), a weekly loop (얼라이언스 듀얼 VS Mon–Sat, plus rotating 좀비 시즈, 고스트 옵스, 프론트라인 브레이크스루, Bounty Hunter, Desert Storm), a bi-weekly loop (둠스데이, 좀비 인베이전, General's Trial), and the seasonal loop (8-week 시즌 with a mid-season 수도/왕국 대 왕국-equivalent war — 희귀 토양 전쟁 in S2 (see 06-season-2-frozen), 향신료 전쟁 in S3, 코퍼 War in S4, 골드vein / 골드en Palace in S5, faction war in S6 (see 07-future-seasons)). Between seasons the map opens up for off-season cross-server PvP (미티어라이트 아이언 워, 스카이 배틀프론트). Serious 얼라이언스s stack VS Day themes on top of matching 무기 경쟁 phases to score the same action twice, and reserve their biggest resource dumps for Friday (총동원) and Saturday (Enemy Buster). Preparation — pre-staged construction/research, hoarded speedups, badge stockpiles, and shielded inactive members — decides more of these events than any in-battle skill. Most timers run on server time (00:00 reset); PvP windows are typically 12:00–22:00 server time. 라워 does not currently ship a single "왕국 대 왕국" event by that exact name — the closest analogs are season-scoped 얼라이언스 wars (희토 / 향신료 / 코퍼 / 골드vein) and the off-season 스카이 배틀프론트 and 미티어라이트 아이언 워, which are the game's true inter-server PvP moments.

  1. Daily: 무기 경쟁 (24 h split into six 4-hour phases), Bosses Code, radar tasks, 둠 워커s on active days, hero-shard daily rotations.
  2. Weekly: 얼라이언스 듀얼 (VS, Mon–Sat), 고스트 옵스 (Thursday windows), 좀비 시즈 (once every 1–2 weeks), 데저트 스톰 배틀필드 (Fri), 프론트라인 브레이크스루 (Sun), Bounty Hunter (rotating), the Hot Deal / 반짝이는 시장 cycles.
  3. Bi-weekly: 좀비 인베이전 (glacieradon post-S2), 둠스데이 (Sun), General's Trial (4-day window), Bounty Hunter alternating cycles.
  4. 시즌al: 8-week season with a locked schedule of week-by-week unlocks that culminates in a mid-to-late season "big war" (희토 / 향신료 / 코퍼 / 골드vein 골드en Palace / faction war in S6). Between seasons: 스카이 배틀프론트 and 미티어라이트 아이언 워 fill the gap.

Serious 얼라이언스s treat these not as independent objects but as overlapping scoring surfaces. Every action (a T10 training completion, a valor badge spend, a 1-min speedup) can simultaneously credit 무기 경쟁, 얼라이언스 듀얼, and daily individual missions — provided you time it right. The R5 coordination job is to schedule those overlaps.

무기 경쟁 — the daily backbone

무기 경쟁 is the always-on daily event that structures every other planning decision. It resets at server midnight and splits the 24 h into six 4-hour phases. Five phase types cycle through those six slots:

  • 도시 건물 — building power completions, construction speedups
  • Unit Progression — training/upgrading soldiers (points based on final unit level), training speedups
  • 테크 연맹 기술 — tech power completions, research speedups
  • Drone Boost — drone combat data (10 pts each), stamina consumption
  • 히어로 Advancement — elite recruits, hero EXP ≥ 2,000, skill medals

The sixth slot repeats one of the above (varies by server day).

Scoring rule that decides everything else: actions score their full 무기 경쟁 value only when they complete during the matching phase. The exact same action completed in a mismatched phase scores nothing for 무기 경쟁. That is why "save it for the right phase" appears on every 무기 경쟁 guide.

Chest ladder per phase: three chests per phase — 1군 (1 badge), 1군 (2 badges), 3rd (3 badges), 6 badges per phase max. Daily badge chest ladder: Blue (2 badges), Purple (8 badges), 골드 (18 badges) — reaching 골드 requires clearing 3rd-tier chests in three separate phases (3×3 = 9 badges won't clear it; you need 18 total from Blues+Purples+골드s).

Stage 스왑: once per day, R5 can swap the point requirements and current progress of the current phase forward into a later phase. 연맹 코인 reset to zero after swap — useful when the currently active phase doesn't match your accumulated activities. Cannot rewind.

Enemy Buster (Saturday) safety rules:

  • Purchase shields (8h / 12h / 24h) covering the entire Saturday window before the server reset. 8-hour shields typically cost ~1,500 diamonds.
  • Remove defensive squads from walls (any troop kills you take on Saturday score for the enemy 얼라이언스).
  • Train troops beforehand; stockpile healing speedups.
  • Assign shield-compliance to a war coordinator R4 role. One unshielded high-power inactive can flip the day.
