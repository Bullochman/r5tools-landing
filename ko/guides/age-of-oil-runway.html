Progression in 라스트 워: 서바이벌 runs on six parallel tracks that every account works round-the-clock: hero shards → stars/skills, exclusive weapons or awakening, gear crafting → level → star → mythic, research (과학 센터 trees), chief profession (엔지니어 / 워로드) skill tree, and HQ-driven building/troop training. The universal shard curve is 975 shards to 5★ per hero (25 / 50 / 100 / 300 / 500 stacked, per KR BlueStacks and Theria Games) — identical across R/SR/SSR/UR rarities, only the shard scarcity differs (see 02-heroes for per-rarity roster). Gear runs from 레어 → 에픽 → 전설 (crafted from 4:1 refinement chains bottoming out in 192,000 나사s ≈ 47 days per 전설) → Mythic 5★ (an additional 50 전설 Blueprints + 10 Mythic Blueprints + 750M gold + 500K ore + 6,000 Dielectric Ceramic for a single piece to full 5★). 연맹 기술 is prioritized Development → Economy → 얼라이언스 듀얼 → 특수부대 (the T10 unlock path, gated by four ≥500M-coin gate nodes), with a second 과학 센터 ($10 pack, cleared District 102) doubling throughput. The 치프 profession unlocks at HQ 25 → 시즌 1 entry, caps at Level 40/70/100 by season, and forks 엔지니어 (economy) vs 워로드 (combat) sharing a common right-column of seasonal skills that reset each season. 시즌 6 replaces 전용 무기s with 히어로 각성 for new UR heroes (킴벌리 Week 1, DVA Week 3, 테슬라 Week 5) — the awakening skill overwrites the 특기 slot and maxes at skill level 40. Total power splits roughly 30–35% 병력 / 25–30% 건물 / 20–25% 연맹 기술 / 10–15% 히어로 / 5–10% Equipment / 5–8% 연맹 기술 — meaning research, buildings, and troop tiering dominate; heroes and gear are high-leverage but numerically smaller on the profile total.

1. The six progression tracks

Every 라워 account, from Day 1 to 시즌 6 whales, is simultaneously advancing on six independent tracks. Each track has its own resource pool, own timers, and own diminishing returns — and each one contributes to the same six-component power total. Understanding them as parallel systems (not a linear "leveling") is the mental model every guide converges on:

  1. 히어로 shards → stars → skill levels (02-heroes) — 975 shards to reach 5★ per hero.
  2. 전용 무기s (시즌 1–5) / 히어로 각성 (시즌 6+) — post-5★ hero investment.
  3. Gear crafting → level (1–40) → star (1–5 aka Mythic) — 4 slots per hero × 5 heroes per squad × N squads.
  4. 연맹 기술 (과학 센터 trees) — Development, Economy, 얼라이언스 듀얼, 특수부대, Defense Fortifications, Age of 오일, plus 병력/히어로/Garages.
  5. 치프 profession skill tree — 엔지니어 or 워로드 specialization plus a seasonal right-column that resets per season.
  6. 건물 + troop training (01-core-gameplay / 04-squads-combat) — HQ + 병영 + 병원 + 히어로 cap gating everything above.

Total account power is the sum of all six with the approximate weights: 병력 30–35%, 건물 25–30%, 연맹 기술 20–25%, 히어로 10–15%, Equipment 5–10%, 연맹 기술 5–8% (Last War Handbook — Power Calculation 가이드 2026; U7Buy Power Scaling Explained 2026). 효과ive combat power weights differently — Last War Handbook's TCP = (TP × 0.5) + (HP × 0.3) + (RP × 0.15) + (EP × 0.05) formula compresses buildings and 얼라이언스 tech to zero and elevates troops-and-heroes to the vast majority of what actually shows up on the battlefield.

The KR moneylifeblog phrasing frames it as "5 upgrades per tier" — 1★ costs 5 shards each of 5 upgrades (25 total), 2★ costs 10 × 5 (50 total), 3★ costs 20 × 5 (100 total), 4★ costs 60 × 5 (300 total), 5★ costs 100 × 5 (500 total). Same number, different granularity — this matches the in-game UI which asks for a small shard count per click.

조각 sources (Theria Games — 히어로 조각s 가이드; Cpt Hedgehog — 히어로 조각s Calculator; LDShop 2026):

  • 술집 — daily free recruits, mostly R/SR filler, occasional SSR/UR named shards.
  • 엘리트 모집 — SSR-guaranteed pool with UR pity.
  • Advanced (전설) 모집 — UR-focused, ~1% UR / ~4% SSR base rate.
  • 소원 — 200 소원 Tickets → 10 shards of a specific named hero. Primary way to farm a target UR without pure RNG.
  • Campaign farming — SSR shards drop from named stages (메이슨 at 4-3, 바이올렛 at 8-5). UR shards do not drop from campaign.
  • Trade & Plunder — ~2 shards per truck plundered.
  • Honor Store, Campaign Store, 연맹 상점, VIP Store — periodic rotating hero shards.
  • 레이더 tasks, 무기 경쟁, 얼라이언스 듀얼 milestones, 수도 War, 좀비 시즈 — event-milestone shards.
  • Battle Pass (Growth / 시즌 Pass) — named UR shards on premium and free tracks.
  • Black Market (post-season) — 시즌 currency spent on shard choice boxes; 무과금's long-tail.
