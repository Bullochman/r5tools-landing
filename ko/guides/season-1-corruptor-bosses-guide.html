시즌 1 — The 크림슨 플레이그 (a.k.a. "둠스데이")

시즌 1 first went live on May 13, 2025 on server range S101-S196, per 퍼스트펀 Group's official Facebook announcement (referenced in a "running for" community post at facebook.com/lastwarsurvival) [facebook.com/lastwarsurvival post 399609049726381, 2025-05-13, via search 2026-07-15 — 티어 A official social post]. Subsequent server groups roll into 시즌 1 on a staggered schedule as newer 워존s age past the ~90-100-day pre-season eligibility threshold; no consolidated public rollout calendar exists (filed to open-questions). The KB-derivable rule of thumb: any 워존 whose founding date is >~120 days old is either in-시즌-1 or has already progressed past it. See LW Atlas (lwatlas.com) and 농장Ops (lastwar.farm) for live per-server 시즌 status queries.

Terminology alert. Community shorthand — especially inside 얼라이언스 chat and on YouTube — often calls 시즌 1 "둠스데이." That is misleading. The official season title is "The 크림슨 플레이그." In-game, "둠스데이" is a separate, permanent, bi-weekly event (둠 워커 랠리) that has nothing to do with the 시즌 1 map or ministries. The two overlap thematically (post-apocalyptic, viruses, "doom" enemies), which is why the term stuck. This article covers the season. See 09-events for the standalone 둠스데이 event.

시즌 1 (officially "The 크림슨 플레이그") is 라스트 워: 서바이벌's first competitive season — a 48-day virus-themed campaign preceded by a 2-week Pre-시즌 and followed by a ~4-5 week Celebration phase. The map, called the Land of Fog and Rain, is grouped into 8 워존s/servers competing for a central prize called the 종말의 도시 (a level-7 super-capitol) which unlocks on Day 28. All player power is gated behind a new stat — 바이러스 저항 (VR) — which is grown almost exclusively by upgrading the 바이러스 연구소 (max +10,000 across 30 levels). Without enough 바이러스 저항 (VR), damage output can drop by 60-99% and incoming damage can be inflated by up to 900%, making the stat mandatory for city/stronghold captures and 파멸의 정예 kills. 얼라이언스 ranking is decided by 얼라이언스 영향력 포인트 accumulated from held cities (100k–1M each depending on level 1-6), culminating in the cross-워존 8강 토너먼트 tournament in weeks 5-7 (see 13-meta-competitive for the tournament tier structure). Small 얼라이언스s that cannot compete for city ranks may permanently switch to 건설자 연맹 mode in weeks 4-7 to trade cities-and-glory for stable construction rewards.

The term "둠스데이" that 얼라이언스 chats use for 시즌 1 is a misnomer inherited from the "파멸의 정예" / "둠 워커" enemy family. Official name: The 크림슨 플레이그. Known KR name: 크림슨 플레이그 (시즌 1).

1. 시즌 structure and duration

시즌 1 runs on this cadence, in order:

  1. Pre-시즌 — 2 weeks. Preview mechanics, 시즌 Boost bonus display, 시즌 Preview (unlock story tiles by hitting daily goals), and a Pre-시즌 수도 Conquest on Day 6 of Week 2. During pre-season 수도 Conquest, any 얼라이언스 can join without needing adjacent land — this is intentionally more open than the mid-season 수도 rules.
  2. 전술 시즌 — 8 weekly cycles (~56 days; theriagames reports 48 days — a ~1-week disagreement possibly reflecting a patch or "active content" vs "wind-down" terminology drift; see also 06-season-2-frozen for the same S2 disagreement). All seasonal buildings, resources, virus mechanics and territory-capture rules are live.
  3. Celebration Phase — ~4-5 weeks. Black Market 이벤트 (28 days), Bingo Task (Week 1), Champion Duel tournament, and Black Market Cash weekly challenges (Weeks 2-4). 시즌al buildings and their power bonuses are removed at the end of Celebration; permanent rewards (medals, hero shards, exclusive weapon activations) stay.

The season repeats mission "weeks" internally: each mission has a start day and a mission goal that unlocks a delayed number of days later. The whole 8-week schedule is server-time synchronised across all 8 워존s in a group so that 워존-vs-워존 events line up.

  • 요새s (level 1 only) — the entry-level territory. Guarded by a 오염자 (a new NPC mini-boss). Adjacency to at least one Stronghold you own is required to declare on Cities.
  • Cities, levels 1 through 6. Levels unlock on a schedule (see numbers table). Each city has a "durability" that scales with level and generates 얼라이언스 영향력 포인트 while held.
  • 종말의 도시 (level 7, map centre) — the former capital of the world, now Legion-held. 해금s on Day 28. Same rules as 수도 Capture but 8-hour contest.
  • 검은땅 (검은땅 in KR). PvP zones where cross-워존 attack is permitted (on foreign 워존s every day; on your own 워존 only on Saturdays).
  • 얼라이언스 Outposts. Placed on foreign 워존s during 서버 원정 (Week 3+). Max 4 outposts per 얼라이언스, one per foreign 워존. Construction-protected on placement.
