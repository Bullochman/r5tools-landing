A 라스트 워: 서바이벌 얼라이언스 is a 100-player cooperative unit built on a five-rank hierarchy (R5 → R1), a shared research tree paid in coin and diamond donations, a construction/research "help" mechanic that shaves seconds-to-hours off every teammate's timers, and a geographic clustering pattern ("the 하이브") that is the substrate for every 얼라이언스-level event and defense. The most rewarding 얼라이언스 activity outside PvP is Marshal's Guard (얼라이언스 Exercise), a 30-minute 랠리-marathon against five NPC tanks positioned inside a 3×3-tile event platform at the center of the 하이브 (the 라워_얼라이언스_Ops_Center 하이브 Grid Manager plans this layout); the largest capturable seasonal objective is the 21×21-tile 요새, and Ring 1 members must sit against its outer mud to deny enemy landing zones. 얼라이언스 듀얼 (VS) is the recurring 6-day cross-server tournament (Mon–Sat, first to 7 victory points wins) that pairs with the 무기 경쟁 event and is the primary way 얼라이언스s compete for server-level status. R5 assigns the four titled R4 slots — 워로드, 모집관, 여신, 집사 — and in 시즌 6 these titles gate the four 얼라이언스 skills (세르펜트 브레스, 코브라 결계, 밤 군대, 페더 라이트닝). The R4 cap starts at 8 and rises to 10 at 연맹 선물 Level 10.

Positioning. The 3×3 event platform is placed as close to the 얼라이언스 하이브 as possible to minimize march time. 랠리 leaders (R5, titled R4s, top-power R3s) sit in Ring 1 against the platform. See "하이브 formations" below.

하이브 formations

The "하이브" is the geographic clustering of 얼라이언스 bases around 얼라이언스 buildings (turrets, flag) and event objects (Marshal's Guard platform, 요새). 하이브 discipline is one of the most-ignored and most-consequential 얼라이언스 rules — a scattered 얼라이언스 loses defensive reinforcement coverage, march-time-limited events like Marshal's Guard, and 좀비 시즈 reinforcement chains.

Base and event object sizes (tiles).

  • Player HQ / city: 2×2 tiles per base footprint (per 하이브-planner tools; note some sources treat member "cells" as 3×3 in planner UIs where one cell = one base + 1-tile buffer).
  • Marshal's Guard platform: 3×3 tiles (1 event cell).
  • 연맹 센터 (world building placement varies; the on-base structure is 5-tile-footprint per planners): 5×5 tiles.
  • 미사일 silo (turret/rocket world object): 4×4 tiles.
  • 요새 seasonal fortress: 21×21 tiles (a 7-cell × 7-cell block in a 3-tile-per-cell planner grid).

Marshal's Guard 하이브 shape.

  • Center = the 3×3 event platform (1 planner cell).
  • 권장 surrounding grid: 10×10 cells.
  • 멤버s pack tightly against the platform and each other, no gaps needed for this event.
  • Ring capacity ≈ ring distance × 8 (a single ring around the 3×3 center has 8 slots; 1군 ring 16; 3rd ring 24).
  • Strongest players (R5 → titled R4 → untitled R4 → R3 by total power) fill Ring 1 first so their rallies enjoy shorter march time.

요새 하이브 shape (defensive).

The Stronghold is a 21×21-tile fortress surrounded by mud. Any enemy who lands within 9 tiles of the mud can 랠리 the Stronghold — so the defensive job of the 하이브 is to occupy those tiles so densely that no enemy can teleport a 3×3 city into a gap.

The convention used by the 얼라이언스 하이브 Grid Manager tool (and echoed by most competitive 얼라이언스s) is:

  • 2-tile empty gap between every pair of 얼라이언스 members, in every direction, and between rings. This gap is narrow enough to deny a 3×3 enemy footprint but wide enough that a legitimate teammate can land there if needed.
  • Corner-anchored rings: each ring is a hollow rectangular frame whose four corners are always occupied. Inner edges pack at pitch 5 tiles (3-tile member + 2-tile gap) from corner to corner. The remaining edge stub may be 1 tile — still too narrow for an enemy 3×3 to slot in.

요새 (요새) — capture rules

시즌 1 seasonal building. 21×21-tile fortress guarded by NPC infected zombies scaled to the stronghold's level.

  • Capture cap: 2 요새s per 얼라이언스 per day (global across all 워존s).
  • Adjacency rule: you cannot capture an isolated stronghold — you must own an adjacent stronghold or (in some 워존s) an outpost connected to a Level 1 or 2 요새.
  • Requirement: 바이러스 저항 (VR) stat.
  • 얼라이언스 stronghold cap starts at 3. Each captured city adds capacity: +1 per L1–L5 city, +2 per L6 city.
  • Protection after capture: 36 hours.
  • Abandon: takes 1 hour. If protection expires mid-abandon, the abandon countdown continues.
  • Defending doesn't consume your daily capture cap.
