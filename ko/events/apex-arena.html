스톰 아레나 / 에이펙스 아레나 / 3v3 브롤 — the arena ecosystem (added 2026-07-15)

The KB was missing coverage of the arena events. Per [lastwarhandbook.com/guides/arena-system-master-guide search excerpt, 2026-07-15, 티어 B]:

Three arena modes:

  1. 3v3 브롤 — standard daily arena; alternates every 2 weeks with 스톰 아레나
  2. 스톰 아레나 — 2-week rotation with 3v3 브롤; different rules per cycle
  3. 에이펙스 아레나 — restricted to Top 200 players by total hero power on your server; weekly settlement rewards; the high-end competitive surface

무과금 daily action: open 3v3 브롤, give the daily 3 thumbs-ups — this earns diamonds per day for ~30 seconds of effort. Cited as the single most-important 30-second 무과금 habit.

Weekly settlement: rewards distributed based on 아레나 골드 total + resulting rank at weekly reset. Higher ranks receive proportionally greater diamond/materials payouts.
