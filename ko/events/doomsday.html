라스트 워: 서바이벌's calendar is a stack of overlapping loops: a daily loop (무기 경쟁, six 4-hour phases), a weekly loop (얼라이언스 듀얼 VS Mon–Sat, plus rotating 좀비 시즈, 고스트 옵스, 프론트라인 브레이크스루, Bounty Hunter, Desert Storm), a bi-weekly loop (둠스데이, 좀비 인베이전, General's Trial), and the seasonal loop (8-week 시즌 with a mid-season 수도/왕국 대 왕국-equivalent war — 희귀 토양 전쟁 in S2 (see 06-season-2-frozen), 향신료 전쟁 in S3, 코퍼 War in S4, 골드vein / 골드en Palace in S5, faction war in S6 (see 07-future-seasons)). Between seasons the map opens up for off-season cross-server PvP (미티어라이트 아이언 워, 스카이 배틀프론트). Serious 얼라이언스s stack VS Day themes on top of matching 무기 경쟁 phases to score the same action twice, and reserve their biggest resource dumps for Friday (총동원) and Saturday (Enemy Buster). Preparation — pre-staged construction/research, hoarded speedups, badge stockpiles, and shielded inactive members — decides more of these events than any in-battle skill. Most timers run on server time (00:00 reset); PvP windows are typically 12:00–22:00 server time. 라워 does not currently ship a single "왕국 대 왕국" event by that exact name — the closest analogs are season-scoped 얼라이언스 wars (희토 / 향신료 / 코퍼 / 골드vein) and the off-season 스카이 배틀프론트 and 미티어라이트 아이언 워, which are the game's true inter-server PvP moments.

  1. Daily: 무기 경쟁 (24 h split into six 4-hour phases), Bosses Code, radar tasks, 둠 워커s on active days, hero-shard daily rotations.
  2. Weekly: 얼라이언스 듀얼 (VS, Mon–Sat), 고스트 옵스 (Thursday windows), 좀비 시즈 (once every 1–2 weeks), 데저트 스톰 배틀필드 (Fri), 프론트라인 브레이크스루 (Sun), Bounty Hunter (rotating), the Hot Deal / 반짝이는 시장 cycles.
  3. Bi-weekly: 좀비 인베이전 (glacieradon post-S2), 둠스데이 (Sun), General's Trial (4-day window), Bounty Hunter alternating cycles.
  4. 시즌al: 8-week season with a locked schedule of week-by-week unlocks that culminates in a mid-to-late season "big war" (희토 / 향신료 / 코퍼 / 골드vein 골드en Palace / faction war in S6). Between seasons: 스카이 배틀프론트 and 미티어라이트 아이언 워 fill the gap.
  • S1 둠스데이: 수도 Conquest (weeks 2, 4, 6 pre-season windows) + weekly 수도 rotation among top 3 얼라이언스s (RPX/OTL/UYL cycle, historically 8h Fridays 9am CST though modern schedule may differ per server).
  • S2 시즌 2 (폴라 스톰): 희귀 토양 전쟁 — 4-week faction war (weeks 4–7) between 반군 vs 헌병대 factions, top 200 얼라이언스s split 100/100 across 8 워존s. Fight days Wednesday + Saturday, roles alternate weekly.
  • S3 골든 킹덤: 향신료 전쟁 — Wed/Sat fights between Scarlet Legion and 골드en Tribe factions. Week 7 is the Invasion Right Contest (Days 1–6 accumulate points; Day 7 수도 War with +10 M 향신료 for holder).
  • S4 에버나이트 아일: 코퍼 War (faction war analog).
  • S5 와일드 웨스트: 골드vein War + 골드en Palace Conquest — 골드en Palace attackable Saturdays of weeks 5, 6, 7 at 13:00 server time for 1-hour windows. Only 얼라이언스s with adjacent L10 은행 요새 or L10 도시 can attack. First to 100% capture wins; both defender and 골드en Palace can garrison up to 100 squads. Reward tier: ~42k Honor 연맹 코인 from main chests (3M pts) + 6–10k Honor 연맹 코인 from kill leaderboards.
  • S6 그림자 열대우림 / 잃어버린 열대우림 (April 2026 launch): 도시 파괴 system + Faction 테크 + Pacts + staggered 히어로 각성 (킴벌리 Wk1, DVA Wk3, 테슬라 Wk5 per LDShop / Cpt-Hedge / majority live-season reporting; Lootbar's Wk6 claim is the outlier, cross-referenced 02-heroes + 12-progression + 13-meta-competitive). Faction selection at season start is strategically decisive.

둠스데이 — bi-weekly Sunday 둠 워커 rush

Bi-weekly Sundays. 둠 워커s spawn every 4 hours across the 워존 (typically killed within minutes due to server-wide activity). Participation requires base level 8+.

Rewards in three categories:

  1. 첫킬 보상s, claimable up to 3x per event
  2. Achievement rewards from server-wide milestones
  3. Damage-ranking rewards distributed post-event (top 200 damage dealers)

While 둠 워커s are attacking bases, idle-unit death rate decreases. Walkers do not breach the HQ structure itself.

둠 워커 level unlocks by server-season progression (added 2026-07-15 per [lastwargame.online/en/doomsday-event/ WebSearch excerpt, 2026-07-15, 티어 B]):

Weekly event pair rotation: the odd/even week number since the server's current season started decides which pair runs — General's Trial + 둠스데이 on one parity, 좀비 인베이전 + Rampage Bosses on the other. This resolves an open question about why a specific week seems to skip 둠스데이 — it's the alternating parity, not a bug.

이벤트 무과금 should attend for shards / rewards but not sweat rankings:

  • General's Trial — attempt highest solo tier that you can clear; don't 랠리-fail higher tiers just for participation.
  • 좀비 시즈 — participate for team score; do not spend healing speedups on losses (breached bases take no further hits).
  • 둠스데이 — first 3 kills give guaranteed rewards; top-200 damage ranking is a P2W push not worth 무과금 speedup spend.
  • 프론트라인 브레이크스루 — only 3 participations required for rewards; don't grind the whole 5-level ladder unless it's your daily.
