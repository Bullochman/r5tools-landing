라스트 워: 서바이벌's calendar is a stack of overlapping loops: a daily loop (무기 경쟁, six 4-hour phases), a weekly loop (얼라이언스 듀얼 VS Mon–Sat, plus rotating 좀비 시즈, 고스트 옵스, 프론트라인 브레이크스루, Bounty Hunter, Desert Storm), a bi-weekly loop (둠스데이, 좀비 인베이전, General's Trial), and the seasonal loop (8-week 시즌 with a mid-season 수도/왕국 대 왕국-equivalent war — 희귀 토양 전쟁 in S2 (see 06-season-2-frozen), 향신료 전쟁 in S3, 코퍼 War in S4, 골드vein / 골드en Palace in S5, faction war in S6 (see 07-future-seasons)). Between seasons the map opens up for off-season cross-server PvP (미티어라이트 아이언 워, 스카이 배틀프론트). Serious 얼라이언스s stack VS Day themes on top of matching 무기 경쟁 phases to score the same action twice, and reserve their biggest resource dumps for Friday (총동원) and Saturday (Enemy Buster). Preparation — pre-staged construction/research, hoarded speedups, badge stockpiles, and shielded inactive members — decides more of these events than any in-battle skill. Most timers run on server time (00:00 reset); PvP windows are typically 12:00–22:00 server time. 라워 does not currently ship a single "왕국 대 왕국" event by that exact name — the closest analogs are season-scoped 얼라이언스 wars (희토 / 향신료 / 코퍼 / 골드vein) and the off-season 스카이 배틀프론트 and 미티어라이트 아이언 워, which are the game's true inter-server PvP moments.

  • S1 둠스데이: 수도 Conquest (weeks 2, 4, 6 pre-season windows) + weekly 수도 rotation among top 3 얼라이언스s (RPX/OTL/UYL cycle, historically 8h Fridays 9am CST though modern schedule may differ per server).
  • S2 시즌 2 (폴라 스톰): 희귀 토양 전쟁 — 4-week faction war (weeks 4–7) between 반군 vs 헌병대 factions, top 200 얼라이언스s split 100/100 across 8 워존s. Fight days Wednesday + Saturday, roles alternate weekly.
  • S3 골든 킹덤: 향신료 전쟁 — Wed/Sat fights between Scarlet Legion and 골드en Tribe factions. Week 7 is the Invasion Right Contest (Days 1–6 accumulate points; Day 7 수도 War with +10 M 향신료 for holder).
  • S4 에버나이트 아일: 코퍼 War (faction war analog).
  • S5 와일드 웨스트: 골드vein War + 골드en Palace Conquest — 골드en Palace attackable Saturdays of weeks 5, 6, 7 at 13:00 server time for 1-hour windows. Only 얼라이언스s with adjacent L10 은행 요새 or L10 도시 can attack. First to 100% capture wins; both defender and 골드en Palace can garrison up to 100 squads. Reward tier: ~42k Honor 연맹 코인 from main chests (3M pts) + 6–10k Honor 연맹 코인 from kill leaderboards.
  • S6 그림자 열대우림 / 잃어버린 열대우림 (April 2026 launch): 도시 파괴 system + Faction 테크 + Pacts + staggered 히어로 각성 (킴벌리 Wk1, DVA Wk3, 테슬라 Wk5 per LDShop / Cpt-Hedge / majority live-season reporting; Lootbar's Wk6 claim is the outlier, cross-referenced 02-heroes + 12-progression + 13-meta-competitive). Faction selection at season start is strategically decisive.

시즌 3 향신료 전쟁 + 시즌 5 골드en Palace + 시즌 6 Faction War

향신료 전쟁 (S3 골든 킹덤):

  • Wed + Sat fight rounds (2 rounds/week)
  • Factions: Scarlet Legion vs 골드en Tribe
  • Week 7: Invasion Right Contest (Days 1–6 points from Apex/스톰 아레나, Advanced 스톰 아레나, Desert Storm, 얼라이언스 듀얼, Trade Truck Plunder)
  • Week 7 Day 7: 수도 War — 수도 control = +10 M 향신료, each 무기 = +5 M 향신료

코퍼 War (S4 에버나이트 아일): faction war analog; same alternating-attacker cadence.

골드vein War + 골드en Palace Conquest (S5 와일드 웨스트):

  • 골드en Palace attackable Sat weeks 5, 6, 7 at 13:00 server time, 1-hour windows
  • Only 얼라이언스s with adjacent L10 은행 요새 OR L10 도시 can attack
  • First to 100% capture = wins
  • 방어군 cap: 100 squads (both defender and 골드en Palace)
  • Rewards: ~42k Honor Pts from main chests (3M pts) + 6–10k Honor Pts from kill leaderboards

시즌 6 그림자 열대우림 / 잃어버린 열대우림 (April 2026):

  • 도시 파괴 mechanic (permanently remove weaker city with coordinated 랠리 + no active Pact)
  • Faction 테크 + Pacts (7-day non-aggression, same-faction only)
  • Staggered 히어로 각성 rollout: 킴벌리 Wk1, DVA Wk3, 테슬라 Wk5 (per LDShop / Cpt-Hedge / majority live-season reporting; Lootbar cites Wk6 — outlier)
  • 각성 조각s drop from S6 ruins + weekly events

향신료 전쟁 (S3 골든 킹덤):

  • Wed + Sat fight rounds (2 rounds/week)
  • Factions: Scarlet Legion vs 골드en Tribe
  • Week 7: Invasion Right Contest (Days 1–6 points from Apex/스톰 아레나, Advanced 스톰 아레나, Desert Storm, 얼라이언스 듀얼, Trade Truck Plunder)

  • 02-heroes — 얼라이언스 듀얼 Thursday is the day hero shards are spent for max scoring. 고스트 옵스 on Thursday drops shards for heroes not in 술집 (루시우스, 모리슨, 아담, 윌리엄스, 스카일러, 맥그리거, 스테트만, 피오나). 히어로 각성 in 시즌 6 turns weekly-event 각성 조각 yields into a leaderboard race. 킴벌리's Barrage Strike is the single most-cited 좀비 시즈 skill.
  • 03-chief and 04-squads-combat — Enemy Buster (VS Saturday) is where 치프 gear + squad power decides how many 랠리 kills you can convert. Combat skill spikes matter most in Desert Storm, Winter Storm, and season-war fight days.
  • 05-season-1-crimson-plague — 수도 Conquest is a S1 pre-season event (Day 6 of Pre-시즌 Wk 2, up to 4 hours). Weekly 수도 rotation historically used a 3-얼라이언스 cycle (RPX/OTL/UYL) with 4/2/2-hour splits.
  • 06-season-2-frozen — 희귀 토양 전쟁 is the 시즌 2 event stack: 반군 vs 헌병대 faction split, Wed/Sat fight days weeks 4–7, 워로드 missiles, 얼라이언스 furnace 20%/30% plunder cap. 시즌 length affects when 글레이시아라돈 becomes accessible.
  • 07-future-seasons — 향신료 전쟁 (S3), 코퍼 War (S4), 골드vein War + 골드en Palace (S5), S6 그림자 열대우림 faction war with 도시 파괴. Same faction/attacker rotation cadence; different flavor resources.
  • 12-progression — event-earned 스킬 훈장s / Blueprints / 각성 조각s feed the six progression tracks; 무기 경쟁 is the primary 스킬 훈장 source (up to 8,000/day).
  • 08-얼라이언스-systems — 얼라이언스 Exercise 5% R5/priority-R4 랠리 bonus; 좀비 시즈 48h 얼라이언스-membership requirement; 고스트 옵스 assist limits; Desert Storm's top-32 얼라이언스 rank floor; 희귀 토양 전쟁 declaration group matching (same-ranking only).
  • 10-economy — Speedups are the master resource across every scoring event. Save 1-min speedups for their matching 무기 경쟁 phases + VS 데이. 다이아 for shields (~1,500 for 8h) are the highest-ROI PvP-day spend.
  • 11-monetization — 반짝이는 시장 (Mythic Gear Blueprints, 3,000 coins to clear), Custom Weekly Pass, Special 훈련 Pass, Drone 훈련 Pass, 히어로 Growth Pass — all Hot Deal rotations. New Year Battle Pass + Anniversary bundles are the two largest monetization windows per year.
  • 13-meta-competitive — 얼라이언스 듀얼 League tier + 희토 / 향신료 / 골드vein leaderboards are the meta-competitive surface. 스카이 배틀프론트 and 미티어라이트 아이언 워 fill the off-season leaderboard void.
  • 14-ui-reference — 이벤트 Hub is the master calendar; 무기 경쟁 dashboard shows current phase; 얼라이언스 듀얼 dashboard shows Day theme + point tally; 수도 UI shows Ministry application queue.

워로드 미사일: high-level attackers can launch a 워로드 미사일 at an enemy 얼라이언스 화로:

  • 3-minute (180-second) preparation window after launch during which the 워로드's own base is revealed to the entire server via world-chat broadcast and the 워로드 enters "전쟁 열풍" status. [lastwar-tutorial.com 희토 War, 2026-07-15; lastwar-tutorial.com Ultimate Strategy 가이드, 2026-07-15]
  • Launch can be attempted during both the Preparation Stage and the Combat Stage of a war round, not only during the 30-minute Prep. [lastwar-tutorial.com Ultimate Strategy 가이드, 2026-07-15]
  • Impact zone: 25×25 tile grid ("검은땅") in 시즌 2 that lasts 1 hour, during which bases inside cannot activate shields. 화로 takes 30,000 durability damage on a successful impact. [lastwar-tutorial.com 희토 War, 2026-07-15]
  • 시즌 3 blast size expansion (added 2026-07-16): 시즌 3 골든 킹덤 / 향신료 전쟁 / 파벌 대전 expands the same 워로드 미사일 mechanic to a 35×35 tile blast. 시즌 2 remains 25×25. Any downstream 워로드 미사일 simulator tool that spans multiple seasons must key blast size off the current season. [lastwarhandbook.com 시즌 3 향신료 전쟁 & 파벌 대전 Complete 가이드 search-excerpt 2026-07-16 티어 B; direct fetch 403]
  • Multiple 워로드s from allied attacker 얼라이언스s can queue simultaneous missiles at the same furnace. [lastwar-tutorial.com 희토 War, 2026-07-15]

라스트 워: 서바이벌 runs on an 8-week seasonal treadmill: every server rotates through a fresh themed map with new resources, buildings, PvP events, and hero/weapon rollouts. Since 시즌 2 (폴라 스톰) ended, 퍼스트펀 has shipped four full seasons — S3 골든 킹덤 (Egyptian desert / 향신료 전쟁 / Curse Resistance), S4 에버나이트 아일 (Sakura island / 등대 / 블러드 나이트), S5 와일드 웨스트 (시즌 5 / Banks / 무역 열차 / merged 8-워존 map), and S6 그림자 열대우림 (faction warfare / permanent city destruction / 히어로 각성). 시즌 7 is confirmed by community creators as launching approximately August 2026 but no official theme/mechanic details have leaked publicly at the time of this research (2026-07-14). Each season introduces a new seasonal resource loop, a new PvP conflict pattern, and a designated hero for UR ascension or 각성 — and the trend from S5→S6 is toward fewer, longer war windows and permanent, high-stakes territorial outcomes rather than continuous grind (documented in 13-meta-competitive).

The 8-week main season subdivides consistently: Weeks 1–3 are build-out (buildings unlocked progressively, seasonal resource stockpiling), Weeks 4–6 are the main PvP conflict (향신료 전쟁 / 구리 전쟁 / 도시 Clash / 파벌 대전 prelims), and Weeks 7–8 are the season-defining endgame (수도 War, 조상 사원, 파벌 대전 final).
