얼라이언스 듀얼 (VS 데이)

The main weekly 얼라이언스 competition. Also called "얼라이언스 VS" or just "VS." Runs Mon–Sat.

Minimum entry: HQ 10+, 얼라이언스 has ≥20 members.

Additional gate confirmed 2026-07-15: only the top 32 얼라이언스s in the 워존 are eligible to participate — even at ≥20 members and HQ 10+, an 얼라이언스 ranked #33 or lower in server power is locked out until it climbs the leaderboard [treyexgaming.com/last-war-survival-얼라이언스-duel-guide/ WebFetch 2026-07-15, 티어 B; corroborated by lastwargame.online/en/game-얼라이언스-duel/ search 2026-07-15]. This is a load-bearing gate for the R5-tools "who should we recruit next" question — top-32 threshold is the practical eligibility bar, not the ≥20-member floor.

Bracket / league structure (2026-07-15 addition): 얼라이언스s are grouped into leagues of 16 for a 4-week season with weekly promote/demote by duel result [treyexgaming.com]. So the ≥20-member and HQ-10 gates get an 얼라이언스 in the door; the 4-week league then decides whether it climbs or drops. R5s should treat the first duel of a new league as the highest-leverage matchup — a Week-1 loss locks the 얼라이언스 out of the top bracket for the rest of the season.

Pairing: cross-server. Your 얼라이언스 is matched against one from another server for the week. Matchmaking uses 얼라이언스 power and server age; the exact algorithm is not published. Newer or weaker 얼라이언스s may pair intra-server at low tiers.

Each day gives points for specific in-game actions (radar missions on Mon, construction speedups on Tue, research speedups on Wed, hero XP on Thu, troop training on Fri, killing enemy troops on Sat). Actions taken during the matching 무기 경쟁 phase double-dip — this alignment is required, not optional, for competitive 얼라이언스s.

Because Day 6 alone is worth 4 VPs, winning it plus any 3 of Days 2–5 flips a losing week. Enemy Buster carries a 5× point multiplier for killing troops belonging to the paired duel 얼라이언스 vs. any other kills — the single largest scoring lever in the format. Any offensive action drops your shield, so Day 6 is a shield/kill trade throughout the 24 hours.

The first 5 minutes after daily reset score nothing — waiting for the countdown before dumping speedups or claiming radar chests is standard practice.
