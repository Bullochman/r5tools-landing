Additional S6 mechanics:

  • 얼라이언스 협약s — 7-day non-aggression treaties between same-faction 얼라이언스s. Prevent 도시 파괴. Share adjacency benefits and open joint communication channels for up to 200 members. Make faction selection load-bearing.
  • 파벌 기술 — faction-wide research tree unlocked via Stronghold donations, applies buffs to all faction members after individual contribution thresholds met.
  • 워 메리트 economy — earned via kills, captures, reinforcements, donations, rankings. 티어s Bronze → 엘리트 with corresponding daily stipends and stat bonuses. 워 메리트 Shop trades.
  • 균류 연구소 (max ~L60) — enhances 바이러스 저항 (VR) (analog to 광전기 실험실 / 저주 연구실).
  • 포자 공장 (max L30) — produces 포자s.
  • 성역 timer buff: doubled from 10 → 20 minutes (occupies 1/3 of the 60-minute attack window). Sources call this a "stealth change" that significantly restricts strategic flexibility.
  • 얼라이언스 스킬 cooldowns: 3–6 days.
  • Card meta shift: the popular "4+1 hybrid squad strategy" was removed from 글로벌 익스페디션, forcing pure 탱크 / 미사일 / 항공기 formations backed by attribute-specific Tactics Cards.
  • Beneath the Ruins (added 2026-07-15) — 시즌 6's dedicated minigame, launched in TWO phases [Cpt Hedgehog Beneath the Ruins, 2026-07-15; TheGamesEdge Beneath the Ruins 가이드, 2026-07-15]:
  • Solo Mode (Week 1 Day 2+): descending-platformer challenge. 가이드 your character downward through an ever-deepening stage, avoiding hazards. 1 new stage unlocks per day. Missed days stack — play back-to-back to catch up. Platform types: Regular (safe), Crumbly (breaks after ~1 sec), Spike (1 HP damage on landing — avoid at low HP), Travelators (moving belts — go with the flow, fighting them costs time). HP regenerates when standing on safe platforms. Only left/right movement. Reward: Rainforest Mushrooms (larger on later stages), plus season-exclusive gifts.
  • PvP Duel Mode (Week 2+): competitive descent against another commander, same stage simultaneously, first to bottom wins. No HP regeneration. Entry paid in 워 메리트 deposit up to 500 (or 1,500 with 시즌 스킬). Rewards up to 3,000 워 메리트 per match [TheGamesEdge, 2026-07-15]. Wager spirit similar to S5's 하이눈 Duel.
  • 글로벌 익스페디션 — new dedicated building & schedule (added 2026-07-15) — moved out of the Alert 미사일 발사대 into a new dedicated 원정 Base building [LDShop S6 글로벌 익스페디션 가이드, 2026-07-15]. Three GE rounds per season, each 14 days long (roughly Weeks 2, 4, 6 of the main season). Named 각성 조각s drop at rank thresholds — one of the best regular free shard sources in the game.
