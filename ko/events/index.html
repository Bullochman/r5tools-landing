Every recurring event

  • 무기 경쟁 — The daily backbone — 5 daily-rotating task-router chests worth ~50% of daily reward income.
  • 얼라이언스 듀얼 VS — The weekly backbone — 6-day scored VS-Day event with 얼라이언스 vs 얼라이언스 matchmaking above top-32 워존 gate.
  • VS 데이 — Weekly 얼라이언스 듀얼 VS competition — the phase-stack optimizer's target.
  • 왕국 대 왕국 — Cross-server war — 라워 ships this as 크로스서버 전쟁 랠리, not the traditional 왕국 대 왕국.
  • 좀비 시즈 — Coop zombie wave defense — L1 base wave, harder tiers per season.
  • 좀비 인베이전 — Bi-weekly world-boss farm.
  • Zombie Rush — 5v5 team boss battle, bi-weekly Sundays.
  • Zombie Cataclysm — Alternate name/mode of the Sunday 5v5 team boss battle.
  • 둠스데이 — Bi-weekly Sunday 둠 워커 rush — 시즌 1 flagship event still present in later seasons.
  • 고스트 옵스 — Thursday hero-shard hunt event.
  • 데저트 스톰 배틀필드 — Friday 30-minute PvP — 15 buildings capturable per side.
  • 윈터 스톰 배틀필드 — 5v5 arena mode with a new 2026 point-based reward system.
  • 얼라이언스 Exercise — 30-minute 얼라이언스 tank 랠리 — the entry-level 랠리 practice.
  • General's Trial — 4-day bi-weekly single-squad-power PvE gauntlet.
  • 프론트라인 브레이크스루 — 5-level Sunday roguelike PvE.
  • Wanted Bosses — Weekly Codes 39/64/87 rotation — hero-type-buffed boss damage race.
  • Bounty Hunter — Bullet-shooting redemption event.
  • 반짝이는 시장 — Hot Deal cycle — approximately monthly, 6-day windows.
  • 미티어라이트 아이언 워 — Off-season cross-server PvP over meteorite iron.
  • 스카이 배틀프론트 — Off-season 얼라이언스 airship war — 7-day event.
  • 희귀 토양 전쟁 — 시즌 2 flagship faction war — blizzard-and-plunder mechanic.
  • 향신료 전쟁 — 시즌 3 flagship — 100–600/hr spice per city, 30% steal cap, Wed/Sat war days weeks 4–8.
  • 구리 전쟁 — 시즌 4 flagship — 8-round 15% cap, Wed/Sat weeks 4–7.
  • 하이눈 — 시즌 5 Bank event — 15k/day cap, Wed/Sat war days.
  • 제단 Conquest — 시즌 6 Tuesday altar contest — max 3 held per faction, 100% capture required.
  • 얼라이언스 듀얼 Warm-up Match — New 2026-07 pre-league match variant — practice vs matchmaking.
  • 얼라이언스 듀얼 League Match — Scored competitive 얼라이언스 듀얼 with matchmaking above top-32 워존 gate.
  • 지휘관 최고봉 Sprint — One-time server-launch 7-day sprint.
  • Strongest 총사령관 — 서버-launch celebration event, top-commander companion.
  • Anniversary Celebration — Yearly celebration cycle with retention rewards and returning-hero banners.
  • New Year Celebration — Recurring seasonal celebration event stack.
  • 스톰 아레나 — 아레나 ecosystem — 에이펙스 아레나 / 3v3 브롤 companion mode.
  • 에이펙스 아레나 — Top-tier arena ladder for individual chiefs.
  • 3v3 브롤 — 3-squad arena mode within the 스톰 아레나 ecosystem.
  • Ministry Assignments — 시즌 1 대통령-appointed cabinet slots — Ministry buffs matter across every mode.
  • 수도 War — 시즌 1 flagship — 수도 Conquest chooses the 대통령 who assigns 내각.
  • 수도 Conquest — Alternate name for 수도 War — 시즌 1 finale.
  • 시즌 Battle Pass — Recurring per-season battle pass — hero-shard milestone track.
  • Growth Pass — Recurring general-progression battle pass — daily/weekly resource + shard track.
  • 각성 Battle Pass — 시즌 6 hero-각성 battle pass — ~70 shards per awakening week.
  • 전용 무기 Battle Pass — 시즌 1–5 — ~50 EW shards per pass, unlock-in-window.
