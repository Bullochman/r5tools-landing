Mission types:

  • UR\*: premium, drone parts + hero shards + exclusive weapon shards
  • UR: standard high-tier (L4 drops 1 shard, L5 drops 2 shards, star UR drops 5 shards)
  • SSR (purple): lower tier

이벤트 무과금 should hit every cycle (tier-1 priority):

  1. Wanted Bosses (Codes 39/64/87) — 5 free attempts/day, no cost, Upgrade 광석 + Synthetic Resin compounds long-term. Never skip a day.
  2. 무기 경쟁 — free daily; hoard speedups + valor badges between cycles, dump them during matching phases only.
  3. 얼라이언스 듀얼 VS — Wednesday's valor-badge spend + Thursday's shard spend are the two biggest single-day contributions a free player can make. Chest 1–3 (Basic, 540k pts) is a realistic 무과금 weekly target.
  4. 고스트 옵스 — 3 assists + 5 plunders per Thursday hits 6 rewards with zero premium currency. Prefer 전용 무기 조각s over UR shards per LDShop — the weapon system is the 무과금 power multiplier that closes the gap with whales.
  5. Trade Truck plunders — 4 enemy trucks per day; six-refresh rule (refresh, plunder 1–2, wait 10–30 sec, refresh again — by the 6th refresh, a UR-quality truck is near-certain) [lw-spy.com/blog/last-war-truck-plunder-guide/ WebSearch 2026-07-15, 티어 B]. Reindeer Sleds are the highest-priority truck type — 전용 무기 조각s are cited as the single most valuable reward in the entire truck system.
  6. 반짝이는 시장 — even without diamond spend, 무과금 earns ~300–360 골드s/6-day cycle from the daily-diamond exchange (only if diamonds are saved for it, not spent on shields). 골드s do NOT expire between cycles. Stack 8–10 cycles = one full Mythic Gear Blueprint set (3,000 coins). This is the single most important long-cycle 무과금 strategy.

16.9 킴벌리 전용 무기 (미사일 Shadow, S1 debut)

  • 해금s Day 4 Week 1
  • Requires 킴벌리 at 5-star
  • Activation cost: 50 shards
  • Max level: 30
  • Battle Pass provides 70 shards (activation + Lvl 2)
  • 08-얼라이언스-systems — 시즌 1 하이브 placement decisions and the shift into the "Land of Fog and Rain" map. The user's own 라워_얼라이언스_Ops_Center → 얼라이언스 하이브 Grid Manager is designed specifically for pre-season 하이브 planning and is used against 시즌 1 layouts.
  • 06-season-2-frozen — 시즌 2 (폴라 스톰) is the direct successor. Different mechanic (frost vs virus), same territorial structure.
  • 02-heroes — 킴벌리's 전용 무기 (미사일 Shadow) debuts in S1 Wk 1 Day 4; 메이슨 UR Upgrade begins Week 2; DVA and 테슬라 전용 무기s rotate through Battle Pass slots. 히어로 picks pivot around who can hold-up-in-Contaminated-Land.
  • 03-chief — Base building continues in parallel; 시즌 1 adds VRI + 단백질 농장s + Military Bases (removed at Celebration end).
  • 08-얼라이언스-systems — 내각 carry forward from base game; 수도 Conquest during Pre-시즌 is the first 얼라이언스-vs-얼라이언스 event of the season. 워존 결투 + 8강 토너먼트 are cross-server extensions.
  • 04-squads-combat — 랠리 leader's 바이러스 저항 (VR) gate is a 시즌-1-specific rule that changes 랠리 leader selection (usually highest-VRI R5 or R4 instead of highest-power).
  • 03-chief — 직업 Hall is a 시즌 1 introduction. 엔지니어 vs 전쟁 리더 choice is once per season.
  • 09-events — 유전자 재조합, Clean-up Operation, 유전자 조각 Collector, Blood Red Legion, 도시 Clash, UR Upgrade, and 건설자 연맹 are all 시즌-1 events. The standalone 둠스데이 event is a permanent bi-weekly ritual and does NOT come and go with the season.
  • 11-monetization — 시즌 Pass (unlocks 5th 단백질 농장, +250 VR, +30% march speed, 7-day duration), Battle Passes for exclusive weapons.
  • 13-meta-competitive — 8-tier 얼라이언스 ranking + 워존 ranking define the meta competitive structure for the season.
