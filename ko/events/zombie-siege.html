라스트 워: 서바이벌's calendar is a stack of overlapping loops: a daily loop (무기 경쟁, six 4-hour phases), a weekly loop (얼라이언스 듀얼 VS Mon–Sat, plus rotating 좀비 시즈, 고스트 옵스, 프론트라인 브레이크스루, Bounty Hunter, Desert Storm), a bi-weekly loop (둠스데이, 좀비 인베이전, General's Trial), and the seasonal loop (8-week 시즌 with a mid-season 수도/왕국 대 왕국-equivalent war — 희귀 토양 전쟁 in S2 (see 06-season-2-frozen), 향신료 전쟁 in S3, 코퍼 War in S4, 골드vein / 골드en Palace in S5, faction war in S6 (see 07-future-seasons)). Between seasons the map opens up for off-season cross-server PvP (미티어라이트 아이언 워, 스카이 배틀프론트). Serious 얼라이언스s stack VS Day themes on top of matching 무기 경쟁 phases to score the same action twice, and reserve their biggest resource dumps for Friday (총동원) and Saturday (Enemy Buster). Preparation — pre-staged construction/research, hoarded speedups, badge stockpiles, and shielded inactive members — decides more of these events than any in-battle skill. Most timers run on server time (00:00 reset); PvP windows are typically 12:00–22:00 server time. 라워 does not currently ship a single "왕국 대 왕국" event by that exact name — the closest analogs are season-scoped 얼라이언스 wars (희토 / 향신료 / 코퍼 / 골드vein) and the off-season 스카이 배틀프론트 and 미티어라이트 아이언 워, which are the game's true inter-server PvP moments.

  1. Daily: 무기 경쟁 (24 h split into six 4-hour phases), Bosses Code, radar tasks, 둠 워커s on active days, hero-shard daily rotations.
  2. Weekly: 얼라이언스 듀얼 (VS, Mon–Sat), 고스트 옵스 (Thursday windows), 좀비 시즈 (once every 1–2 weeks), 데저트 스톰 배틀필드 (Fri), 프론트라인 브레이크스루 (Sun), Bounty Hunter (rotating), the Hot Deal / 반짝이는 시장 cycles.
  3. Bi-weekly: 좀비 인베이전 (glacieradon post-S2), 둠스데이 (Sun), General's Trial (4-day window), Bounty Hunter alternating cycles.
  4. 시즌al: 8-week season with a locked schedule of week-by-week unlocks that culminates in a mid-to-late season "big war" (희토 / 향신료 / 코퍼 / 골드vein 골드en Palace / faction war in S6). Between seasons: 스카이 배틀프론트 and 미티어라이트 아이언 워 fill the gap.

좀비 시즈 — the coop wave defense

좀비 시즈 is the 얼라이언스 PvE co-op event where 20 escalating zombie waves attack every eligible member's base simultaneously. Waves last ~30 seconds each with a short break.

Structure:

  • Waves 1–5: Low; members solo-defend
  • Waves 6–10: 건물; monitor weaker members but do not reinforce yet
  • Waves 11–15: High; reinforcements activate here — stronger members cover pre-identified weak bases
  • Waves 16–20: Maximum; max reinforcement effort to keep the roster alive

Difficulty levels 1–12 (11–12 unlock after 시즌 3):

  • Levels 1–5: introductory, most 얼라이언스s clear all 20 waves comfortably
  • Levels 6–9: mid-tier, mixed formations from wave 11
  • Level 10+: high-tier, pre-planned reinforcement mandatory for waves 15–20
  • Level 12: extreme, requires pacing top squads for the final waves

Level 1 wave power baseline: minimum 782K (wave 1) escalating to 4.5M (wave 20). Later waves send 2–3 zombies simultaneously.

Wave-power scaling model confirmed 2026-07-15. Cross-check via [lastwarhandbook.com/guides/zombie-siege-guide via search excerpt, 2026-07-15, 티어 B] — the formula for total zombie power hitting the wall at higher difficulties is: total zombie power = per-group power × number of groups. Search-excerpt example given: Difficulty Level 4, Wave 15 = 3 groups × ~9.0 M each = ~27 M total zombie power. This means the "782K → 4.5M" figures are the per-group power at Difficulty 1; higher difficulties multiply the group count (multiple simultaneous groups per wave from Level 3+) rather than raising per-group HP linearly. Difficulty selection is R4/R5 gated with HQ-minimum floors; Difficulty 9+ is 워존-age-gated (unlocks gradually as the 워존 matures) [ldshop.gg/blog/last-war-survival/last-war-zombie-siege-guide.html search excerpt, 2026-07-15]. This upgrades a previously 티어-C single-sourced (theriagames) numeric claim to two-티어-B corroboration plus a structural explanation of how the number scales.

레이더 Clue 연맹 코인 are the currency that unlocks 좀비 시즈 access. Earn during cooldown windows between cycles:

  • Defeating Doom Legion / 파멸의 정예 / 둠 워커s: +300 each
  • Killing zombies, assisting allies, geological sampling, gathering, digging: +10 each

Eligibility:

  • 48+ hours in 얼라이언스
  • Offline <72 hours
  • No cross-server transfer state
  • Base level meets selected difficulty

Defensive rules:

  • 방어막s do NOT protect against 좀비 시즈 attacks
  • All three squad slots should be assigned to base defense
  • 증원ment from stronger players is permitted from wave 11
  • Once a base breaches, it receives no more attacks that event (breaches also cost 50 diamonds to extinguish burning HQs — don't spend them)
  • Frenesia gear provides +1% damage vs zombies; siege banners function against zombies

편성: 좀비 시즈 rewards AoE and frontline durability. 킴벌리's Barrage Strike (8 rockets random-target) is cited universally as the single best zombie-siege skill. 윌리엄스 as frontline anchor for team damage reduction. DVA for priority targets, 스카일러 for CC, 머피/피오나 for flex.

Distinct from 좀비 시즈 and Invasion, this is a bi-weekly Sunday event where the server 얼라이언스 defeats a zombie boss that has invaded the "National Assembly / 국회." Real-time 5v5 team battle on a dedicated battle server (troop losses separate from HQ troops). Win by scoring most points in the time limit OR reaching 30,000 points first. Boss appearance order: Baseball Boss → Boxing Boss → Rugby Boss. Reward: 훈장s of Merit ("명예훈장") → dedicated store.

이벤트 무과금 should attend for shards / rewards but not sweat rankings:

  • General's Trial — attempt highest solo tier that you can clear; don't 랠리-fail higher tiers just for participation.
  • 좀비 시즈 — participate for team score; do not spend healing speedups on losses (breached bases take no further hits).
  • 둠스데이 — first 3 kills give guaranteed rewards; top-200 damage ranking is a P2W push not worth 무과금 speedup spend.
  • 프론트라인 브레이크스루 — only 3 participations required for rewards; don't grind the whole 5-level ladder unless it's your daily.
