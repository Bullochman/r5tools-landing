라스트 워: 서바이벌's calendar is a stack of overlapping loops: a daily loop (무기 경쟁, six 4-hour phases), a weekly loop (얼라이언스 듀얼 VS Mon–Sat, plus rotating 좀비 시즈, 고스트 옵스, 프론트라인 브레이크스루, Bounty Hunter, Desert Storm), a bi-weekly loop (둠스데이, 좀비 인베이전, General's Trial), and the seasonal loop (8-week 시즌 with a mid-season 수도/왕국 대 왕국-equivalent war — 희귀 토양 전쟁 in S2 (see 06-season-2-frozen), 향신료 전쟁 in S3, 코퍼 War in S4, 골드vein / 골드en Palace in S5, faction war in S6 (see 07-future-seasons)). Between seasons the map opens up for off-season cross-server PvP (미티어라이트 아이언 워, 스카이 배틀프론트). Serious 얼라이언스s stack VS Day themes on top of matching 무기 경쟁 phases to score the same action twice, and reserve their biggest resource dumps for Friday (총동원) and Saturday (Enemy Buster). Preparation — pre-staged construction/research, hoarded speedups, badge stockpiles, and shielded inactive members — decides more of these events than any in-battle skill. Most timers run on server time (00:00 reset); PvP windows are typically 12:00–22:00 server time. 라워 does not currently ship a single "왕국 대 왕국" event by that exact name — the closest analogs are season-scoped 얼라이언스 wars (희토 / 향신료 / 코퍼 / 골드vein) and the off-season 스카이 배틀프론트 and 미티어라이트 아이언 워, which are the game's true inter-server PvP moments.

  1. Daily: 무기 경쟁 (24 h split into six 4-hour phases), Bosses Code, radar tasks, 둠 워커s on active days, hero-shard daily rotations.
  2. Weekly: 얼라이언스 듀얼 (VS, Mon–Sat), 고스트 옵스 (Thursday windows), 좀비 시즈 (once every 1–2 weeks), 데저트 스톰 배틀필드 (Fri), 프론트라인 브레이크스루 (Sun), Bounty Hunter (rotating), the Hot Deal / 반짝이는 시장 cycles.
  3. Bi-weekly: 좀비 인베이전 (glacieradon post-S2), 둠스데이 (Sun), General's Trial (4-day window), Bounty Hunter alternating cycles.
  4. 시즌al: 8-week season with a locked schedule of week-by-week unlocks that culminates in a mid-to-late season "big war" (희토 / 향신료 / 코퍼 / 골드vein 골드en Palace / faction war in S6). Between seasons: 스카이 배틀프론트 and 미티어라이트 아이언 워 fill the gap.

Serious 얼라이언스s treat these not as independent objects but as overlapping scoring surfaces. Every action (a T10 training completion, a valor badge spend, a 1-min speedup) can simultaneously credit 무기 경쟁, 얼라이언스 듀얼, and daily individual missions — provided you time it right. The R5 coordination job is to schedule those overlaps.

얼라이언스 듀얼 VS — the weekly backbone

얼라이언스 듀얼 VS ("연맹 대전" in KR) is the Monday–Saturday weekly war that pairs your 얼라이언스 with a matched opponent from another server. Matchmaking happens Sunday–Monday morning based on 얼라이언스 power. Each of the six days has a theme that dictates which actions score.

Victory threshold: 7 victory points win the week. The clean path: win Mon–Thu (1+2+2+2 = 7). Losing Saturday when you're already up 7 costs nothing structural, but Saturday's 4 points swing more matches than any other day — most 얼라이언스s that fall behind Mon–Thu try to catch up on Saturday's Enemy Buster.

Individual reward chests:

  • Basic (Chests 1–3): 540,000 pts total
  • Advanced (Chests 4–6): 2,300,000 pts (requires tech unlock via valor-badge tree)
  • Super (Chests 7–9): 7,200,000 pts (requires tech unlock)

용맹 배지s are the master resource. They (a) count as scoring on Wednesday and (b) fuel the 얼라이언스 듀얼 research tree that unlocks Chests 4–9 (compounding future badge income) and the 특수부대 tree (T10 troop unlock). Rule: only spend badges during active VS windows.

Enemy Buster (Saturday) safety rules:

  • Purchase shields (8h / 12h / 24h) covering the entire Saturday window before the server reset. 8-hour shields typically cost ~1,500 diamonds.
  • Remove defensive squads from walls (any troop kills you take on Saturday score for the enemy 얼라이언스).
  • Train troops beforehand; stockpile healing speedups.
  • Assign shield-compliance to a war coordinator R4 role. One unshielded high-power inactive can flip the day.

Double-dip / triple-scoring peaks — when a VS Day matches an 무기 경쟁 phase:

  • Tue 기지 확장 × 무기 경쟁 도시 건물 = building completions score both
  • Wed 과학의 시대 × 무기 경쟁 테크 연맹 기술 = research completions score both
  • Fri 총동원 × 무기 경쟁 Unit Progression = "waterfall training" scoring peak

The R5 coordination job is to force these three-way overlaps by pre-staging.

얼라이언스 듀얼 VS ("연맹 대전" in KR) is the Monday–Saturday weekly war that pairs your 얼라이언스 with a matched opponent from another server. Matchmaking happens Sunday–Monday morning based on 얼라이언스 power. Each of the six days has a theme that dictates which actions score.

용맹 배지s are the master resource. They (a) count as scoring on Wednesday and (b) fuel the 얼라이언스 듀얼 research tree that unlocks Chests 4–9 (compounding future badge income) and the 특수부대 tree (T10 troop unlock). Rule: only spend badges during active VS windows.

향신료 전쟁 (S3 골든 킹덤):

  • Wed + Sat fight rounds (2 rounds/week)
  • Factions: Scarlet Legion vs 골드en Tribe
  • Week 7: Invasion Right Contest (Days 1–6 points from Apex/스톰 아레나, Advanced 스톰 아레나, Desert Storm, 얼라이언스 듀얼, Trade Truck Plunder)
  • Week 7 Day 7: 수도 War — 수도 control = +10 M 향신료, each 무기 = +5 M 향신료

이벤트 무과금 should hit every cycle (tier-1 priority):

  1. Wanted Bosses (Codes 39/64/87) — 5 free attempts/day, no cost, Upgrade 광석 + Synthetic Resin compounds long-term. Never skip a day.
  2. 무기 경쟁 — free daily; hoard speedups + valor badges between cycles, dump them during matching phases only.
  3. 얼라이언스 듀얼 VS — Wednesday's valor-badge spend + Thursday's shard spend are the two biggest single-day contributions a free player can make. Chest 1–3 (Basic, 540k pts) is a realistic 무과금 weekly target.
  4. 고스트 옵스 — 3 assists + 5 plunders per Thursday hits 6 rewards with zero premium currency. Prefer 전용 무기 조각s over UR shards per LDShop — the weapon system is the 무과금 power multiplier that closes the gap with whales.
  5. Trade Truck plunders — 4 enemy trucks per day; six-refresh rule (refresh, plunder 1–2, wait 10–30 sec, refresh again — by the 6th refresh, a UR-quality truck is near-certain) [lw-spy.com/blog/last-war-truck-plunder-guide/ WebSearch 2026-07-15, 티어 B]. Reindeer Sleds are the highest-priority truck type — 전용 무기 조각s are cited as the single most valuable reward in the entire truck system.
  6. 반짝이는 시장 — even without diamond spend, 무과금 earns ~300–360 골드s/6-day cycle from the daily-diamond exchange (only if diamonds are saved for it, not spent on shields). 골드s do NOT expire between cycles. Stack 8–10 cycles = one full Mythic Gear Blueprint set (3,000 coins). This is the single most important long-cycle 무과금 strategy.

R5 implication: the first month is a "solo grind" period where 얼라이언스 events don't yet exist. R5s recruited to a brand-new 워존 should not expect 얼라이언스 듀얼 scoring or 좀비 시즈 coordination until Week 3–4; the tooling suite should treat "워존 age" as a first-class filter (a Week-2 워존 doesn't need a VS-day planner yet).
