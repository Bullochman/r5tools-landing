12. Interaction with alliance systems

Progression tracks aren't fully isolated — several are gated or accelerated by alliance participation:

  • Alliance Duel VS Days — where Valor Badges (used in Alliance Duel + Special Forces + Defense Fortifications research trees) are earned. No alliance = no badges = no T10 timeline.
  • Alliance Tech (Alliance Center building) — a shared research tree where alliance members donate resources to unlock small buffs (5–8% of total power). Individually weak; collectively meaningful over months. See 08-alliance-systems.
  • Alliance Help — reduces construction and research times per help-click (up to 20 per member per project). Every alliance push cycle depends on this.
  • Ammo Bonanza and Boss Code Name 39 — event Mythic Blueprint faucets that only pay out proportional to alliance-level damage contribution.

Multiplied across 20 pieces (5 heroes × 4 slots) for a single fully-Mythic squad: ~19B Coins, ~12.8M Ore, ~153,000 Ceramic, 1,000 Legendary Blueprints, 200 Mythic Blueprints.

  • Diamonds (다이아, "gems") — the day-to-day soft-premium currency. Earned from daily missions, events, alliance help, Arms Race, VIP chest, mail rewards, Duel VS, arena, and every celebration event. Priced against real money only in bulk Diamond packs (100 Diamonds at $0.99 up through 10,000 at $99.99 per most guides; see numbers section). Diamonds pay for: Tavern hero-recruitment rolls, VIP Store items, VIP Time activation, teleporters, Survivor Recruitment Tickets, Glittering Market daily coin exchange (500 Diamonds → 60 Coins), and general in-game store purchases.
  • Gold Bricks (금괴) — the true premium currency, only obtainable via real-money purchase or occasional gift-code / free-brick-event grants (e.g. 4,000 free bricks servers 3-2450, Jun 22 – Dec 19, 2026, for 60+ day-old accounts at Base ≥12). Every paid pass tier is priced in Gold Bricks, not Diamonds. Battle Pass Advanced = 500 GB (~$4.99); Battle Pass Premium = 2,000 GB (~$19.99); Super Monthly Pass = 2,500 GB (~$24.99); Custom Weekly Elite = up to 10,000 GB. Gold Bricks also unlock special bundles, rare items, and event exchanges that Diamonds cannot buy at all.
  • VIP 6 (second builder) — 15,000 cumulative points. Reachable in 30–50 days F2P with alliance contributions and event VIP Points. Every guide unanimously ranks this as the single most valuable point in the VIP curve. The second builder queue is permanent and does not require VIP Time to operate; it doubles construction throughput for the account's entire life.
  • VIP 8 (Agent Shirley + Honorable Campaign auto-battle) — 50,000 points. Shirley provides march-size and attack buffs that compound into every rally, VS Kill Day march, and Season War engagement.
  • VIP 11 (+50% Construction + 15% Research) — 250,000 points. First tier where the active-VIP-Time speed differential is visibly larger than pack-buyer benchmarks. Requires VIP Time to actually run.
  • VIP 15 — 20% unit healing + 10% training speed (VIP Time-gated).
  • VIP 18 — 50,000,000 cumulative points. Grants +12.5% hero HP/ATK/DEF across all squads, exclusive developer base skin, and the cosmetic prestige tier. Real-world cost estimate from Vault: 18–24 months of $1,000+/month sustained spend even with alliance-gift VIP Points and bundle efficiency; direct 1:1 Diamond conversion would theoretically be 50M Diamonds but nobody actually pays that.
