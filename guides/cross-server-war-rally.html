Last War: Survival's calendar is a stack of overlapping loops: a daily loop (Arms Race, six 4-hour phases), a weekly loop (Alliance Duel VS Mon–Sat, plus rotating Zombie Siege, Ghost Ops, Frontline Breakthrough, Bounty Hunter, Desert Storm), a bi-weekly loop (Doomsday, Zombie Invasion, General's Trial), and the seasonal loop (8-week Season with a mid-season Capitol/KvK-equivalent war — Rare Soil War in S2 (see 06-season-2-frozen), Spice Wars in S3, Copper War in S4, Goldvein / Golden Palace in S5, faction war in S6 (see 07-future-seasons)). Between seasons the map opens up for off-season cross-server PvP (Meteorite Iron War, Sky Battlefront). Serious alliances stack VS Day themes on top of matching Arms Race phases to score the same action twice, and reserve their biggest resource dumps for Friday (Total Mobilization) and Saturday (Enemy Buster). Preparation — pre-staged construction/research, hoarded speedups, badge stockpiles, and shielded inactive members — decides more of these events than any in-battle skill. Most timers run on server time (00:00 reset); PvP windows are typically 12:00–22:00 server time. LWS does not currently ship a single "KvK" event by that exact name — the closest analogs are season-scoped alliance wars (Rare Soil / Spice / Copper / Goldvein) and the off-season Sky Battlefront and Meteorite Iron War, which are the game's true inter-server PvP moments.

Arms Race is the always-on daily event that structures every other planning decision. It resets at server midnight and splits the 24 h into six 4-hour phases. Five phase types cycle through those six slots:

Alliance Duel VS ("연맹 대전" in KR) is the Monday–Saturday weekly war that pairs your alliance with a matched opponent from another server. Matchmaking happens Sunday–Monday morning based on alliance power. Each of the six days has a theme that dictates which actions score.

Enemy Buster (Saturday) safety rules:

  • Purchase shields (8h / 12h / 24h) covering the entire Saturday window before the server reset. 8-hour shields typically cost ~1,500 diamonds.
  • Remove defensive squads from walls (any troop kills you take on Saturday score for the enemy alliance).
  • Train troops beforehand; stockpile healing speedups.
  • Assign shield-compliance to a war coordinator R4 role. One unshielded high-power inactive can flip the day.

LWS does not currently ship a discrete event literally called "KvK" (that name belongs to sibling titles Kingshot, Whiteout Survival, State of Survival). What LWS ships as its inter-kingdom/warzone war content is a seasonal alliance war whose exact form changes each season:

  • S1 Doomsday: Capitol Conquest (weeks 2, 4, 6 pre-season windows) + weekly Capital rotation among top 3 alliances (RPX/OTL/UYL cycle, historically 8h Fridays 9am CST though modern schedule may differ per server).
  • S2 Frozen (Polar Storm): Rare Soil War — 4-week faction war (weeks 4–7) between Rebels vs Gendarmerie factions, top 200 alliances split 100/100 across 8 warzones. Fight days Wednesday + Saturday, roles alternate weekly.
  • S3 Golden Kingdom: Spice Wars — Wed/Sat fights between Scarlet Legion and Golden Tribe factions. Week 7 is the Invasion Right Contest (Days 1–6 accumulate points; Day 7 Capitol War with +10 M Spice for holder).
  • S4 Evernight Isle: Copper War (faction war analog).
  • S5 Wild West: Goldvein War + Golden Palace Conquest — Golden Palace attackable Saturdays of weeks 5, 6, 7 at 13:00 server time for 1-hour windows. Only alliances with adjacent L10 Bank Stronghold or L10 City can attack. First to 100% capture wins; both defender and Golden Palace can garrison up to 100 squads. Reward tier: ~42k Honor Points from main chests (3M pts) + 6–10k Honor Points from kill leaderboards.
  • S6 Shadow Rainforest / Lost Rainforest (April 2026 launch): City Destruction system + Faction Tech + Pacts + staggered Hero Awakening (Kimberly Wk1, DVA Wk3, Tesla Wk5 per LDShop / Cpt-Hedge / majority live-season reporting; Lootbar's Wk6 claim is the outlier, cross-referenced 02-heroes + 12-progression + 13-meta-competitive). Faction selection at season start is strategically decisive.

The phase structure commonly labeled "Ranking / Warpath / Preparation / Free Attack" in KvK guides for other games is not a separate LWS event surface. In LWS you get the equivalent phases distributed across:

Capitol / Grand Nexus / Presidential elections: Winning alliance's R5 becomes President and appoints Vice President + 8 Ministry positions (HQ 16+ minimum to apply). Each position gates a global server buff (attack/defense/HP, construction %, research %, resource output, hospital cap, training). Conqueror bonus (during KvK/season-war windows) multiplies these ~25%. Ministries pay out via 30-minute wait between applications, 50-application cap per position, 24-hour expiration.

Wave-power scaling model confirmed 2026-07-15. Cross-check via [lastwarhandbook.com/guides/zombie-siege-guide via search excerpt, 2026-07-15, Tier B] — the formula for total zombie power hitting the wall at higher difficulties is: total zombie power = per-group power × number of groups. Search-excerpt example given: Difficulty Level 4, Wave 15 = 3 groups × ~9.0 M each = ~27 M total zombie power. This means the "782K → 4.5M" figures are the per-group power at Difficulty 1; higher difficulties multiply the group count (multiple simultaneous groups per wave from Level 3+) rather than raising per-group HP linearly. Difficulty selection is R4/R5 gated with HQ-minimum floors; Difficulty 9+ is warzone-age-gated (unlocks gradually as the warzone matures) [ldshop.gg/blog/last-war-survival/last-war-zombie-siege-guide.html search excerpt, 2026-07-15]. This upgrades a previously Tier-C single-sourced (theriagames) numeric claim to two-Tier-B corroboration plus a structural explanation of how the number scales.
