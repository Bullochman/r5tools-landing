Each base has its own current base temperature, computed as ambient_tile_temperature + sum(active_heat_bonuses). Heat sources stack additively. When the resulting number drops below the freeze threshold, the base enters a countdown.

The universal freeze threshold is -20 °C. If a base's current temperature stays below -20 °C for a continuous 5-minute countdown, the base transitions into the Frozen state. [multiple sources: theriagames.com, lastwar-guide.org, medievalfun.com, lastwartutorial.com]

  • Rally — cannot start rallies, cannot join in-flight rallies. If a base freezes mid-rally-sequence during a capture attempt, it stops contributing until it thaws.
  • Teleport — no random, targeted, alliance-formation, or cross-server teleports.
  • Relocation — the base cannot move on the map.
  • Shield — inside missile-created "Contaminated Land" zones, shields cannot be activated (see Warlord Missile below).

To escape Frozen state, the base's total temperature must rise above the freeze threshold and hold for 5 minutes (mirroring the freeze countdown). The primary defrost methods are:

A "safe ally heating limit" of roughly +38 °C is referenced as the cutoff before fire damage risk begins around +40 °C, meaning heating too aggressively can flip from freeze protection into over-heat penalty. [lastwar-guide.org Week 4 guide]

Blizzard interaction with the ping. If the missile lands during an active blizzard, random-teleported bases can arrive on frozen tiles with insufficient personal heat and start the 5-minute freeze countdown immediately at their new location — a compounding penalty that removes them from the war entirely. [lastwar-guide.org Season 2 Week 4, 2026-07-15]

Minimum survival stack without an Alliance Furnace in a Week 4 -70 °C blizzard: L30 furnace overdrive (+30) + L3 Tower of Victory (+5) + Intensive Overload (+6) = +41 °C bonus, still leaves you at ambient — 70 + 41 = -29 °C in the outer ring, which trips the -20 °C freeze if you're anywhere but the very corners. This is why the Alliance Furnace is not optional past Week 3. [heaven-guardian.com, lastwar-guide.org]
