Heroes in Last War: Survival are the single largest lever on squad power. There are three combat types — Tank (탱크), Aircraft (전투기), and Missile Vehicle (미사일) — in a rock-paper-scissors: Tank > Missile, Missile > Aircraft, Aircraft > Tank, at roughly ±20% damage (see 04-squads-combat for the damage-formula math). Heroes exist at four rarities — R, SR, SSR, UR — with SSR→UR promotion available for one designated hero per season (Mason S1, Violet S2, Scarlett S3, Sarah S4, Venom S5, Braz S6). Every hero has four skills (Auto Attack, Tactical/Active, Passive, Expertise), reaches 5★ with 975 shards, and unlocks progression tiers at 1★/2★/3★/5★. Season 6 (Shadow/Lost Rainforest, launched 2026-04-13) introduced Hero Awakening, which replaces the "Exclusive Weapon" system for new UR seasonal releases and adds a 4th-skill overlay to legacy heroes (Kimberly Week 1, DVA Week 3, Tesla Week 5). The current meta is Tank-dominant, built around Kimberly (UR AoE carry) with Williams/Murphy as anchors and Marshall as the only UR support in the game.

  1. Auto Attack (top-left) — fires automatically each time the attack cooldown resolves. Physical or Energy damage class depending on hero. Priority to upgrade only for pure DPS carries; tank Auto Attacks are decorative and should be skipped on the medal queue.
  2. Tactical (Active) skill (top-right) — the hero's signature move. Fires on a cooldown after battle start (usually 10–20s), then repeats. This is where the biggest single-skill power spikes live. First priority on medals for damage dealers.
  3. Passive (bottom-left) — always-on aura or self-buff. Priority for tanks (survivability) and supports (team buffs).
  4. Expertise (bottom-right) — unlocks at 4★. For nearly every UR hero this is "Super Sensing," a flat +20% self HP/ATK/DEF plus +10% skill cooldown speed. Season 6 Awakening replaces the Expertise skill with a hero-specific 4th skill that inherits the +20/+10 bonuses.

Slot positioning matters: front row (slots 1–2) takes physical damage first and needs tanks; back row (slots 3–5) should hold DPS and support. Williams and Murphy are the two mandatory front-row tanks in nearly every meta comp. Kimberly, DVA, Tesla, and Marshall sit in the back row.

  • Level 10 — enhances the hero's Tactical skill (e.g., Kimberly Barrage Strike II: fires 16 rockets instead of 8, each dealing 302.06% ATK Energy damage).
  • Level 20 — the critical milestone: unlocks a squad-wide "+7.5% ATK/DEF/HP to all heroes of this hero's type" aura. This is why exclusive weapon priority is "unlock one hero's weapon and level it to 20 before touching any second weapon" per the Packsify and LDShop guides.
  • Level 30 — the finisher: hero-specific top-end effect. For Kimberly this is "Lethal Fireworks" — every rocket becomes a splash attack against 3 nearby units when Energy Amplification hits 4 stacks.

Hero Awakening (Season 6 onward)

Season 6 (Shadow / Lost Rainforest, launched 2026-04-13) discontinued the Exclusive Weapon system for new heroes. Instead, Hero Awakening was introduced as a persistent progression layer for the flagship UR heroes:

  • Requirements: hero must be 5★ and their Exclusive Weapon must be Level 20+ (for legacy heroes with existing weapons) OR their Awakening slot unlocked (for new S6+ heroes without weapons).
  • Cost: 50 hero-specific Awakening Shards to unlock; additional shards to promote through 3 Red Stars.
  • Effect: The Expertise slot is replaced with a hero-specific 4th skill that inherits the Super Sensing +20 HP/ATK/DEF and +10% CDR but layers on a new active effect. Red Star tiers scale the new skill's numeric ceiling.

Season 6 Awakening schedule (per Cpt Hedge, LDShop, Lootbar):

  • Week 1: Kimberly — Awakening skill "Priceless Resolve," Energy damage overlay on her Barrage Strike engine.
  • Week 3: DVA — Awakening skill "Starred Ace." At Lv.1: DVA goes airborne for 5s, gains Ace Boost, and each Auto Attack triggers a follow-up that prioritizes front-row enemies for Energy Damage equal to 251.09% of Attack to 1 random enemy.
  • Week 5 (Lootbar says Week 6): Tesla — Awakening skill Vortex Resonance. Replaces Super Sensing Expertise (inherits the +20/+10 bonuses). 15-second cooldown, caps at Lv.40. Fires a chain-lightning bolt that bounces between enemies 7 times (9 times at 5 Awakening Stars), dealing Energy Damage equal to 726.47% ATK per bounce at Lv.40 [cpt-hedge.com season-6-tesla-awakening, 2026]. Applies the Inductive Current debuff (2 stacks per bounce) once Tesla reaches 1 Awakening Star. 2★ / 4★ add +20% / +40% damage respectively; 3★ / 5★ each add 1 more bounce. Tesla was A-tier pre-Awakening and moves to SS-tier once Vortex Resonance hits 1+ Red Star.

Red Star costs (per LDShop Kimberly guide): | Red Star tier | Shards | Per-tier notes | |---------------|--------|----------------| | 1st Red Star | 80 total (20 per sub-tier) | Awakening skill capped at level 10 | | 2nd Red Star | 200 total (40 per sub-tier) | ~+1.5M squad power gain | | 3rd Red Star | 350 total (70 per sub-tier) | Full ceiling |
