Last War: Survival monetizes through three stackable revenue rails: a tiered permanent-currency shop (Diamonds and the premium Gold Bricks (금괴) currency, $0.99–$999.90 per pack), an 18-tier lifetime VIP program driven by cumulative VIP Points (1 Diamond = 1 VIP Point, capped at 2,500 per purchase, threshold 15,000 to unlock the pivotal second builder at VIP 6 (see 03-chief for the full VIP 1–18 tier map) and 50,000,000 for VIP 18), and a rotating catalog of passes and battle passes — the Super Monthly Pass ($24.99, 30 days), seasonal Battle Pass (Advanced 500 GB / Premium 2,000 GB), Custom Weekly / Special Training / Drone Training / Hero Growth passes, plus the Growth Fund and milestone-gated Dawn Fund. Hero recruitment is a gacha with published UR rates around 0.14% on the standard pool (Kimberly example: 0.1436%); pity and guaranteed-UR thresholds exist only inside limited-time rate-up events, and the only reliable single-hero pity outside gacha is the Wall of Honor shard-refund on SSR→UR promotion. Expected spend to reach the top of any leaderboard is empirically $1,000+/month for 18–24 months to close in on VIP 18 and max UR heroes; F2P earns roughly 55–210 Diamonds/day and can still reach HQ 30 in 6–8 months by leaning on alliance leech, hoarding, and the Wall of Honor. The 2026 monetization overhaul removed the standalone Awakening Battle Pass and moved Awakening Shards to F2P sources (Hero Awakening Trial + Global Expedition), shifting revenue toward Weekly Passes, Glittering Market coin packs, and top-up events.

  • Diamonds (다이아, "gems") — the day-to-day soft-premium currency. Earned from daily missions, events, alliance help, Arms Race, VIP chest, mail rewards, Duel VS, arena, and every celebration event. Priced against real money only in bulk Diamond packs (100 Diamonds at $0.99 up through 10,000 at $99.99 per most guides; see numbers section). Diamonds pay for: Tavern hero-recruitment rolls, VIP Store items, VIP Time activation, teleporters, Survivor Recruitment Tickets, Glittering Market daily coin exchange (500 Diamonds → 60 Coins), and general in-game store purchases.
  • Gold Bricks (금괴) — the true premium currency, only obtainable via real-money purchase or occasional gift-code / free-brick-event grants (e.g. 4,000 free bricks servers 3-2450, Jun 22 – Dec 19, 2026, for 60+ day-old accounts at Base ≥12). Every paid pass tier is priced in Gold Bricks, not Diamonds. Battle Pass Advanced = 500 GB (~$4.99); Battle Pass Premium = 2,000 GB (~$19.99); Super Monthly Pass = 2,500 GB (~$24.99); Custom Weekly Elite = up to 10,000 GB. Gold Bricks also unlock special bundles, rare items, and event exchanges that Diamonds cannot buy at all.

VIP program — 18 permanent tiers, one temporary state

Two distinct systems sit under the "VIP" label and they must not be conflated:

  1. VIP Level (1–18) — permanent. Determined by cumulative VIP Points ever earned. Once you reach a level, that level and its structural unlocks stay forever, regardless of whether VIP Time is active. Cumulative-points thresholds documented across sources: VIP 1 (0), VIP 2 (500), VIP 3 (1,000), VIP 4 (5,000), VIP 6 (15,000), VIP 7 (30,000), VIP 8 (50,000), VIP 9 (100,000), VIP 11 (250,000), VIP 18 (50,000,000). The gaps between levels widen exponentially past VIP 11.
  2. VIP Time — temporary activation window during which percentage-based bonuses (construction/research/training speed, +% resource output) apply. Activation costs: 1,000 Diamonds / 24 h, 3,000 Diamonds / 7 days (~430/day), 10,000 Diamonds / 30 days (~330/day). Without active VIP Time, the permanent unlocks still work but the % speed bonuses turn off.

VIP Point sources. Free daily: ~200 VIP Points/day from the Daily VIP Chest and completing "Today's Theme" + Duel Progress tasks (some estimates 200–300/day). Purchase: 1 Diamond = 1 VIP Point, cap 2,500 per single purchase. Alliance: when any alliance member buys a premium pack, the Alliance Gift Chest often includes VIP Points for every member. Packs: dedicated VIP Point bundles frequently ship in ~4 × 2,500-point units. Rally rewards and event drops add smaller drips.

Practical thresholds every account cares about:

  • VIP 6 (second builder) — 15,000 cumulative points. Reachable in 30–50 days F2P with alliance contributions and event VIP Points. Every guide unanimously ranks this as the single most valuable point in the VIP curve. The second builder queue is permanent and does not require VIP Time to operate; it doubles construction throughput for the account's entire life.
  • VIP 8 (Agent Shirley + Honorable Campaign auto-battle) — 50,000 points. Shirley provides march-size and attack buffs that compound into every rally, VS Kill Day march, and Season War engagement.
  • VIP 11 (+50% Construction + 15% Research) — 250,000 points. First tier where the active-VIP-Time speed differential is visibly larger than pack-buyer benchmarks. Requires VIP Time to actually run.
  • VIP 15 — 20% unit healing + 10% training speed (VIP Time-gated).
  • VIP 18 — 50,000,000 cumulative points. Grants +12.5% hero HP/ATK/DEF across all squads, exclusive developer base skin, and the cosmetic prestige tier. Real-world cost estimate from Vault: 18–24 months of $1,000+/month sustained spend even with alliance-gift VIP Points and bundle efficiency; direct 1:1 Diamond conversion would theoretically be 50M Diamonds but nobody actually pays that.

  2. Growth Fund (~$4.99–$9.99) — pays out across the account's entire lifetime via HQ-milestone-gated tranches: Diamonds, VIP Points, hero shards, gear pieces released as the base advances. Superior to any generic $9.99 gem pack because rewards compound with progression. Vault ranks S-tier: "always worth it." Recommendation: buy before HQ 28 — after that, most milestone rewards have already unlocked and value collapses.
  3. Dawn Fund — a second milestone-gated fund. Structure mirrors Growth Fund; releases speed-ups, Diamonds, and hero materials as HQ level progresses. Same "buy early or lose value" curve.
  • Free track — modest rewards at every tier (Gems, speedups, basic resources, some hero shards) accessible to every player.
  • Advanced (500 GB / ~$4.99) — unlocks the mid-tier reward path plus battle-pass-point bonuses that accelerate free-track objectives.
  • Premium (2,000 GB / ~$19.99) — top-tier path. During UR-hero seasons the Premium track delivered enough named seasonal hero shards to reach 5★ on the season carry (e.g. Kimberly Weeks 1–3, DVA Weeks 3–5, Tesla Weeks 6–8 during Season 6). Vault rates the Awakening-season Premium Battle Pass S-tier.
  • Custom Weekly Pass — Premium 500 GB (~$4.99), Elite 10,000 GB (~$9.99–$19.99). Premium = pick 2 daily rewards from a menu; Elite (unlocks after Premium bought) = pick 4 daily. Immediate rewards: Premium 500 Diamonds + 500 VIP Points + 1 Alliance Gift + 50 Energy Badges; Elite adds 1,000 Diamonds + 10,000 VIP Points cumulative + 100 more Energy Badges. Ranked "one of the best value purchases in the game."
  • Special Training Pass (Gear focus, 7 days) — Freebie + Advanced (~$5.99) + Premium (~$23.99).
  • Drone Training Pass (Drone materials, 7 days) — Freebie + Advanced (~$5.99) + Premium (~$23.99).
  • Hero Growth Battle Pass (Hero shards / EW shards focus, ~7–14 days, typically Weeks 2 & 6) — Advanced 500 GB, Supreme/Premium 2,000 GB. Delivers Universal Legendary Hero Shards or Exclusive Weapon Shards for the featured hero, plus skill medals and ore. F2P can hit 4,000–6,000 points across daily/target tasks; 9,000 (all reward tiers) requires the premium bonus.
