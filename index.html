SEASON 2 AT A GLANCE · POLAR STORM

The four diagrams every R5 needs on Day 1 of Season 2 — memorize these and you're ahead of most warzones.

Thermal Map — 7 concentric bands

열지도 · 7개 동심 온도대
Base temperature drops from -15°C at the corners to -80°C at the Capitol. Cross the -20°C line for 5 minutes and your base Freezes — no rally, no teleport, no relocation. Furnaces reverse the drop, additively stacking.

Warlord Missile · 25×25 blast + ping-away

군주 미사일 · 25×25 폭발 + 튕겨내기
On impact, every base inside the 25×25 zone (except Warlords) is randomly teleported away — furnace defenders scatter and must re-reinforce under fire. Intercept during the 3-minute prep window by rallying the Warlord's revealed base = 72h missile cooldown.

Megahive vs Tight-hive · walk-back time

메가하이브 vs 밀집형
Tight hive
✓ tight furnace radius reinforcements
✗ one missile catches whole hive
✓ simple to coordinate
✗ shorter walk = easy counter
Best for <60 members
Megahive
✓ long walk-back defends furnace
✓ missile hits fewer bases
✗ gaps invite sneaks
✗ needs disciplined 100+ alliance
Best for 90-100 members
The megahive pattern trades defensive density for longer walk-back time — attackers who lose the furnace fight have to march 3× farther to defend their own home base. Weaknesses: enemies sneak into interior gaps for premium spawn spots.

HOW IT WORKS · ROSTER → SUITE IN 2 MINUTES

The flagship Roster Extractor is the front door. Once your roster is CSV, every other tool in the suite eats the same file — no re-uploading, no re-typing.

STEP 1

Record 90 seconds.

Open your alliance member list in-game. Start iOS/Android screen recording. Slowly scroll from R5 down to R1. Stop. You now have a .mov / .mp4 with every row visible.

STEP 2

Upload & review.

Drop the file into roster.r5tools.io. ffmpeg extracts frames; Claude Sonnet 4.6 vision reads every row; dedup + rank-sort auto. You get an editable table to fix any OCR misreads before export.

STEP 3

Download & feed the suite.

Same CSV plugs into Landing Planner, Heat Simulator, Coal Burn, Freeze-Risk, and every other tool. Each extraction is fingerprinted (SHA-256 of R5 + top-8) so you can track alliance growth across seasons.
