Alliance Duel (VS Days)

The main weekly alliance competition. Also called "Alliance VS" or just "VS." Runs Mon–Sat.

Minimum entry: HQ 10+, alliance has ≥20 members.

Additional gate confirmed 2026-07-15: only the top 32 alliances in the warzone are eligible to participate — even at ≥20 members and HQ 10+, an alliance ranked #33 or lower in server power is locked out until it climbs the leaderboard [treyexgaming.com/last-war-survival-alliance-duel-guide/ WebFetch 2026-07-15, Tier B; corroborated by lastwargame.online/en/game-alliance-duel/ search 2026-07-15]. This is a load-bearing gate for the R5-tools "who should we recruit next" question — top-32 threshold is the practical eligibility bar, not the ≥20-member floor.

Bracket / league structure (2026-07-15 addition): alliances are grouped into leagues of 16 for a 4-week season with weekly promote/demote by duel result [treyexgaming.com]. So the ≥20-member and HQ-10 gates get an alliance in the door; the 4-week league then decides whether it climbs or drops. R5s should treat the first duel of a new league as the highest-leverage matchup — a Week-1 loss locks the alliance out of the top bracket for the rest of the season.

Pairing: cross-server. Your alliance is matched against one from another server for the week. Matchmaking uses alliance power and server age; the exact algorithm is not published. Newer or weaker alliances may pair intra-server at low tiers.

Each day gives points for specific in-game actions (radar missions on Mon, construction speedups on Tue, research speedups on Wed, hero XP on Thu, troop training on Fri, killing enemy troops on Sat). Actions taken during the matching Arms Race phase double-dip — this alignment is required, not optional, for competitive alliances.

Victory Points (VPs):

DayVPs to daily winner
Day 1 (Radar)1
Days 2–52 each
Day 6 (Enemy Buster)4
Total pool13

Because Day 6 alone is worth 4 VPs, winning it plus any 3 of Days 2–5 flips a losing week. Enemy Buster carries a 5× point multiplier for killing troops belonging to the paired duel alliance vs. any other kills — the single largest scoring lever in the format. Any offensive action drops your shield, so Day 6 is a shield/kill trade throughout the 24 hours.

The first 5 minutes after daily reset score nothing — waiting for the countdown before dumping speedups or claiming radar chests is standard practice.
