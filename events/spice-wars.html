Last War: Survival's calendar is a stack of overlapping loops: a daily loop (Arms Race, six 4-hour phases), a weekly loop (Alliance Duel VS Mon–Sat, plus rotating Zombie Siege, Ghost Ops, Frontline Breakthrough, Bounty Hunter, Desert Storm), a bi-weekly loop (Doomsday, Zombie Invasion, General's Trial), and the seasonal loop (8-week Season with a mid-season Capitol/KvK-equivalent war — Rare Soil War in S2 (see 06-season-2-frozen), Spice Wars in S3, Copper War in S4, Goldvein / Golden Palace in S5, faction war in S6 (see 07-future-seasons)). Between seasons the map opens up for off-season cross-server PvP (Meteorite Iron War, Sky Battlefront). Serious alliances stack VS Day themes on top of matching Arms Race phases to score the same action twice, and reserve their biggest resource dumps for Friday (Total Mobilization) and Saturday (Enemy Buster). Preparation — pre-staged construction/research, hoarded speedups, badge stockpiles, and shielded inactive members — decides more of these events than any in-battle skill. Most timers run on server time (00:00 reset); PvP windows are typically 12:00–22:00 server time. LWS does not currently ship a single "KvK" event by that exact name — the closest analogs are season-scoped alliance wars (Rare Soil / Spice / Copper / Goldvein) and the off-season Sky Battlefront and Meteorite Iron War, which are the game's true inter-server PvP moments.

  • S1 Doomsday: Capitol Conquest (weeks 2, 4, 6 pre-season windows) + weekly Capital rotation among top 3 alliances (RPX/OTL/UYL cycle, historically 8h Fridays 9am CST though modern schedule may differ per server).
  • S2 Frozen (Polar Storm): Rare Soil War — 4-week faction war (weeks 4–7) between Rebels vs Gendarmerie factions, top 200 alliances split 100/100 across 8 warzones. Fight days Wednesday + Saturday, roles alternate weekly.
  • S3 Golden Kingdom: Spice Wars — Wed/Sat fights between Scarlet Legion and Golden Tribe factions. Week 7 is the Invasion Right Contest (Days 1–6 accumulate points; Day 7 Capitol War with +10 M Spice for holder).
  • S4 Evernight Isle: Copper War (faction war analog).
  • S5 Wild West: Goldvein War + Golden Palace Conquest — Golden Palace attackable Saturdays of weeks 5, 6, 7 at 13:00 server time for 1-hour windows. Only alliances with adjacent L10 Bank Stronghold or L10 City can attack. First to 100% capture wins; both defender and Golden Palace can garrison up to 100 squads. Reward tier: ~42k Honor Points from main chests (3M pts) + 6–10k Honor Points from kill leaderboards.
  • S6 Shadow Rainforest / Lost Rainforest (April 2026 launch): City Destruction system + Faction Tech + Pacts + staggered Hero Awakening (Kimberly Wk1, DVA Wk3, Tesla Wk5 per LDShop / Cpt-Hedge / majority live-season reporting; Lootbar's Wk6 claim is the outlier, cross-referenced 02-heroes + 12-progression + 13-meta-competitive). Faction selection at season start is strategically decisive.

Season 3 Spice Wars + Season 5 Golden Palace + Season 6 Faction War

Spice Wars (S3 Golden Kingdom):

  • Wed + Sat fight rounds (2 rounds/week)
  • Factions: Scarlet Legion vs Golden Tribe
  • Week 7: Invasion Right Contest (Days 1–6 points from Apex/Storm Arena, Advanced Storm Arena, Desert Storm, Alliance Duel, Trade Truck Plunder)
  • Week 7 Day 7: Capitol War — Capitol control = +10 M Spice, each Cannon = +5 M Spice

Copper War (S4 Evernight Isle): faction war analog; same alternating-attacker cadence.

Goldvein War + Golden Palace Conquest (S5 Wild West):

  • Golden Palace attackable Sat weeks 5, 6, 7 at 13:00 server time, 1-hour windows
  • Only alliances with adjacent L10 Bank Stronghold OR L10 City can attack
  • First to 100% capture = wins
  • Garrison cap: 100 squads (both defender and Golden Palace)
  • Rewards: ~42k Honor Pts from main chests (3M pts) + 6–10k Honor Pts from kill leaderboards

Season 6 Shadow Rainforest / Lost Rainforest (April 2026):

  • City Destruction mechanic (permanently remove weaker city with coordinated rally + no active Pact)
  • Faction Tech + Pacts (7-day non-aggression, same-faction only)
  • Staggered Hero Awakening rollout: Kimberly Wk1, DVA Wk3, Tesla Wk5 (per LDShop / Cpt-Hedge / majority live-season reporting; Lootbar cites Wk6 — outlier)
  • Awakening Shards drop from S6 ruins + weekly events

Spice Wars (S3 Golden Kingdom):

  • Wed + Sat fight rounds (2 rounds/week)

  • 05-season-1-crimson-plague — Capitol Conquest is a S1 pre-season event (Day 6 of Pre-Season Wk 2, up to 4 hours). Weekly Capital rotation historically used a 3-alliance cycle (RPX/OTL/UYL) with 4/2/2-hour splits.
  • 06-season-2-frozen — Rare Soil War is the Season 2 event stack: Rebels vs Gendarmerie faction split, Wed/Sat fight days weeks 4–7, warlord missiles, alliance furnace 20%/30% plunder cap. Season length affects when Glacieradon becomes accessible.
  • 07-future-seasons — Spice Wars (S3), Copper War (S4), Goldvein War + Golden Palace (S5), S6 Shadow Rainforest faction war with City Destruction. Same faction/attacker rotation cadence; different flavor resources.

  • 08-alliance-systems — Alliance Exercise 5% R5/priority-R4 rally bonus; Zombie Siege 48h alliance-membership requirement; Ghost Ops assist limits; Desert Storm's top-32 alliance rank floor; Rare Soil War declaration group matching (same-ranking only).

  • Season 3 blast size expansion (added 2026-07-16): Season 3 Golden Kingdom / Spice Wars / Faction Duel expands the same Warlord Missile mechanic to a 35×35 tile blast. Season 2 remains 25×25. Any downstream Warlord Missile simulator tool that spans multiple seasons must key blast size off the current season. [lastwarhandbook.com Season 3 Spice Wars & Faction Duel Complete Guide search-excerpt 2026-07-16 Tier B; direct fetch 403]

Last War: Survival runs on an 8-week seasonal treadmill: every server rotates through a fresh themed map with new resources, buildings, PvP events, and hero/weapon rollouts. Since Season 2 (Polar Storm) ended, First Fun has shipped four full seasons — S3 Golden Kingdom (Egyptian desert / Spice Wars / Curse Resistance), S4 Evernight Isle (Sakura island / Lighthouse / Blood Night), S5 Wild West (Golden Wasteland / Banks / Trade Train / merged 8-warzone map), and S6 Shadow Rainforest (faction warfare / permanent city destruction / Hero Awakening). Season 7 is confirmed by community creators as launching approximately August 2026 but no official theme/mechanic details have leaked publicly at the time of this research (2026-07-14). Each season introduces a new seasonal resource loop, a new PvP conflict pattern, and a designated hero for UR ascension or Awakening — and the trend from S5→S6 is toward fewer, longer war windows and permanent, high-stakes territorial outcomes rather than continuous grind (documented in 13-meta-competitive).

The 8-week main season subdivides consistently: Weeks 1–3 are build-out (buildings unlocked progressively, seasonal resource stockpiling), Weeks 4–6 are the main PvP conflict (Spice Wars / Copper Wars / City Clash / Faction Duel prelims), and Weeks 7–8 are the season-defining endgame (Capitol War, Ancestral Temple, Faction Duel final).
