Every recurring event

  • Arms Race — The daily backbone — 5 daily-rotating task-router chests worth ~50% of daily reward income.
  • Alliance Duel VS — The weekly backbone — 6-day scored VS-Day event with alliance vs alliance matchmaking above top-32 warzone gate.
  • VS Days — Weekly Alliance Duel VS competition — the phase-stack optimizer's target.
  • KvK — Cross-server war — LWS ships this as Cross-Server War Rally, not the traditional KvK.
  • Zombie Siege — Coop zombie wave defense — L1 base wave, harder tiers per season.
  • Zombie Invasion — Bi-weekly world-boss farm.
  • Zombie Rush — 5v5 team boss battle, bi-weekly Sundays.
  • Zombie Cataclysm — Alternate name/mode of the Sunday 5v5 team boss battle.
  • Doomsday — Bi-weekly Sunday Doom Walker rush — Season 1 flagship event still present in later seasons.
  • Ghost Ops — Thursday hero-shard hunt event.
  • Desert Storm Battlefield — Friday 30-minute PvP — 15 buildings capturable per side.
  • Winter Storm Battlefield — 5v5 arena mode with a new 2026 point-based reward system.
  • Alliance Exercise — 30-minute alliance tank rally — the entry-level rally practice.
  • General's Trial — 4-day bi-weekly single-squad-power PvE gauntlet.
  • Frontline Breakthrough — 5-level Sunday roguelike PvE.
  • Wanted Bosses — Weekly Codes 39/64/87 rotation — hero-type-buffed boss damage race.
  • Bounty Hunter — Bullet-shooting redemption event.
  • Glittering Market — Hot Deal cycle — approximately monthly, 6-day windows.
  • Meteorite Iron War — Off-season cross-server PvP over meteorite iron.
  • Sky Battlefront — Off-season alliance airship war — 7-day event.
  • Rare Soil War — Season 2 flagship faction war — blizzard-and-plunder mechanic.
  • Spice Wars — Season 3 flagship — 100–600/hr spice per city, 30% steal cap, Wed/Sat war days weeks 4–8.
  • Copper Wars — Season 4 flagship — 8-round 15% cap, Wed/Sat weeks 4–7.
  • High Noon — Season 5 Bank event — 15k/day cap, Wed/Sat war days.
  • Altar Conquest — Season 6 Tuesday altar contest — max 3 held per faction, 100% capture required.
  • Alliance Duel Warm-up Match — New 2026-07 pre-league match variant — practice vs matchmaking.
  • Alliance Duel League Match — Scored competitive Alliance Duel with matchmaking above top-32 warzone gate.
  • Top Commander Sprint — One-time server-launch 7-day sprint.
  • Strongest Commander — Server-launch celebration event, top-commander companion.
  • Anniversary Celebration — Yearly celebration cycle with retention rewards and returning-hero banners.
  • New Year Celebration — Recurring seasonal celebration event stack.
  • Storm Arena — Arena ecosystem — Apex Arena / 3v3 Brawl companion mode.
  • Apex Arena — Top-tier arena ladder for individual chiefs.
  • 3v3 Brawl — 3-squad arena mode within the Storm Arena ecosystem.
  • Ministry Assignments — Season 1 President-appointed cabinet slots — Ministry buffs matter across every mode.
  • Capital War — Season 1 flagship — Capitol Conquest chooses the President who assigns Ministries.
  • Capitol Conquest — Alternate name for Capital War — Season 1 finale.
  • Season Battle Pass — Recurring per-season battle pass — hero-shard milestone track.
  • Growth Pass — Recurring general-progression battle pass — daily/weekly resource + shard track.
  • Awakening Battle Pass — Season 6 hero-Awakening battle pass — ~70 shards per awakening week.
  • Exclusive Weapon Battle Pass — Seasons 1–5 — ~50 EW shards per pass, unlock-in-window.
