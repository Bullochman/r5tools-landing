Alliance Exercise — 30-minute alliance tank rally

Periodic (every 2–3 days). 30-minute event. Minimum 20 alliance members. Marshall's tanks spawn on the alliance marshal platform.

  • Preparation: members donate wrenches, earning +500 alliance points each; withdraw gathering troops 5–10 min before launch
  • Exercise: 5 tanks spawn, rally with 3-min rallies ONLY (no 1-min rallies)
  • Reward tiers: progress through 5 damage-based phases
  • R5/R4 rally leader bonuses: R5 + R4 with Warlord / Butler / Muse / Recruiter roles grant +5% damage in their rallies; standard R4 grants +2.5%
  • MVP bonus: 10x rewards when minimum damage threshold met

Standard tactic: weaker squads open rallies, stronger squads join others' rallies for the R5-role bonus.

A Last War: Survival alliance is a 100-player cooperative unit built on a five-rank hierarchy (R5 → R1), a shared research tree paid in coin and diamond donations, a construction/research "help" mechanic that shaves seconds-to-hours off every teammate's timers, and a geographic clustering pattern ("the hive") that is the substrate for every alliance-level event and defense. The most rewarding alliance activity outside PvP is Marshal's Guard (Alliance Exercise), a 30-minute rally-marathon against five NPC tanks positioned inside a 3×3-tile event platform at the center of the hive (the LWS_Alliance_Ops_Center Hive Grid Manager plans this layout); the largest capturable seasonal objective is the 21×21-tile Military Stronghold, and Ring 1 members must sit against its outer mud to deny enemy landing zones. Alliance Duel (VS) is the recurring 6-day cross-server tournament (Mon–Sat, first to 7 victory points wins) that pairs with the Arms Race event and is the primary way alliances compete for server-level status. R5 assigns the four titled R4 slots — Warlord, Recruiter, Muse, Butler — and in Season 6 these titles gate the four alliance skills (Serpent Breath, Snake Barrier, Night Army, Thunder Feathers). The R4 cap starts at 8 and rises to 10 at Alliance Gift Level 10.

The four R4 titles that R5 can assign are Warlord (전쟁군주), Recruiter (모집관), Muse (여신), and Butler (집사). In lore they map to attack/defense, member intake, morale, and daily housekeeping respectively — but in-game they are load-bearing because each is glued to exactly one alliance skill in Season 6 and each provides the same +5% rally-damage buff during Marshal's Guard. Assigning a title costs nothing and takes seconds; the reliable pattern is "assign a title to every R4 before every Alliance Exercise."

Marshal's Guard (Alliance Exercise / 군사 훈련)

The alliance's most rewarding recurring PvE event. Cadence: every 2–3 days. Duration: 30 minutes per instance.

Format. R4 or R5 places the Marshal's Guard platform on the world map. The platform is a 3×3-tile object placed at the anchor coordinate the leader picks (the working anchor for planning tools is {x:486, y:432} on many servers, but it's leader-controlled). Five Marshal's tanks appear on the platform; the alliance rallies them one at a time until either all five die or the 30-minute clock runs out.

Difficulty levels 1–10+. R4/R5 picks the level when placing. Level scales tank HP and reward payouts:

  • Levels 1–3: HQ 10–15 alliances.
  • Levels 4–6: mixed HQ 15–20.
  • Levels 7–10: HQ 20+ core.

Officer guidance: pick a level your alliance can realistically clear with 5–10 minutes remaining. Overreaching wastes marches; underreaching leaves rewards on the table.

Rally rules.

  • Rallies in this event are 3 minutes only — 1-minute quick rallies and solo attacks do not score.
  • One personal rally at a time, unlimited joins on others'.
  • Auto-fill kicks in at 30 seconds remaining, pulling offline members' garrisoned troops.
  • Recommended cadence: stagger new rally starts ~30 seconds apart during the first 5 minutes to build a continuous join pipeline; after each of your marches returns, immediately join the rally with the shortest remaining timer.

Damage buffs.

  • R5 rally: +5% damage to the rally.
  • Titled R4 rally: +5% damage to the rally.
  • Untitled R4 rally: +2.5% damage to the rally.
  • Drill Field donations: up to +25% rally damage buff, alliance-wide.
  • War Fever effect: +1% rally damage.

Since titles cost nothing to assign, no serious alliance ever runs a Marshal's Guard with an untitled R4.

MVP / alliance-wide multiplier. If at least one participant reaches the point threshold, alliance rewards are multiplied by 10× (per Lastwargame.online). Reward stages on the alliance-wide progress bar have 5 tiers based on cumulative union damage.

Positioning. The 3×3 event platform is placed as close to the alliance hive as possible to minimize march time. Rally leaders (R5, titled R4s, top-power R3s) sit in Ring 1 against the platform. See "Hive formations" below.
