Storm Arena / Apex Arena / 3v3 Brawl — the arena ecosystem (added 2026-07-15)

The KB was missing coverage of the arena events. Per [lastwarhandbook.com/guides/arena-system-master-guide search excerpt, 2026-07-15, Tier B]:

Three arena modes:

  1. 3v3 Brawl — standard daily arena; alternates every 2 weeks with Storm Arena
  2. Storm Arena — 2-week rotation with 3v3 Brawl; different rules per cycle
  3. Apex Arena — restricted to Top 200 players by total hero power on your server; weekly settlement rewards; the high-end competitive surface

F2P daily action: open 3v3 Brawl, give the daily 3 thumbs-ups — this earns diamonds per day for ~30 seconds of effort. Cited as the single most-important 30-second F2P habit.

Weekly settlement: rewards distributed based on Arena Coin total + resulting rank at weekly reset. Higher ranks receive proportionally greater diamond/materials payouts.
