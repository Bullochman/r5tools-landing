  • S1 Doomsday: Capitol Conquest (weeks 2, 4, 6 pre-season windows) + weekly Capital rotation among top 3 alliances (RPX/OTL/UYL cycle, historically 8h Fridays 9am CST though modern schedule may differ per server).
  • S2 Frozen (Polar Storm): Rare Soil War — 4-week faction war (weeks 4–7) between Rebels vs Gendarmerie factions, top 200 alliances split 100/100 across 8 warzones. Fight days Wednesday + Saturday, roles alternate weekly.
  • S3 Golden Kingdom: Spice Wars — Wed/Sat fights between Scarlet Legion and Golden Tribe factions. Week 7 is the Invasion Right Contest (Days 1–6 accumulate points; Day 7 Capitol War with +10 M Spice for holder).
  • S4 Evernight Isle: Copper War (faction war analog).
  • S5 Wild West: Goldvein War + Golden Palace Conquest — Golden Palace attackable Saturdays of weeks 5, 6, 7 at 13:00 server time for 1-hour windows. Only alliances with adjacent L10 Bank Stronghold or L10 City can attack. First to 100% capture wins; both defender and Golden Palace can garrison up to 100 squads. Reward tier: ~42k Honor Points from main chests (3M pts) + 6–10k Honor Points from kill leaderboards.
  • S6 Shadow Rainforest / Lost Rainforest (April 2026 launch): City Destruction system + Faction Tech + Pacts + staggered Hero Awakening (Kimberly Wk1, DVA Wk3, Tesla Wk5 per LDShop / Cpt-Hedge / majority live-season reporting; Lootbar's Wk6 claim is the outlier, cross-referenced 02-heroes + 12-progression + 13-meta-competitive). Faction selection at season start is strategically decisive.

Ghost Ops on Thursday drops shards for heroes not in Tavern (Lucius, Morrison, Adam, Williams, Schuyler, McGregor, Stetmann, Fiona).   • 06-season-2-frozen — Rare Soil War is the Season 2 event stack: Rebels vs Gendarmerie faction split, Wed/Sat fight days weeks 4–7, warlord missiles, alliance furnace 20%/30% plunder cap. Season length affects when Glacieradon becomes accessible.
  • 07-future-seasons — Spice Wars (S3), Copper War (S4), Goldvein War + Golden Palace (S5), S6 Shadow Rainforest faction war with City Destruction. Same faction/attacker rotation cadence; different flavor resources.
  • 12-progression — event-earned Skill Medals / Blueprints / Awakening Shards feed the six progression tracks; Arms Race is the primary Skill Medal source (up to 8,000/day).
  • 08-alliance-systems — Alliance Exercise 5% R5/priority-R4 rally bonus; Zombie Siege 48h alliance-membership requirement; Ghost Ops assist limits; Desert Storm's top-32 alliance rank floor; Rare Soil War declaration group matching (same-ranking only).
  • 10-economy — Speedups are the master resource across every scoring event. Save 1-min speedups for their matching Arms Race phases + VS Days. Diamonds for shields (~1,500 for 8h) are the highest-ROI PvP-day spend.
  • 11-monetization — Glittering Market (Mythic Gear Blueprints, 3,000 coins to clear), Custom Weekly Pass, Special Training Pass, Drone Training Pass, Hero Growth Pass — all Hot Deal rotations. New Year Battle Pass + Anniversary bundles are the two largest monetization windows per year.

  1. Pre-Season — 2 weeks. Preview mechanics, Season Boost bonus display, Season Preview (unlock story tiles by hitting daily goals), and a Pre-Season Capitol Conquest on Day 6 of Week 2. During pre-season Capitol Conquest, any alliance can join without needing adjacent land — this is intentionally more open than the mid-season Capitol rules.
  2. Active Season — 8 weekly cycles (~56 days; theriagames reports 48 days — a ~1-week disagreement possibly reflecting a patch or "active content" vs "wind-down" terminology drift; see also 06-season-2-frozen for the same S2 disagreement). All seasonal buildings, resources, virus mechanics and territory-capture rules are live.
  3. Celebration Phase — ~4-5 weeks. Black Market Event (28 days), Bingo Task (Week 1), Champion Duel tournament, and Black Market Cash weekly challenges (Weeks 2-4). Seasonal buildings and their power bonuses are removed at the end of Celebration; permanent rewards (medals, hero shards, exclusive weapon activations) stay.
