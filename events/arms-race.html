Last War: Survival's calendar is a stack of overlapping loops: a daily loop (Arms Race, six 4-hour phases), a weekly loop (Alliance Duel VS Mon–Sat, plus rotating Zombie Siege, Ghost Ops, Frontline Breakthrough, Bounty Hunter, Desert Storm), a bi-weekly loop (Doomsday, Zombie Invasion, General's Trial), and the seasonal loop (8-week Season with a mid-season Capitol/KvK-equivalent war — Rare Soil War in S2 (see 06-season-2-frozen), Spice Wars in S3, Copper War in S4, Goldvein / Golden Palace in S5, faction war in S6 (see 07-future-seasons)). Between seasons the map opens up for off-season cross-server PvP (Meteorite Iron War, Sky Battlefront). Serious alliances stack VS Day themes on top of matching Arms Race phases to score the same action twice, and reserve their biggest resource dumps for Friday (Total Mobilization) and Saturday (Enemy Buster). Preparation — pre-staged construction/research, hoarded speedups, badge stockpiles, and shielded inactive members — decides more of these events than any in-battle skill. Most timers run on server time (00:00 reset); PvP windows are typically 12:00–22:00 server time. LWS does not currently ship a single "KvK" event by that exact name — the closest analogs are season-scoped alliance wars (Rare Soil / Spice / Copper / Goldvein) and the off-season Sky Battlefront and Meteorite Iron War, which are the game's true inter-server PvP moments.

  1. Daily: Arms Race (24 h split into six 4-hour phases), Bosses Code, radar tasks, Doom Walkers on active days, hero-shard daily rotations.
  2. Weekly: Alliance Duel (VS, Mon–Sat), Ghost Ops (Thursday windows), Zombie Siege (once every 1–2 weeks), Desert Storm Battlefield (Fri), Frontline Breakthrough (Sun), Bounty Hunter (rotating), the Hot Deal / Glittering Market cycles.
  3. Bi-weekly: Zombie Invasion (glacieradon post-S2), Doomsday (Sun), General's Trial (4-day window), Bounty Hunter alternating cycles.
  4. Seasonal: 8-week season with a locked schedule of week-by-week unlocks that culminates in a mid-to-late season "big war" (Rare Soil / Spice / Copper / Goldvein Golden Palace / faction war in S6). Between seasons: Sky Battlefront and Meteorite Iron War fill the gap.

Serious alliances treat these not as independent objects but as overlapping scoring surfaces. Every action (a T10 training completion, a valor badge spend, a 1-min speedup) can simultaneously credit Arms Race, Alliance Duel, and daily individual missions — provided you time it right. The R5 coordination job is to schedule those overlaps.

Arms Race — the daily backbone

Arms Race is the always-on daily event that structures every other planning decision. It resets at server midnight and splits the 24 h into six 4-hour phases. Five phase types cycle through those six slots:

  • City Building — building power completions, construction speedups
  • Unit Progression — training/upgrading soldiers (points based on final unit level), training speedups
  • Tech Research — tech power completions, research speedups
  • Drone Boost — drone combat data (10 pts each), stamina consumption
  • Hero Advancement — elite recruits, hero EXP ≥ 2,000, skill medals

The sixth slot repeats one of the above (varies by server day).

Scoring rule that decides everything else: actions score their full Arms Race value only when they complete during the matching phase. The exact same action completed in a mismatched phase scores nothing for Arms Race. That is why "save it for the right phase" appears on every Arms Race guide.

Chest ladder per phase: three chests per phase — 1st (1 badge), 2nd (2 badges), 3rd (3 badges), 6 badges per phase max. Daily badge chest ladder: Blue (2 badges), Purple (8 badges), Gold (18 badges) — reaching Gold requires clearing 3rd-tier chests in three separate phases (3×3 = 9 badges won't clear it; you need 18 total from Blues+Purples+Golds).

Stage Swap: once per day, R5 can swap the point requirements and current progress of the current phase forward into a later phase. Points reset to zero after swap — useful when the currently active phase doesn't match your accumulated activities. Cannot rewind.

Double-dip / triple-scoring peaks — when a VS Day matches an Arms Race phase:

  • Tue Base Expansion × Arms Race City Building = building completions score both
  • Wed Age of Science × Arms Race Tech Research = research completions score both
  • Fri Total Mobilization × Arms Race Unit Progression = "waterfall training" scoring peak
  • Preparation = weeks 1–3 of a season (grow cities, unlock buildings, form pacts)
  • Ranking = points accumulated all season for Rare Soil / Spice / Copper stockpiles + weekly Arms Race + weekly VS
  • Free Attack / Battle = the Wed/Sat fight-day windows during Rare Soil / Spice War weeks and the Golden-Palace/Capitol windows
  • Daily reset: 00:00 server time = UTC-2 = 02:00 UTC = 21:00 US Central Time (previous day) during standard time.
  • After daily reset there is a 5-minute countdown before scoring begins. Actions completed during that 5-minute window score for nothing — a common mistake is firing 1-min speedups at 00:00 sharp and losing the Arms Race credit. Wait for the "event start" banner.
  • PvP fight windows (Rare Soil War Wed/Sat, Sky Battlefront Fri/Sat, Golden Palace Sat) typically run 12:00–22:00 server time — designed to hit prime-time in most Northern-hemisphere timezones. R5s in KR/JP time (UTC+9) see these as 22:00–08:00 local (overnight); this is a documented frustration in the KR community and is one of the reasons the KR-alliance market values pre-planned tooling more than the EN market does.
  • Event calendar tools (lastwarhandbook.com/events, extremesecrecy.github.io/lastwar-research-trees/guides/calendar.html) auto-convert server time to viewer's local — the KB's Alliance Ops Center suite should surface a local-time toggle for the same reason.

Events F2P should hit every cycle (tier-1 priority):

  1. Wanted Bosses (Codes 39/64/87) — 5 free attempts/day, no cost, Upgrade Ore + Synthetic Resin compounds long-term. Never skip a day.
  2. Arms Race — free daily; hoard speedups + valor badges between cycles, dump them during matching phases only.
  3. Alliance Duel VS — Wednesday's valor-badge spend + Thursday's shard spend are the two biggest single-day contributions a free player can make. Chest 1–3 (Basic, 540k pts) is a realistic F2P weekly target.
  4. Ghost Ops — 3 assists + 5 plunders per Thursday hits 6 rewards with zero premium currency. Prefer Exclusive Weapon Shards over UR shards per LDShop — the weapon system is the F2P power multiplier that closes the gap with whales.
  5. Trade Truck plunders — 4 enemy trucks per day; six-refresh rule (refresh, plunder 1–2, wait 10–30 sec, refresh again — by the 6th refresh, a UR-quality truck is near-certain) [lw-spy.com/blog/last-war-truck-plunder-guide/ WebSearch 2026-07-15, Tier B]. Reindeer Sleds are the highest-priority truck type — Exclusive Weapon Shards are cited as the single most valuable reward in the entire truck system.
  6. Glittering Market — even without diamond spend, F2P earns ~300–360 Coins/6-day cycle from the daily-diamond exchange (only if diamonds are saved for it, not spent on shields). Coins do NOT expire between cycles. Stack 8–10 cycles = one full Mythic Gear Blueprint set (3,000 coins). This is the single most important long-cycle F2P strategy.
