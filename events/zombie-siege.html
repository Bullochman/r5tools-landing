Last War: Survival's calendar is a stack of overlapping loops: a daily loop (Arms Race, six 4-hour phases), a weekly loop (Alliance Duel VS Mon–Sat, plus rotating Zombie Siege, Ghost Ops, Frontline Breakthrough, Bounty Hunter, Desert Storm), a bi-weekly loop (Doomsday, Zombie Invasion, General's Trial), and the seasonal loop (8-week Season with a mid-season Capitol/KvK-equivalent war — Rare Soil War in S2 (see 06-season-2-frozen), Spice Wars in S3, Copper War in S4, Goldvein / Golden Palace in S5, faction war in S6 (see 07-future-seasons)). Between seasons the map opens up for off-season cross-server PvP (Meteorite Iron War, Sky Battlefront). Serious alliances stack VS Day themes on top of matching Arms Race phases to score the same action twice, and reserve their biggest resource dumps for Friday (Total Mobilization) and Saturday (Enemy Buster). Preparation — pre-staged construction/research, hoarded speedups, badge stockpiles, and shielded inactive members — decides more of these events than any in-battle skill. Most timers run on server time (00:00 reset); PvP windows are typically 12:00–22:00 server time. LWS does not currently ship a single "KvK" event by that exact name — the closest analogs are season-scoped alliance wars (Rare Soil / Spice / Copper / Goldvein) and the off-season Sky Battlefront and Meteorite Iron War, which are the game's true inter-server PvP moments.

  1. Daily: Arms Race (24 h split into six 4-hour phases), Bosses Code, radar tasks, Doom Walkers on active days, hero-shard daily rotations.
  2. Weekly: Alliance Duel (VS, Mon–Sat), Ghost Ops (Thursday windows), Zombie Siege (once every 1–2 weeks), Desert Storm Battlefield (Fri), Frontline Breakthrough (Sun), Bounty Hunter (rotating), the Hot Deal / Glittering Market cycles.
  3. Bi-weekly: Zombie Invasion (glacieradon post-S2), Doomsday (Sun), General's Trial (4-day window), Bounty Hunter alternating cycles.
  4. Seasonal: 8-week season with a locked schedule of week-by-week unlocks that culminates in a mid-to-late season "big war" (Rare Soil / Spice / Copper / Goldvein Golden Palace / faction war in S6). Between seasons: Sky Battlefront and Meteorite Iron War fill the gap.

Zombie Siege — the coop wave defense

Zombie Siege is the alliance PvE co-op event where 20 escalating zombie waves attack every eligible member's base simultaneously. Waves last ~30 seconds each with a short break.

Structure:

  • Waves 1–5: Low; members solo-defend
  • Waves 6–10: Building; monitor weaker members but do not reinforce yet
  • Waves 11–15: High; reinforcements activate here — stronger members cover pre-identified weak bases
  • Waves 16–20: Maximum; max reinforcement effort to keep the roster alive

Difficulty levels 1–12 (11–12 unlock after Season 3):

  • Levels 1–5: introductory, most alliances clear all 20 waves comfortably
  • Levels 6–9: mid-tier, mixed formations from wave 11
  • Level 10+: high-tier, pre-planned reinforcement mandatory for waves 15–20
  • Level 12: extreme, requires pacing top squads for the final waves

Level 1 wave power baseline: minimum 782K (wave 1) escalating to 4.5M (wave 20). Later waves send 2–3 zombies simultaneously.

Wave-power scaling model confirmed 2026-07-15. Cross-check via [lastwarhandbook.com/guides/zombie-siege-guide via search excerpt, 2026-07-15, Tier B] — the formula for total zombie power hitting the wall at higher difficulties is: total zombie power = per-group power × number of groups. Search-excerpt example given: Difficulty Level 4, Wave 15 = 3 groups × ~9.0 M each = ~27 M total zombie power. This means the "782K → 4.5M" figures are the per-group power at Difficulty 1; higher difficulties multiply the group count (multiple simultaneous groups per wave from Level 3+) rather than raising per-group HP linearly. Difficulty selection is R4/R5 gated with HQ-minimum floors; Difficulty 9+ is warzone-age-gated (unlocks gradually as the warzone matures) [ldshop.gg/blog/last-war-survival/last-war-zombie-siege-guide.html search excerpt, 2026-07-15]. This upgrades a previously Tier-C single-sourced (theriagames) numeric claim to two-Tier-B corroboration plus a structural explanation of how the number scales.

Radar Clue Points are the currency that unlocks Zombie Siege access. Earn during cooldown windows between cycles:

  • Defeating Doom Legion / Doom Elite / Doom Walkers: +300 each
  • Killing zombies, assisting allies, geological sampling, gathering, digging: +10 each

Eligibility:

  • 48+ hours in alliance
  • Offline <72 hours
  • No cross-server transfer state
  • Base level meets selected difficulty

Defensive rules:

  • Shields do NOT protect against Zombie Siege attacks
  • All three squad slots should be assigned to base defense
  • Reinforcement from stronger players is permitted from wave 11
  • Once a base breaches, it receives no more attacks that event (breaches also cost 50 diamonds to extinguish burning HQs — don't spend them)
  • Frenesia gear provides +1% damage vs zombies; siege banners function against zombies

Formation: Zombie Siege rewards AoE and frontline durability. Kimberly's Barrage Strike (8 rockets random-target) is cited universally as the single best zombie-siege skill. Williams as frontline anchor for team damage reduction. DVA for priority targets, Schuyler for CC, Murphy/Fiona for flex.

Distinct from Zombie Siege and Invasion, this is a bi-weekly Sunday event where the server alliance defeats a zombie boss that has invaded the "National Assembly / 국회." Real-time 5v5 team battle on a dedicated battle server (troop losses separate from HQ troops). Win by scoring most points in the time limit OR reaching 30,000 points first. Boss appearance order: Baseball Boss → Boxing Boss → Rugby Boss. Reward: Medals of Merit ("명예훈장") → dedicated store.

Events F2P should attend for shards / rewards but not sweat rankings:

  • General's Trial — attempt highest solo tier that you can clear; don't rally-fail higher tiers just for participation.
  • Zombie Siege — participate for team score; do not spend healing speedups on losses (breached bases take no further hits).
  • Doomsday — first 3 kills give guaranteed rewards; top-200 damage ranking is a P2W push not worth F2P speedup spend.
  • Frontline Breakthrough — only 3 participations required for rewards; don't grind the whole 5-level ladder unless it's your daily.
