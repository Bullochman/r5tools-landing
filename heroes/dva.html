In the meta (2026 sourced excerpts)

Heroes in Last War: Survival are the single largest lever on squad power. There are three combat types — Tank (탱크), Aircraft (전투기), and Missile Vehicle (미사일) — in a rock-paper-scissors: Tank > Missile, Missile > Aircraft, Aircraft > Tank, at roughly ±20% damage (see 04-squads-combat for the damage-formula math). Heroes exist at four rarities — R, SR, SSR, UR — with SSR→UR promotion available for one designated hero per season (Mason S1, Violet S2, Scarlett S3, Sarah S4, Venom S5, Braz S6). Every hero has four skills (Auto Attack, Tactical/Active, Passive, Expertise), reaches 5★ with 975 shards, and unlocks progression tiers at 1★/2★/3★/5★. Season 6 (Shadow/Lost Rainforest, launched 2026-04-13) introduced Hero Awakening, which replaces the "Exclusive Weapon" system for new UR seasonal releases and adds a 4th-skill overlay to legacy heroes (Kimberly Week 1, DVA Week 3, Tesla Week 5). The current meta is Tank-dominant, built around Kimberly (UR AoE carry) with Williams/Murphy as anchors and Marshall as the only UR support in the game.

Slot positioning matters: front row (slots 1–2) takes physical damage first and needs tanks; back row (slots 3–5) should hold DPS and support. Williams and Murphy are the two mandatory front-row tanks in nearly every meta comp. Kimberly, DVA, Tesla, and Marshall sit in the back row.

Season 6 Awakening schedule (per Cpt Hedge, LDShop, Lootbar):

  • Week 1: Kimberly — Awakening skill "Priceless Resolve," Energy damage overlay on her Barrage Strike engine.
  • Week 3: DVA — Awakening skill "Starred Ace." At Lv.1: DVA goes airborne for 5s, gains Ace Boost, and each Auto Attack triggers a follow-up that prioritizes front-row enemies for Energy Damage equal to 251.09% of Attack to 1 random enemy.
  • Week 5 (Lootbar says Week 6): Tesla — Awakening skill Vortex Resonance. Replaces Super Sensing Expertise (inherits the +20/+10 bonuses). 15-second cooldown, caps at Lv.40. Fires a chain-lightning bolt that bounces between enemies 7 times (9 times at 5 Awakening Stars), dealing Energy Damage equal to 726.47% ATK per bounce at Lv.40 [cpt-hedge.com season-6-tesla-awakening, 2026]. Applies the Inductive Current debuff (2 stacks per bounce) once Tesla reaches 1 Awakening Star. 2★ / 4★ add +20% / +40% damage respectively; 3★ / 5★ each add 1 more bounce. Tesla was A-tier pre-Awakening and moves to SS-tier once Vortex Resonance hits 1+ Red Star.

The dominant 2026 competitive rally formation across every major server cluster is a Tank-lead hybrid built around the Kimberly / DVA burst window. Community consensus from four independent 2026 guides (LW Spy, Packsify, Last War Handbook, LDShop) converges on the following five-hero cores:

S-tier / meta rally comp (Tank formation, 4+1 hybrid):

  • Williams (front-center) — Williams's damage-reduction passive only applies to the whole formation when he is in the front-center slot; placed elsewhere it applies only to adjacent heroes. This positioning rule is the #1 pilot error in the meta comp per LW Spy [lw-spy.com/blog/last-war-best-hero-formation-2026, 2026].
  • Kimberly (back row) — main AoE-DPS carry.
  • DVA (back row, opposite side from Kimberly) — maximizes the aircraft-pair stacking interaction; DVA and Kimberly's active-skill windows overlap for burst that "frequently exceeds what any other DPS pair in the game can produce" [lw-spy.com, 2026].
  • Schuyler (SR) — passive accelerates the aircraft squad's skill cooldown by 18%, meaning the burst window comes around ~1.3× per fight instead of once [lw-spy.com, 2026].
  • Murphy or Fiona (flex slot) — Murphy for rally-survivability, Fiona for rally-DPS. Alliance meta dictates which.

S-tier all-purpose (pure Tank mono, Marshall support):

  • Williams / Kimberly / Marshall / DVA / Stetmann — hits the 4-Tank formation bonus while using Marshall to amplify. "The 4 same-type Tanks plus Marshall produces both the formation buff (+20% ATK/HP) and support amplification — this is the single highest-output composition a competitive account can field" [ldshop.gg Kimberly build guide, 2026; packsify.com best-squad-lineup, 2026].

Aircraft mono (pure formation, S6 late-game):

  • Lucius / Carlie / Morrison / Schuyler / DVA — Carlie is "the only Aircraft-type tank and makes the formation viable"; DVA "runs at full Aircraft formation bonus in this lineup, producing her highest effective output" [ldshop.gg aircraft-hero-skills, 2026]. Note: Kimberly is NOT part of this comp — she's a Tank. Common newbie confusion because her Barrage Strike visual looks aerial.

Canonical opener sequences [lastwarhandbook.com, 2026]:

  • Tank squad: Kimberly Barrage Strike → Murphy Ironclad Barrier → Marshall Command Strategy.
  • Aircraft squad: DVA Steel Barrage → Vortex Missile → Carlie Energy Adaptation.

F2P hero investment priority order (Wardawgg, Packsify, LDShop 2026 consensus):

  1. Kimberly to 5★ — "she's your main carry and nothing else matters until she's maxed" [ldshop.gg best-f2p-tank-teams, 2026]. Also the safest investment in the game (useful across every mode).
  2. Marshall alongside Kimberly — global ATK amplifier. Marshall's Command Strategy (2nd skill) boosts entire squad ATK for 8s.
  3. DVA — second hero built by most whale accounts; single-target burst partner to Kimberly.
  4. Murphy — F2P starter UR, frontline anchor. Every account gets him free.
  5. Mason (SSR→UR promotion) — F2P Tank DPS with the highest promotion ROI (shards drop from long-standing farm sources at Normal Stage 4-3).
  6. Everyone else is optional until 1–5 are 5★.
