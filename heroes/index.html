UR heroes

  • Kimberly — Tank. Season 1 Tank UR — AoE Energy-damage carry, the 2026 meta anchor for Tank rally comps.
  • Murphy — Tank. F2P starter UR Tank — frontline anchor, Ironclad Barrier, mandatory for early rally comps.
  • Marshall — Tank. The only UR support in LWS — global ATK amplifier used in 4+1 hybrid comps across every type.
  • Williams — Tank. Season 4 Tank UR — secondary anchor whose damage reduction only applies from front-center slot.
  • Stetmann — Tank. Season 5 Tank UR — sustained EM DPS Tank with strong armor-shred kit.
  • DVA — Aircraft. Season 1 Aircraft UR — single-target burst partner to Kimberly, Awakening Week 3 in Season 6.
  • Carlie — Aircraft. Season 2 Aircraft UR — the only Aircraft-type tank, makes the Aircraft mono formation viable.
  • Schuyler — Aircraft. Season 3 Aircraft UR — 18% aircraft-squad CDR passive with stun control.
  • Lucius — Aircraft. Season 4 Aircraft UR — universal-shield defense/support Aircraft.
  • Morrison — Aircraft. Season 5 Aircraft UR — armor-shred single-target Aircraft DPS.
  • Tesla — Missile. Season 1 Missile UR — back-row Energy DPS; Awakening turns her SS-tier via Vortex Resonance chain lightning.
  • Swift — Missile. Season 2 Missile UR — finisher against low-HP targets.
  • McGregor — Missile. Season 3 Missile UR — Missile taunt/frontline, protects backline via targeting pull.
  • Adam — Missile. Season 4 Missile UR — counter-attack Missile defender.
  • Fiona — Missile. Season 5 Missile UR — the cornerstone of the Missile squad, multi-target cluster attacks.

SSR heroes

  • Mason — Tank. Season 1 SSR→UR promotion Tank — the most-recommended F2P promotion, shards farm at Normal Stage 4-3.
  • Violet — Tank. Season 2 SSR→UR promotion Tank — conditional promotion; only worth it if she's in your active squad.
  • Scarlett — Tank. Season 3 SSR→UR promotion Tank — skip unless you specifically need her Season 3 comp.
  • Sarah — Aircraft. Season 4 SSR→UR promotion Aircraft — situational promotion for S4 comps only.
  • Venom — Missile. Season 5 SSR→UR promotion Missile (weeks 3–7 only) — poison DPS, community consensus skip.
  • Braz — Aircraft. Season 6 SSR→UR promotion Aircraft — situational rocket DPS.
  • Cage — Aircraft. SSR Aircraft — +40 DEF short-cooldown defensive skill.
  • Elsa — Missile. SSR Missile — –18% damage from monsters, useful for zombie modes.
  • Maxwell — Aircraft. SSR Aircraft — front-row burst DPS.
  • Monica — Tank. SSR Tank — energy support kit.
  • Richard — Tank. SSR Tank — dual-rocket attack kit.
  • Farhad — Tank. SSR Tank — mid-tier bulk pick.

SR heroes

  • Loki — Tank. SR Tank — shotgun burst kit, strongest of the SR pool.
  • Gump — Tank. SR Tank — pyro AoE, cheap filler damage dealer.
  • Ambolt — Aircraft. SR Aircraft — first Aircraft hero most accounts touch; missile-AoE kit.
  • Kane — Missile. SR Missile — grenade single-target.
