In the meta (2026 sourced excerpts)

  • R (Rare, common) — starter units. Barely used past the tutorial. Nomenclature varies; some sources call them "Common."
  • SR (Super Rare, blue) — four heroes total, all damage dealers: Ambolt, Gump, Kane, Loki. Loki is the strongest of the group thanks to a shotgun burst kit; Ambolt is usually the first Aircraft hero every account touches. Rarely competitive past HQ 20 but useful for filler slots, event side-teams, and early campaign clears. Levelable with Rare Hero Universal Shards, which are the cheapest and most plentiful shard type.
  • SSR (Epic, purple) — the mid-tier bulk of the roster (13+ heroes). Includes Mason, Violet, Scarlett, Sarah, Venom, Braz, Cage, Elsa, Maxwell, Monica, Richard, Farhad, Kane (SR), and others. Six of these are "SSR→UR promotion" candidates (see below). A 5★ SSR generally out-performs a 3★ UR, so SSRs are the F2P bridge until UR heroes cross 4★.
  • UR (Legendary, orange/red) — the endgame roster. 15+ heroes as of Season 6, one added per season on average. Divided across Tank, Aircraft, and Missile. Murphy is the only free-to-play UR who begins in every account's inventory; every other UR requires either shards from Legendary Recruitment, seasonal battle passes, or SSR→UR promotion.
