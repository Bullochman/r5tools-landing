Refund Policy

Version 1.0 · Last updated: July 17, 2026

Not legal advice. This is our promise to you in plain English. It is not a substitute for legal counsel or your local consumer-protection rights, which always take precedence.
Short version: If you are unhappy, email evanjones@mdr.net with your Stripe receipt. Refunds are unconditional inside the windows below and land back on the same card within 5 business days.

1. The windows

TierPriceRefund windowConditions
Personal $10 14 days from purchase Unconditional. Any reason or no reason.
Alliance Bundle $50 14 days from purchase Unconditional. Any reason or no reason.
Alliance Founding (lifetime pricing) $30 30 days from purchase Unconditional. Longer window because this tier locks a lifetime price.

2. Why unconditional?

We would rather give your money back than have you feel stuck. If the tools do not fit your alliance, if the game changes on you, or if you just changed your mind — that is a valid reason. We do not require a written explanation, and we do not push back with counter-offers.

3. How to request a refund

  1. Email evanjones@mdr.net from the address used at checkout (or include your Stripe receipt if you paid from a different address).
  2. Subject line: "Refund request".
  3. Include: the Stripe receipt number, and (optionally) a one-liner about what did not work — we read every reply and use it to improve.

4. Refund method and turnaround

  • Refunds are returned to the same payment method used at checkout (usually the same card).
  • We process refunds within 5 business days. Stripe typically posts the refund to your card within another 5–10 business days, depending on your bank.
  • We do not refund to a different card, bank account, or crypto wallet. If your original card is closed, contact your bank — refunded funds usually still arrive.

5. What happens to your access

  • Once we process the refund, your access code is deactivated within 24 hours.
  • If you shared an Alliance code with your alliance, all shared uses are also deactivated.
  • Any data you uploaded is retained according to our Privacy Policy unless you ask us to delete it.

6. Outside the refund window

After the window closes, refunds are handled case-by-case. If the service was down for more than 48 continuous hours during your paid term, or if a critical feature broke and we could not fix it, we will pro-rate a refund for the affected period. Email evanjones@mdr.net and we will work it out.

7. Chargebacks

If something is wrong, please email us before filing a chargeback with your bank. Chargebacks cost us a $15 fee no matter the outcome and are much slower than a normal refund. We have never declined a good-faith refund request; a direct email is the fastest path.

8. Your consumer-protection rights

Nothing in this policy limits any statutory refund or consumer-protection right you have under the law of your country or state. If your local law gives you a longer refund window than this policy (for example, the EU 14-day withdrawal right for digital goods, or specific Korean e-commerce cooling-off rules), that longer window applies.

9. Contact

evanjones@mdr.net — usually answered within a day.
