Season 6: Shadow Rainforest

Season 6 (Shadow / Lost Rainforest) — 4v4 faction warfare, Hero Awakening, Altars, permanent city destruction, and the Braz SSR→UR promotion.

Launch: April 13, 2026 (first server rollout; rolling thereafter). Pre-season went live late March 2026. Duration: 2-week pre-season + 8 weeks main + celebration week. Theme: The Shadow Rainforest, home of three ancient clans — the Great River (their lifeline) has been poisoned by a spreading virus.

S6 is the biggest structural overhaul the game has shipped to date. Community sources uniformly rank it above any prior season in mechanical complexity.

Three clans (two playable + one neutral):

  • Deepwood Clan (green stag totem) — forest, "hunting and combat," defensive/resource-focused
  • Wetland Clan (blue crocodile totem) — swamp, "survival and resource control," aggressive/city-capturing
  • Great River Clan (neutral) — controls the central zone including the Ancestral Temple (the Capitol)

Map: Shadow Rainforest Mega Map = 8 warzones + 1 central area, 100% mirror-symmetrical for fair start. Warzones are split 4-vs-4 between factions. Environmental fog reduces visibility (shared among nearby allies, so clustered alliances have an advantage over spread-out ones).

Faction assignment: Pre-season Week 1 auto-groups 8 warzones into 2 groups of 4. Strongest warzone in each group = Faction Leader. Coin toss between leaders determines Deepwood vs Wetland — locked for the season.

Signature mechanic #1: Permanent City Destruction. In prior seasons, captured cities could be reclaimed. In S6, captured enemy-faction cities are automatically destroyed and cannot be rebuilt for the entire season. Impact on faction Influence Points:

  • Attacking faction: +50% of city's IP value
  • Defending faction: –100% of city's IP value (net 150% swing per destruction)
  • Destroyed cities do not count toward the attacker's city cap

Signature mechanic #2: Altars (Central Zone objectives). 5 Altars in the Great River central zone, each granting different Alliance Skills tied to a leadership role:

Altar rules: open every Tuesday. Alliances hold max 3 Altars simultaneously. Capturing unoccupied Altars requires highest progress at timer end; occupied Altars demand exactly 100% completion. Altars cannot be used as stepping stones to seize neighboring territory — they exist as isolated capturable nodes.

Signature mechanic #3: Hero Awakening. New progression tier above UR ascension. Replaces a hero's Expertise Skill (their 4th slot) with a more powerful Awakening Skill, plus major stat boosts (HP/ATK/DEF) and an updated model/effects.

Unlock requirements:

  • Hero at 5 stars
  • Exclusive Weapon at Level 20+
  • 50 Named Awakening Shards

Shard sources: Battle Pass (70 named), paid packs (up to 300), Awakening Trial (10 free/hero), Global Expedition Weeks 2/4/6.

Season 6 Awakening rotation:

  • Week 1: Kimberly
  • Week 3: D.Va
  • Week 6: Tesla — resolved 2026-07-15. Earlier sources cited Week 5, but the confirmed release cadence matches the Exclusive Weapon rhythm of Weeks 1/3/6 per Last War Handbook and LDShop [Last War Handbook Awakening Guide, 2026-07-15; LDShop Hero Awakening, 2026-07-15]. Week 5 sightings appear to have been early speculation rather than the shipped schedule.

MAJOR MONETIZATION SHIFT (mid-Season 6): In an update published around late Q2 2026, First Fun permanently removed all Awakening Bundles and Awakening Battle Passes — direct paid unlocks for Awakened heroes no longer exist. All Awakening Shards must now be obtained through in-game gameplay (Battle Pass rewards, Awakening Trial, Global Expedition) [LDShop Hero Awakening Guide, 2026-07-15]. The broader "pack removal" wave in the same window also stripped the $20/$50/$100 pop-up bundles and Custom Weekly Passes, replaced by a compensation package including 200 Exclusive Weapon Shards + 1,000 Valor Badges to every player's inbox [LDShop Update Guide via search snippet, 2026-07-15]. Stated goal per developer messaging: slow the hyper-competitive whale-driven power spike and shift toward season-pass style monetization.

Additional S6 mechanics:

  • Alliance Pacts — 7-day non-aggression treaties between same-faction alliances. Prevent City Destruction. Share adjacency benefits and open joint communication channels for up to 200 members. Make faction selection load-bearing.
  • Faction Technology — faction-wide research tree unlocked via Stronghold donations, applies buffs to all faction members after individual contribution thresholds met.
  • War Merit economy — earned via kills, captures, reinforcements, donations, rankings. Tiers Bronze → Elite with corresponding daily stipends and stat bonuses. War Merit Shop trades.
  • Fungus Institute (max ~L60) — enhances Virus Resistance (analog to Optoelectronic Lab / Curse Research Lab).
  • Spore Factory (max L30) — produces Spores.
  • Sanctuary timer buff: doubled from 10 → 20 minutes (occupies 1/3 of the 60-minute attack window). Sources call this a "stealth change" that significantly restricts strategic flexibility.
  • Alliance Skill cooldowns: 3–6 days.
  • Card meta shift: the popular "4+1 hybrid squad strategy" was removed from Global Expedition, forcing pure Tank / Missile / Aircraft formations backed by attribute-specific Tactics Cards.
  • Beneath the Ruins (added 2026-07-15) — Season 6's dedicated minigame, launched in TWO phases [Cpt Hedgehog Beneath the Ruins, 2026-07-15; TheGamesEdge Beneath the Ruins Guide, 2026-07-15]:
  • Solo Mode (Week 1 Day 2+): descending-platformer challenge. Guide your character downward through an ever-deepening stage, avoiding hazards. 1 new stage unlocks per day. Missed days stack — play back-to-back to catch up. Platform types: Regular (safe), Crumbly (breaks after ~1 sec), Spike (1 HP damage on landing — avoid at low HP), Travelators (moving belts — go with the flow, fighting them costs time). HP regenerates when standing on safe platforms. Only left/right movement. Reward: Rainforest Mushrooms (larger on later stages), plus season-exclusive gifts.
  • PvP Duel Mode (Week 2+): competitive descent against another commander, same stage simultaneously, first to bottom wins. No HP regeneration. Entry paid in War Merit deposit up to 500 (or 1,500 with Season Skill). Rewards up to 3,000 War Merit per match [TheGamesEdge, 2026-07-15]. Wager spirit similar to S5's High Noon Duel.
  • Global Expedition — new dedicated building & schedule (added 2026-07-15) — moved out of the Alert Tower into a new dedicated Expedition Base building [LDShop S6 Global Expedition Guide, 2026-07-15]. Three GE rounds per season, each 14 days long (roughly Weeks 2, 4, 6 of the main season). Named Awakening Shards drop at rank thresholds — one of the best regular free shard sources in the game.

Ancestral Temple / Faction Duel Finale (Week 7): All-out battle between Deepwood and Wetland for control of the Ancestral Temple and Central Area. Winner takes 30,000,000 Influence Points toward faction ranking (no individual alliance/commander points). All commanders in your warzone are locked to the Ancestral Temple and your designated Strategic Area once battle begins.

Hero: Braz (SSR) receives UR upgrade capability in S6.

Cosmetics: "Enchanted Fungus" HQ base skin (~1,500 Glitter Coins), +5% damage to monsters/zombies.
