Season 5: Wild West

Season 5 (Wild West / Golden Wasteland) — Banks, Trade Trains, Alliance Safe Time, High Noon caps, and the Venom SSR→UR promotion.

Launch: Rolling per-server. Community timeline places Season 5 launch in early 2026 (before S6 launched April 13, 2026). MedievalFun's preview article (Nov 7, 2025) said Season 5 arrives "in 11 days," implying ~November 18, 2025 as first server rollout. Duration: 2-week pre-season + 8 weeks main. Retains celebration week pattern. Theme: Wild West / Golden Wasteland / Land of Liberty. Venom is the featured hero.

The structural break: single-warzone → 8-warzone shared map. S5 introduced "Octagon Clash": 8 warzones from 8 different servers converge onto a single battlefield. Map is 9× larger than previous seasons, plus a central area. This foreshadows and enables the S6 faction model.

New seasonal resources: | Resource | Purpose | Source | |---|---|---| | CrystalGold | Primary ranking currency, deposited in Banks for interest | City passive income, Bank deposits, Doom Elite first-kills, High Noon (max 15,000/day from Week 3), Trade Train whiskey trades (Week 2+) | | Coffee / Coffee Beans | Fuels Coffee Factory and Caffeine Institute upgrades | Coffee Factory production | | Whiskey | Trade Train consignment (converts to CrystalGold) | Trade Train |

Signature mechanic: Bank Strongholds.

  • Alliances capture 2 Bank Strongholds/day
  • Default max holding: 4 banks; increases +1 per city controlled up to 12 max
  • 3 CrystalGold deposits/day, up to 15 simultaneous 5-day deposits (interest-bearing)
  • Levels 1–6 available Week 1; Levels 7–10 open Week 4 with Virus Resistance requirements reaching 39,900
  • Sundays are Truce Day — no Bank Strongholds can be taken

Trade Train: A train continuously travels across the continent, stopping at multiple stations. Players consign Whiskey for CrystalGold profit — or raid competitors' trades for rewards. Introduces cross-alliance economic warfare not previously in the game.

War timing overhaul — the biggest quality-of-life change since S2. Previous seasons ran 24/7 combat. S5 introduced:

  • 3 fixed war time slots per day (1 hour each, spaced 7 hours apart)
  • Alliance Safe Time — alliances disable 1 of the 3 daily war slots for 7–15 consecutive hours of immunity (president-managed)
  • Cities can only be captured on war days: Wednesdays and Saturdays (2 captures per war day = 4/week max)

New seasonal buildings:

  • Caffeine Institute — Virus Resistance + coffee recipes
  • Coffee Factories — Coffee production
  • Protector's Field — houses special combat units (continues from S3)
  • Bars — Tank/Air/Missile hero damage buffs

Faction system: eliminated. Unlike S4 and (later) S6, S5 has no factions. Influence Points competition is free-for-all between all alliances across the 8 warzones.

Heroes / weapons:

  • Venom upgrades to UR
  • Mason, Violet, Scarlett, Sarah can also upgrade to UR with available items (returning heroes get "second-chance" ascension in S5)
  • Exclusive weapons: Fiona (Week 1), Stetmann (Week 3), Morrison (Week 6)
  • 4 heroes gain +10% monster damage: Venom, Fiona, Stetmann, Morrison

Pre-season structure (retained from S4):

  • Week 1: Season Preview (7 daily reveals), Season Boost preview, Faction Awards
  • Week 2: Gold Prospecting mini-game (Mon–Fri) → top 100 players/warzone determine Area Selection priority → Area Selection Tue–Sat (warzones choose spawn locations, higher rank = better area, presidents can swap positions until Saturday lock) → Capitol Conquest Day 6 (all alliances compete for Capitol without adjacency)

Season pass — Tycoon's Mansion HQ skin grants +5% Attack, plus HP/Defense bonuses.
