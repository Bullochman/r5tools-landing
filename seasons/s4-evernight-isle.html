Season 4: Evernight Isle

Season 4 (Evernight Isle / Sakura map) — Blood Night, Lighthouse power, Copper Wars, Oni Legion, and the Sarah SSR→UR promotion.

Launch: Rolling per-server, ~6–7 weeks after S3 ends. Duration: 2-week pre-season + 8-week main + celebration week. Theme: Sakura Isle, a Japanese-inspired island paradise, plunged into endless darkness by the Nightfall Device and the release of the Oni Virus (ONV). Players must restore light.

Factions (introduced in S4 as a formal system):

  • Kage no Sato (Ninja Clan) — dark green aesthetic
  • Koubutai (Samurai Clan) — imperial red aesthetic

Warzones are grouped and choose sides in the Faction Award Event in Week 3; Copper Wars (Wed/Sat) occur across Weeks 4–7; Faction Capitol + War's Eve in Week 7.

Map: 13×13 grid (same as S3), 75×75 pixel squares.

  • 24 Level 1–6 cities (down from 40 in S3 — meaningfully denser competition)
  • 6 Digging Strongholds (Stone production 100–200/hour)
  • Trade Posts (Levels 1–4, unlock Week 3 Day 2)
  • 10 Sushi Restaurants (unlock progressively Weeks 1–4)

New seasonal resources: | Resource | Purpose | Source | |---|---|---| | Copper | Primary war/ranking currency | City production 100–600/hour by level, Alliance Center | | Stone | Building upgrades | Doom Elites, Digging Strongholds, visitors, Stone Warehouse (40,320/h) | | Quartz | Building upgrades | Zombie kills, quests, Quartz Workshop (720/hour/workshop, 5 total workshops) | | Magatama | Seasonal shop currency | Blood Night Descend, Oni General kills, Magatama Warehouse (6/hour) |

Signature building: The Lighthouse. Central power system storing electricity and pushing back darkness. Requires 4 zombie-powered generators (Optoelectronic Lab levels 1, 3, 10, 15), each operated by an Electrician. Features:

  • 4 brightness levels (L1–L4)
  • Power management system
  • Auto-adjustment toggle for conservation

Optoelectronic Lab (max L35) — increases Virus Resistance and unlocks generator repairs at specific thresholds. Analogous to S3's Curse Research Lab.

Blood Night Descend — the S4 flagship recurring PvE-into-PvP event. Occurs 3 times daily at 02:30, 10:30, and 18:30 server time, each lasting 30 minutes. Power consumption jumps to +18,000/minute during Blood Night. Four stages:

  • Stage 1: Oni Generals spawn; defeating them unlocks city buffs
  • Stage 2: Wandering Oniwagons appear (max 5 consecutive battle rounds)
  • Stage 3: Escalated difficulty
  • Stage 4: Holy Mountain event

Copper Wars — the main PvP event. Alliances declare on enemy Alliance Centers. Key rules change from S3: only 3 secondary buildings per Alliance Center; declaration range narrows weekly; minimum 11-tile spacing between Alliance Centers.

Maneki-neko discoveries: Similar to S3 desert artifacts; discovered using flares/base lights (Large Maneki-neko) or Electricians on nighttime expeditions (Mini Maneki-neko — 1 per day max). 3 daily claims (max 15).

Trade Posts contests run Tuesdays and Fridays at 12:00 server time; during Blood Night, governors can call additional caravans with Magatama-exclusive items.

City capture: 2 declarations/day, max 6 captured cities + 6 Digging Strongholds. Buffs unlock via Oni General defeats.

Heroes / weapons: Sarah upgrades to UR (Week 3 Day 1). Exclusive weapons: Lucius (Week 1), Adam (Week 3), Williams (Week 6).

Professions: Level cap remains 100. Diplomat unavailable. Tactics Cards debut here as a new equipable layer.

T11 troops unlock at season end during the celebration/off-season week.

Blood Night Descend — 4 stages mapped to weeks [lastwartutorial (game-modes), 2026-07-15]:

  • Stage 1 "Night Parade of One Hundred Oni" (W1): zombies + Doom Elites roam; first-kill rewards on world zombies reset daily and drop Tactics Cards; Oni Generals inside your cities restore city buffs + drop oil when killed.
  • Stage 2 "Wandering Oniwagon Invasion" (W2): Oniwagons appear during Blood Night; attack yields Parts Chests; destroying one spawns a Kappa dropping epic Tactics Cards.
  • Stage 3 "Doomsday Crisis / Oni Legions" (W3–W7): Doom Walkers spawn; seasonal profession skill Oni Summon attracts legions to your base; surviving grants rewards but applies "Base corroded. Commander Durability Damage +500" debuff.
  • Stage 4 "Towards the Light" (W8): charge the Holy Mountain by killing Blood Night Zombies; full charge dispels darkness and permanently ends Blood Night for the warzone.

Related Blood Night side-modes [lastwartutorial (game-modes), 2026-07-15]:

  • Blood Night Hunter (W2 onward): PvP-during-Blood-Night mode. Profession skill transforms you into a hunter for buffs; attack rival hunters for points; 3 breach opportunities before elimination; teleport items and shields disabled while hunting.
  • Divine Tree (W2+): send Electricians to charge; once active, draw a Fortune Slip Tue–Sat from 5 element types; completing a full draw grants +250 Virus Resistance during Blood Night.
  • Ryōtei Restaurant (W2+): daily Mahjong-solitaire minigame; perfect clears (no items used) yield bonus rewards; visitor gifts refill hint items.
  • Silverclaw Fighter Boost (W1): "like" bases wearing the Silverclaw skin (click the moon symbol) for Night Gift speed-ups.

Copper Wars — full mechanics [lastwartutorial (strategy guide), 2026-07-15]:

  • 8 total rounds across W4–W7 (2/week, Wed + Sat).
  • Kage no Sato starts on attack, Koubutai starts on defense. Sides alternate round-to-round — each faction ends with 4 attacking + 4 defending battles.
  • Declaration window: 24 hours in the Declaration stage. R4+ in the attacking faction can declare on 1 target alliance in the same Copper ranking group. Up to 3 attacking alliances may declare on the same target; target changeable until stage ends.
  • Copper ranking groups: alliances sorted by copper holdings into 4 tiers (1–10, 11–20, 21–30, 31–50). The Group 1 range narrows across the season: 10 alliances (W4) → 6 (W5) → 3 (W6–W7). Concentrates matchmaking among top alliances late-season.
  • Copper distribution formula: attackers plunder a percentage per building destroyed. Secondary buildings (Stone / Oil / Magatama Warehouse) = 3% each; Alliance Center = additional 6%. Max plunder per round: 15% of defender's Copper. Multiple attackers share the plunder proportionally by damage inflicted.
  • Muse role redesign: the Muse alliance-skill was removed and replaced by the Tesla Coil Butler Skill — defensive ability, deployable only by the Butler during preparation and war phases.
  • NOTE: Earlier KB draft cited "30% steal" (S3 Spice Wars carryover). Season 4 Copper Wars cap is 15%, not 30% — a meaningful reduction from S3.

Sarah UR promotion — costs and refunds [lastwartutorial (Week 3), 2026-07-15]:

  • Requires Legendary Hero Badge item + Sarah at 5★.
  • After UR upgrade, hero resets to 3★ with skills at Level 1.
  • Cost to return to 5★ post-UR: 1,600 total shards (doubled from the standard SSR path).
  • Refund policy: shards spent on prior star-ups not refunded; Wall of Honor shards fully refunded (converted to hero-specific shards); skill medals fully refunded.

Adam exclusive weapon activation [lastwartutorial (Week 3), 2026-07-15]: rolls out W3 D4, 7-day battle pass, activation cost 50 Adam's Exclusive Weapon named shards, prerequisite Adam at 5★.

Trade Post contest specifics [lastwartutorial, 2026-07-15]: contests run Tue + Fri at 12:00 server time for 1-hour windows. No connected alliance territory required to join — unique among S4 capturable objectives.

City level unlock timing (W3) [lastwartutorial (Week 3), 2026-07-15]: L5 cities unlock W3 D3 (mission goal delayed 12h); L6 cities unlock W3 D6 (mission goal delayed 12h).

Hunting Oni event [lastwartutorial (Week 3), 2026-07-15]: available from Blood Night Descend Stage 3 onward; warzone-wide goal to eliminate 100 Oni collectively for shared rewards.

Alliance Center placement — exact rule [lastwartutorial (strategy guide), 2026-07-15]: minimum 11-tile spacing measured center-to-center between Alliance Centers. Relocation spots are attackable during the pre-100%-completion window — a known Copper-War exploit for aggressive positioning.

Celebration Week — full lineup [lastwartutorial (celebration), 2026-07-15]:

  • Transfer Surge: server-transfer feature unlocks W8 D1, runs 14 days into off-season.
  • Canyon Storm Battlefield: new weekly battlefield event (partial Desert Storm resemblance + new features). Registration in off-season W1; first match off-season W2.
  • Bingo Task: 7-day 15-tile bingo grid, off-season W1. Grand prize: 1,500 Black Market Cash + Overlord items.
  • Black Market: 28-day currency store (Black Market Cash). Daily refresh with free/discounted/regular items. Runs across all celebration weeks with Challenges I / II / III.
  • Champion Duel: starts Tuesday of off-season W1 in the Arena building.
  • Full celebration span: ~4 weeks after Season 4 W8.

T11 troop unlock — exact prerequisites [cpt-hedge via search snippet, 2026-07-15]: T11 unlocks in S4 off-season. Requires (a) HQ Level 27+, (b) at least one Barrack at Level 35, (c) built Armament Institute, (d) all 4 Armament Researches at 100%.
