## Post-hygiene notes (all templates)

- **Never post the same template in 3+ servers within 60 min** — Discord's anti-spam + user overlap flag it fast.
- **Rotate the opener** if reposting a template across multiple servers. Change the first sentence, keep the tool list and pricing identical.
- **Reply within 60 min of posting** — an unanswered "how does the coal calc handle L25?" reply kills engagement dead.
- **Attach screenshots inline, not as links** — Discord auto-expands inline images and the CTR jumps ~3-4x vs bare URL.
- **KR posts read very differently to KR eyes** — Evan's Korean is native-speaker-reviewed here, but if a KR R5 volunteers to polish before posting to a large KR server, take them up on it and credit them.
- **Never argue with a mod who removes the post.** Thank them, ask which channel is appropriate, offer to repost there. Long-run reputation matters more than one post's reach.
