## Personalization checklist (before sending any of the above)

1. Their **actual R5 in-game name** in the greeting — not "Chief" or "R5"
2. One **specific detail** proving you looked at their alliance: their warzone rank, a recent CSW opponent, their alliance tag
3. Their **language** — if their alliance name looks Korean/Japanese/Portuguese, send Template 4 or a translated variant
4. Your **actual R5 name and warzone** in the sign-off (not "the r5tools team")
5. **Time the DM** to their timezone — KR alliances read messages 20:00-23:00 KST; NA alliances 19:00-23:00 ET

## Do NOT

- Send the same template to more than one R5 in the same warzone without personalizing (they compare notes)
- Follow up more than once if no response — R5s are busy, silence = no
- Mention competitors by name
- Pitch the paid tier first — always lead with `RONY-FREE` and let the tool sell itself
- Use marketing words like "revolutionary," "game-changing," "AI-powered" — R5s see through it instantly
