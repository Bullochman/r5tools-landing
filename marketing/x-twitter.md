LWS community X usage is thin — the community lives on Discord, YouTube, and TikTok more than X. Use hashtags sparingly (1-2 per tweet max) and only when they actually match real search behavior. Never stack 5+ hashtags at the bottom of a tweet; that reads bot.

**Actual LWS hashtags in use (verified via X search 2026-07):**
- `#LastWarSurvival` — the primary, ~80% of LWS-related posts use this
- `#LWS` — secondary, mostly by KR + JP players
- `#LastWar` — ambiguous (also used by other games), avoid unless combined with #LWS
- `#Frostbite` / `#PolarStorm` — Season 2 specific, spikes during blizzard weeks

## 5. Automation recommendation

**Recommendation: Typefully.**

Typefully wins for this specific use case because the workflow is (1) draft a thread, (2) schedule tweets 30-45 sec apart, (3) queue a 30-day calendar with per-tweet media attached, (4) let it fire while Evan is running an alliance in-game. Typefully handles thread-native scheduling (unlike Buffer which treats a thread as 12 separate scheduled tweets you have to manually chain), has clean media handling for the 4-tile mosaic screenshots on the launch thread, exposes an analytics view that reads like actual data instead of Buffer's marketing fluff, and its "queue" mode auto-slots the 30-day calendar into the 8:15 PM ET default without a per-tweet timestamp click. **Pros:** thread-first UX, media queue, cheap ($15/mo solo tier). **Cons:** no direct Stripe-link tracking (need to layer UTMs manually), no reply-scheduling (so reply-to-influencer templates stay in a Notes doc, not the tool). **Reject alternatives:** Buffer treats threads as second-class citizens and the media queue is awful for mosaic posts. X native scheduling has no thread support, no draft library, and its calendar view is unusable past 7 days. A custom script is over-engineering for 30 tweets — build it later if the account scales to daily posting from multiple angles (KR-only account, EN-only account, war-recap-only account) where the templating cost pays back. Start on Typefully this week; revisit at Day 30 if the account has crossed 5k followers and content velocity has doubled.

## Posting hygiene

- **Never quote-tweet a competitor's tool.** Free advertising for them, looks petty for you.
- **Reply to every reply on the launch thread** within 4 hours. Thread engagement window closes hard at hour 6.
- **Screenshot every tool tweet** in dark theme, dark background — matches r5tools.io and reads cleaner in the timeline scroll.

- **Do not schedule tweets during a live blizzard event** in your warzone — reply in real time instead. That's when the audience is most engaged and looking for the tool.
- **Rotate the pinned tweet** every 2 weeks between the launch thread and whichever data-drop performed best that fortnight.
- **When the Founding tier hits 10/10, immediately** post: "Founding tier closed at 10. Alliance tier is now $50. Personal $10 and free tier unchanged. Thanks to the 10 R5s who took the flyer." — this is a legitimacy tweet, not a marketing one.
