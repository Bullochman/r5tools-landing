# PH Hunter Outreach — 5 Targets

Getting hunted by a well-known PH hunter is worth ~200-400 organic upvotes on launch day, plus a spot on the "Featured by [Hunter]" side rail. That said: the hunter has to actually *care* about the product, or the endorsement rings hollow and comment engagement dies.

For r5tools.io, the hunter angles are:
1. **Games / gaming tooling** (small overlap on PH; the game-adjacent hunters are the sweet spot)
2. **Solo makers / indie devs** (r5tools is a one-person build; solo-maker champions on PH will hunt this on principle)
3. **AI application layer** (Roster Extractor is a legitimate Claude-vision use case; AI-tooling hunters like real applied cases)
4. **Korean/APAC market** (the KR-first design is distinctive; hunters focused on non-US markets are rare and receptive)

## Rules of engagement

- **Reach out T-14 to T-10 days out.** Sooner is spam, later doesn't give them time to check the product.
- **One channel per hunter.** Don't email + Twitter DM + PH message — pick the one they use most.
- **Never ask for the upvote directly.** Ask them to *hunt* the product (which is the PH-native ask). Upvotes follow the hunt.
- **Give them the free lifetime code up front.** Every DM includes a personalized code so they can log in and actually try it before committing.
- **If they don't reply in 7 days, move on.** No follow-ups. PH hunters get hit by ~50 launches/week; silence = no.

## Fallback: self-hunt

If none of the above respond by T-3 days, self-hunt. Rules:

- Post from your own PH account (create one if you don't have one — do it at least 60 days before launch so it doesn't look freshly-made).
- Have your PH account already have some activity (upvote 20+ products in the 2 weeks before launch, comment on 3-5). PH's algorithm suppresses fresh-account hunts.
- Self-hunts perform ~30-50% worse than well-known-hunter hunts, but ~30-50% is way better than zero. Ship it.

## Post-launch: thank the hunter publicly

Whichever hunter takes it, reply to their PH post the same day thanking them and posting the launch URL in Twitter tagging them. Also send a personal DM thank-you after launch with day-1 metrics (upvotes, signups, code redemptions attributable to their hunt via the tracked code). Hunters keep hunting people who show them the impact — this is the seed for a second launch (e.g., r5tools 2.0 in 6 months) getting the same hunter again.
