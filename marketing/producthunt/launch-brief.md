### Long description (up to 2000 chars — the full pitch)

Last War: Survival is a mobile strategy game with ~100M downloads. In it, 100-player alliances fight 8-week seasons where a single bad landing plan on Day 1 can cost you the entire season. Most alliance leaders (called R5s) plan this in a Google Sheet, a Discord pin, or a group chat. It's chaos.

I'm an R5 myself — RONY / TINO in Warzone 2007, a Korean-dominant server. Six months of trying to coordinate 100 players across a language barrier convinced me the sheet had to die. So I built the suite I wished existed:

**Roster Extractor** — R5 records a 90-second screen scroll of the in-game member list. Claude vision reads all 100 rows in 2 minutes. CSV out. No manual typing, no OCR review needed.

**Season 2 Landing Planner** — R5 picks a Day-1 anchor tile; the planner assigns every member a landing coordinate by rank tier around the Alliance Furnace. Titled R4s in Ring 1, untitled R4s Ring 2, R3s Ring 3.

**Hive Grid Manager** — Marshall's Guard 3×3 and Military Stronghold 21×21 formation planner. Drag-and-drop, corner-anchored ring algorithm, PNG export straight to Discord.

**Freeze Risk Dashboard** — Live per-member board: "Frozen in 3 min", "Furnace deploy needed", "Coal below 12h". The daily driver during Weeks 3-4 of the Polar Storm season.

**Coal Burn Calculator** — Per-member coal budget for the Week-4 -70 °C blizzard, factoring Alliance Furnace overdrive and Home Heating Furnace overdrive stacking. Green/yellow/red grid.

**City Capture Scheduler** — Which of the 6 cities + 4 dig-sites to prioritize, factoring the +5 → +60 °C bonus curve and the 36-hour post-capture protection window.

**Heat Simulator, Season Timeline, VS Days Tracker, Profile Studio, KB Chat** — round out the suite.

- **Tuesday** is PH's proven strongest day. Monday is fine but often gets crowded by teams that queued weekend submissions. Wednesday-Thursday is second-best. Never launch Friday-Sunday (traffic drops 40-60%, and top-hunter attention shifts to weekend threads).

### DO NOT LAUNCH on these weeks (they'll bury you):

- **The week Apple announces new iPhone hardware** (typically 2nd Tuesday of September; every year the tech-Twitter attention + PH traffic gets sucked into Apple-adjacent products for 3-4 days). If iPhone 18 event lands on Tue Sep 8, avoid Sep 8-11 entirely.

- **The two weeks around US Thanksgiving** (mid Nov to early Dec) — engagement craters. Nobody's upvoting on a family holiday.
- **The last week of December** — dead zone, launches get zero traction.
- **Any day the PH homepage is showing a mega-launch** you can preview via `producthunt.com/upcoming` — if there are 3+ big-hunter-backed launches queued for the same Tuesday, shift a week.

### Pre-launch check (run T-48h)
1. Load `producthunt.com/upcoming` and search "game" — see who else is launching that day. If a AAA studio's promo game is on the list, shift the week.
2. Check `producthunt.com/leaderboard` for the last 4 Tuesdays. If the #1 spot averaged 500+ upvotes, you need to hit 300+ minimum to place top-5. If the average is 200-300, you're competitive at 150+.

- **First-person always.** No "we", no "our team", no "the r5tools team". Evan is the solo maker; PH readers reward that.
- **R5-to-R5 tone.** Read `../reddit-posts.md` and `../youtube-outreach.md` for the exact register. Colleague-to-colleague, not marketer-to-buyer.
- **Real numbers, no marketing adjectives.** "94/94 accuracy" not "industry-leading". "$10" not "affordable". "100 players" not "large alliances".
- **The Korean-warzone angle is the differentiator.** Every other LWS tool is EN-first with a KR translation slapped on. r5tools is KR-primary. Lead with this in the maker comment and the description.
- **`RONY-FREE` is the trojan horse.** Every mention of the free tier should include the code — it's memorable, it converts DMs to activations, and it signals confidence (charging for one warzone, free for another).
- **Never use:** "revolutionary", "game-changing", "AI-powered" (redundant), "one-click", "seamless", "cutting-edge", "the future of X". Any of these on PH will get called out in the comments and tank the launch.
