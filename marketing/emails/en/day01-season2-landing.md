The Season 1 map had one obvious meta-formation: hug the north wall, rally into the Fortress at 04:00 UTC. If your alliance is landing S2 the way you landed S1, you're leaving 20–30% of your possible mid-season power on the table.

I built the [Landing Planner](https://r5tools.io/guides/season-2-landing) specifically to solve this. Here's what it does that a Google Sheet can't:

**2. Distance-to-Depot heat map.** The two new S2 depots are the whole reason S2 mid-game feels different. The planner shades every possible landing hex by average travel time to the nearest depot for a T5 march. Land in the yellow band or you're going to be late to every resource push for the whole season.

**3. Auto-suggested R5/R4 grid.** Pick your alliance size (25/50/100), your target strategy (defensive shell / rush-mid / split-flank), and the tool spits out a grid assignment with each member's coord. Officers copy-paste to Discord. Done.
