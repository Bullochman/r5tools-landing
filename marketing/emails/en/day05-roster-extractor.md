If you or your officers are still typing member names into a Google Sheet, please read this once and then never do that again.

The [Roster Extractor](https://roster.r5tools.io) turns a phone-screen recording of your in-game member list into a clean CSV in about 90 seconds. Here's the full flow.

---

**Step 1: Record your member list on your phone.**

Open Last War → Alliance → Members. Start your phone's screen recorder (built-in on iOS + Android). Scroll slowly through the entire list, top to bottom. **Slower than feels natural** — you want each row visible for at least half a second. Stop the recording when you hit the bottom.

Time: about 60 seconds for a 100-member alliance.

**Step 2: Upload the video.**

Go to [https://roster.r5tools.io](https://roster.r5tools.io). Drop the video onto the page (or tap "Choose file" on mobile). No account. No login. If you unlocked a code at r5tools.io, this device is already authenticated.

**Step 3: Wait ~90 seconds.**

The extractor runs OCR frame-by-frame, deduplicates rows, and cross-checks name spellings. You'll see progress in real time. When it's done, you get a clean CSV: Name, Power, Level, Last Active.

**Step 4: Download + do whatever.**

Drop it into your existing Sheet. Feed it to the Freeze Risk Dashboard. Track power-over-time (Alliance tier). Send it to your R4 as a Discord attachment. It's just a CSV.

---

**Why this matters:**

The manual version of this workflow — typing names into a Sheet — takes an R4 about 45 minutes per weekly refresh for a 100-member alliance. Multiply by 4 weeks per season × 5 seasons a year = ~15 hours per year of R4 time spent doing OCR by hand.

The Roster Extractor gets it done in 90 seconds. If your R4s' time is worth *anything*, this alone is a bigger deal than any single planning tool.
