# KR TikTok adapter — 3 scripts

KR TikTok is a **distinct algorithm** from EN TikTok. Same platform, different behavior:

- **KR audiences prefer faster pacing.** Cut the beats tighter. 2-second beats read as normal in KR content, whereas EN viewers need 3-4.
- **KR hooks are more direct.** Skip the setup — lead with the outcome or the number. "$20에 연맹 전체가 도구 사용" ($20 for whole alliance) reads better than "let me tell you about..."
- **On-screen captions in KR TikTok are mandatory**, not optional. Muted viewership rate is higher in KR than in EN.
- **KR players trust concrete numbers over stories.** "1분당 5,760 석탄" (5,760 coal per minute) lands harder than "your coal runs out fast."
- **Use 한국어 hashtags primarily.** #라스트워, #라스트워서바이벌, #라스트워서바이벌공략 have real reach. English hashtags are secondary.

The 3 scripts below are the KR adaptations of Scripts 1, 3, and 5 — the highest-conversion themes.

Voiceover lines are provided in **romanized Korean** for handoff to a KR-native voice actor or as briefing for a KR translator. Hand these to a real KR speaker for final delivery — machine-generated Korean voiceover is instantly recognizable to KR viewers and destroys credibility.

## KR-specific hashtag reference

Use 4-6 per post. Primary should be KR; add 1-2 EN as spillover.

**High-reach KR:**
- #라스트워 — the base term, largest volume
- #라스트워서바이벌 — full game name
- #라스트워서바이벌공략 — "strategy guide" flavor
- #라스트워공략 — shorter guide variant
- #라스트워팁 — "tips" flavor

**Category KR:**
- #모바일게임 (mobile game — broad)
- #모바일게임공략 (mobile game guide)
- #연맹공략 (alliance strategy)
- #꿀팁 (life-hack, use on Roster Extractor + Coal Burn videos)
- #시즌2 (Season 2 — use during S2 window only)
- #폴라스톰 (Polar Storm — S2 window only)

**Do not use:** #게임 (too broad, no LWS surfacing), #ktok (irrelevant), any generic gaming hashtags — KR TikTok gaming algorithm is niche-hashtag-driven, not broad.

## Handoff notes to the KR translator

- Voiceover MUST be recorded by a native KR speaker. Do not use machine TTS.
- The romanized transcriptions above are approximate — the translator should tune wording to sound conversational, not textbook.
- Keep the same pacing as the EN version: 3s hook, 22s body, 5s CTA. KR phrasing tends to be shorter — that's fine, use the extra room for a beat of silence before the CTA.
- Do NOT translate "RONY-FREE" — it's a code string, must appear in Latin characters exactly.
- Do NOT translate the product name "r5tools.io" — it's a URL, must be pronounceable in KR as "r-five-tools-dot-ai-o."
