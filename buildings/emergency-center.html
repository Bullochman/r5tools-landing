The Emergency Center (ICU) unlocks at HQ 22 and catches the would-be-dead overflow at a starting Fainted Unit Ratio of 21% — meaning if your Hospital caps out, 21% of the overflow is saved (still requires healing) and 79% is permanently lost. Emergency Center Fainted Ratio scales with level. [lastwar.wiki hospital, lastwar.wiki emergency-center, theriagames.com emergency-center]

  1. Warehouse per-level protected amounts. cpt-hedge.com per-building tables (definitive) return 403 to WebFetch. Need per-level protected-food, protected-iron, protected-coin, and protected-oil values for warehouses 1–35.
  2. Does Oil have a dedicated vault? No source cross-fetched confirms whether Oil is protected by the Coin Vault, a separate Oil Vault, or unprotected.
  3. Farmland/Iron Mine full upgrade tables L2–L29 and L31–L34. Only L1, L5, L15, L20, L25, L30, L35 sampled from lastwar.wiki. Interior values must be interpolated or fetched from wiki tables directly.
  4. Gold Mine — is L30 truly the max? No source lists L31+ for Gold Mine even under Age of Oil expansion. Confirm whether Coin production caps at 21,600/hr forever.
  5. True fully-buffed L35 Farmland output. Community reports 90–120K/hr with VIP 8 + Legendary survivor + full economy research, but no single sourced number exists.
  6. Smelter daily throughput reconciliation. 5 maxed Smelters at 60/hr × 24 = 7,200 Ore/day. The "120,000+/day" figure in the search snippet must include event/rewards; needs deconvolution.
  7. Exact Alliance Center L1–L35 curve for max concurrent helps and per-tap seconds shaved. Consensus 15–25 helpers × 60–120s but the level-by-level ramp is unpublished.
  8. Resource chest tier sizes by HQ level. Community says "green +360 per even level / +720 per odd, blue = 10× green" but no source publishes the complete HQ-level → chest-yield table.
  9. World-map gathered resources — protected or exposed on delivery? All guides imply exposed but this is inference.
  10. Cpt-hedge.com fetch access. The definitive per-building numeric tables (Food Warehouse, Iron Warehouse, Coin Vault, plus store/plunder calculators) all return 403 to WebFetch. Needs manual pull or MCP-browser session.
  11. Universal Speedup drop rates from Radar Boxes. "5,000-diamond Yellow Box" is documented, but the per-refresh probability of each speedup-type drop is not.
  12. Trade Truck plunder — how many plunder attempts per day? Sources say "four times a day" for plunder; whether that includes refresh-driven plunders or only initial 4 needs disambiguation.
  13. Gold Bricks farming rate for F2P beyond the 2K one-time drops. Are there recurring event drops that meaningfully add up, or is Gold Brick pure paid currency?
  14. Taylor's shards in the VIP shop — total brick cost to fully recruit. Widely cited as "priority 1" but no source lists the total brick cost.
  15. VIP per-level stat table (mirrors Chief open question #8) — full 1–18 delta on resource-production % / construction % / research % per level.
  16. Emergency Center Fainted Unit Ratio curve. Starts at 21% at L1; no source publishes the L2–L35 ramp.
  17. Coin generation from Taylor (and other passive-economy heroes). Referenced by ldshop.gg brick guide as material to the F2P calculus; the per-hero per-hour coin generation is not published.
  18. Stamina cost of every zombie / rebel tier. Only "20/mission" cited generically; per-Elite-Zombie-tier stamina cost is unpublished.
  19. RSS chest opening formula vs HQ. Chest opens are scaled to HQ level per multiple sources; the exact formula (linear? % of some cap?) is not published.
  20. Auto-Collect timing — several profession skills reference an auto-collect for production buildings; the polling interval and whether it triggers before overflow is not documented.
