Additional S6 mechanics:

  • Alliance Pacts — 7-day non-aggression treaties between same-faction alliances. Prevent City Destruction. Share adjacency benefits and open joint communication channels for up to 200 members. Make faction selection load-bearing.
  • Faction Technology — faction-wide research tree unlocked via Stronghold donations, applies buffs to all faction members after individual contribution thresholds met.
  • War Merit economy — earned via kills, captures, reinforcements, donations, rankings. Tiers Bronze → Elite with corresponding daily stipends and stat bonuses. War Merit Shop trades.
  • Fungus Institute (max ~L60) — enhances Virus Resistance (analog to Optoelectronic Lab / Curse Research Lab).
  • Spore Factory (max L30) — produces Spores.
  • Sanctuary timer buff: doubled from 10 → 20 minutes (occupies 1/3 of the 60-minute attack window). Sources call this a "stealth change" that significantly restricts strategic flexibility.
  • Alliance Skill cooldowns: 3–6 days.
  • Card meta shift: the popular "4+1 hybrid squad strategy" was removed from Global Expedition, forcing pure Tank / Missile / Aircraft formations backed by attribute-specific Tactics Cards.
  • Beneath the Ruins (added 2026-07-15) — Season 6's dedicated minigame, launched in TWO phases [Cpt Hedgehog Beneath the Ruins, 2026-07-15; TheGamesEdge Beneath the Ruins Guide, 2026-07-15]:
  • Solo Mode (Week 1 Day 2+): descending-platformer challenge. Guide your character downward through an ever-deepening stage, avoiding hazards. 1 new stage unlocks per day. Missed days stack — play back-to-back to catch up. Platform types: Regular (safe), Crumbly (breaks after ~1 sec), Spike (1 HP damage on landing — avoid at low HP), Travelators (moving belts — go with the flow, fighting them costs time). HP regenerates when standing on safe platforms. Only left/right movement. Reward: Rainforest Mushrooms (larger on later stages), plus season-exclusive gifts.
  • PvP Duel Mode (Week 2+): competitive descent against another commander, same stage simultaneously, first to bottom wins. No HP regeneration. Entry paid in War Merit deposit up to 500 (or 1,500 with Season Skill). Rewards up to 3,000 War Merit per match [TheGamesEdge, 2026-07-15]. Wager spirit similar to S5's High Noon Duel.
  • Global Expedition — new dedicated building & schedule (added 2026-07-15) — moved out of the Alert Tower into a new dedicated Expedition Base building [LDShop S6 Global Expedition Guide, 2026-07-15]. Three GE rounds per season, each 14 days long (roughly Weeks 2, 4, 6 of the main season). Named Awakening Shards drop at rank thresholds — one of the best regular free shard sources in the game.

A Last War: Survival alliance is a 100-player cooperative unit built on a five-rank hierarchy (R5 → R1), a shared research tree paid in coin and diamond donations, a construction/research "help" mechanic that shaves seconds-to-hours off every teammate's timers, and a geographic clustering pattern ("the hive") that is the substrate for every alliance-level event and defense. The most rewarding alliance activity outside PvP is Marshal's Guard (Alliance Exercise), a 30-minute rally-marathon against five NPC tanks positioned inside a 3×3-tile event platform at the center of the hive (the LWS_Alliance_Ops_Center Hive Grid Manager plans this layout); the largest capturable seasonal objective is the 21×21-tile Military Stronghold, and Ring 1 members must sit against its outer mud to deny enemy landing zones. Alliance Duel (VS) is the recurring 6-day cross-server tournament (Mon–Sat, first to 7 victory points wins) that pairs with the Arms Race event and is the primary way alliances compete for server-level status. R5 assigns the four titled R4 slots — Warlord, Recruiter, Muse, Butler — and in Season 6 these titles gate the four alliance skills (Serpent Breath, Snake Barrier, Night Army, Thunder Feathers). The R4 cap starts at 8 and rises to 10 at Alliance Gift Level 10.

The "hive" is the geographic clustering of alliance bases around alliance buildings (turrets, flag) and event objects (Marshal's Guard platform, Military Stronghold). Hive discipline is one of the most-ignored and most-consequential alliance rules — a scattered alliance loses defensive reinforcement coverage, march-time-limited events like Marshal's Guard, and Zombie Siege reinforcement chains.

  • Player HQ / city: 2×2 tiles per base footprint (per hive-planner tools; note some sources treat member "cells" as 3×3 in planner UIs where one cell = one base + 1-tile buffer).
  • Marshal's Guard platform: 3×3 tiles (1 event cell).
  • Alliance Center (world building placement varies; the on-base structure is 5-tile-footprint per planners): 5×5 tiles.
  • Missile silo (turret/rocket world object): 4×4 tiles.
  • Military Stronghold seasonal fortress: 21×21 tiles (a 7-cell × 7-cell block in a 3-tile-per-cell planner grid).
