  1. Pre-train low-tier troops throughout the week (T7/T8 stockpile).
  2. On Day 5, promote them upward through each Barracks tier (T7→T8→T9→T10). Each promotion counts as a training action → scores 4× training points → an army you already had scores you as though you had trained it fresh at every tier.
  3. Effective yield: ~4× the AD training points of just training T10 directly.
  4. Rule: do NOT spend training speedups on any other day of the AD week. Every training speedup saved from Days 1–4 pays out 4× on Day 5. This is universal top-alliance discipline.

VIP has two axes:

  • VIP Level (1–18) — permanent, earned via VIP Points. Milestones unlock new store slots, extra march queues, extra Barracks, better login rewards.
  • VIP Time — consumable buff (activated by ticks earned via top-up, packs, monthly pass, and small diamond purchase). Percent bonuses only apply while ticking down.

  • Development — construction speed, research speed, Fast Builder, Research Enhancement, Extra Barracks. Badge-free, run continuously.
  • Economy — resource output (food/iron/coin), gathering speed, storage cap. Badge-free, run continuously.
  • Alliance Duel / VS Duel — raises the point-gain and chest-tier rewards you personally get from VS Days. Costs Valor Badges. This is the first Valor Badge sink because higher chest tiers make future badges cheaper.
  • Special Forces — the T10 troop path. Bottom node is Unit X. Requires four gatekeeper prereqs (HP Boost III, Attack Boost III, Defense Boost III, Unit X research itself) totalling >500 million coins.
  • Age of Oil — post-Season-2 late-game branch, gated behind Base Expansion research and HQ 31+.
