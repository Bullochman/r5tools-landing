The overlap with game titles is common — e.g. the Warlord title (+5% MG rally, S6 Serpent Breath) is usually assigned to the same person who acts as War Coordinator. The LWS_Alliance_Ops_Center Ministry Assigner tool (planned per TOOLS_ROADMAP) separates the two dimensions explicitly.
