Last War: Survival's economy runs on four permanent resources — Food, Iron, Coin (gold), and Oil — plus two season-locked resources (Coal + Titanium Alloy in Season 2 Polar Storm) and a ranking resource per season (Rare Soil in S2). The economy is not "electricity" or "bread/water" style; those are community coinages for Food and Coin. Coin is the hidden late-game bottleneck: it becomes required from Gold Mine L9 and Oil becomes required for every HQ upgrade from HQ 31–35. Resources are produced by dedicated buildings (Farmland, Iron Mine, Gold Mine, Oil Well, Smelter) that scale from ~1,440/hr at L1 to ~33,840/hr at L30 for basic resources and cap at 12 h of unclaimed production (39,600/hr at L35 for Farmland and Iron Mine, 21,600/hr at L30 for Gold Mine, 1,800/hr at L30 for Oil Well). Warehouses (Food Warehouse, Iron Warehouse, Coin Vault) define a protected threshold — anything at or below is unraidable; anything above is exposed to plunder proportional to the attacker's troop load capacity and the target's stored ratio (see 01-core-gameplay for warehouse building basics and 04-squads-combat for load-capacity mechanics). On top of raw resources sit 6 speedup types (universal, construction, research, training, healing, hero-medal), the Diamond premium currency (used for VIP time, stamina, shields, VIP-shop hero shards), and Gold Bricks — a super-premium tier above diamonds gated behind top-up. Every serious player runs continuous Development + Economy research, does 30 daily 20-min coin donations to the Recommended alliance tech, dispatches 4 Intercity Trade Trucks/day, and hoards speedups for Arms Race + Alliance Duel phase overlap so a single build completion double-scores.

  1. Food (식량 / sikryang) — produced by Farmland. Consumed by every building upgrade, every troop training queue, and every healing action. Community shorthand "bread" or "water" is nonstandard — the in-game English label is simply Food. [lastwar.wiki, lastwargame.online, lastwarhub.com]
  2. Iron (강철 / gangcheol, sometimes 철 / cheol) — produced by Iron Mine. Same universal consumer profile as Food; usually the second-largest expense after Food for building upgrades. [lastwar.wiki iron-mine page]
  3. Coin / Gold (금화 / geumhwa) — produced by Gold Mine. First becomes required at Gold Mine building level 9, and is the primary bottleneck for T10/T11 troop training in late game because the coin cost per elite recruit dwarfs the food+iron cost. [lastwar.wiki gold-mine, lastwarhandbook.com resource-management] Coin is the sink most veteran R5s report as their limiting reagent — food/iron overflow, coin runs dry.
  4. Oil (석유 / seogyu) — introduced with the Age of Oil research tree post-Season-2, produced by Oil Wells (up to 3 concurrent). Becomes mandatory as a tertiary resource for HQ 31→35 upgrades. Even a coin-and-food-rich account is stalled at HQ 30→31 without accumulated oil. [lastwartutorial.com Age of Oil, lastwarhandbook.com Age of Oil, cpt-hedge.com Oil Well]

Levels 31–35 for Farmland and Iron Mine unlock only after the HQ Expansion research in the Age of Oil tree. Gold Mine's max is L30 in every published table; there is no confirmed L31–35 for the Gold Mine.

  1. Assigned Survivor — every production building has a survivor slot. A purple (Epic) survivor gives roughly +32% output; Legendary survivors more; star-level upgrades amplify further. A well-tuned Iron Mine L23 with a strong Legendary survivor "can produce over twice" its 16,560/hr base rate. [tap-guides.com, lootbar.com]
  2. VIP Time boosts — while VIP Time is active, VIP-level-based percent bonuses apply. VIP 8 unlocks +20% Food / Iron / Gold production; higher levels stack more. VIP Time is a consumable buff, not a permanent unlock — the level is permanent but the bonus only applies while VIP Time is running. [ldshop.gg VIP guide, packsify.com]
  3. Economy Research nodes — the Food Production, Iron Production, Coin Production, and later Oil Production research nodes stack additive % boosts. Individually small (couple percent per rank) but the sum across all economy nodes plus survivor plus VIP can more than triple base output at endgame. [packsify.com research-guide, ldshop.gg tech-tree-guide]
  • Oil Well unlocks first via the "Unlock Oil Well" research. L1 → L30 spans ~49 days total build time at base values (before Development speed buffs), consuming ~212M Food, 638M Iron, 137M Gold at max.
  • Oil becomes required for HQ 31, HQ 32, HQ 33, HQ 34, HQ 35 upgrades. Without oil, everything else is stalled.
  • Farmland and Iron Mine unlock L31–35 only after "HQ Expansion" completes in the Age of Oil tree.
  • 2nd and 3rd Oil Wells unlock via mid/late Age of Oil research.
  • Reaching HQ 35 F2P is late Season 4 at the earliest — 6–8 months of focused progression.

  5. True fully-buffed L35 Farmland output. Community reports 90–120K/hr with VIP 8 + Legendary survivor + full economy research, but no single sourced number exists.
  6. Smelter daily throughput reconciliation. 5 maxed Smelters at 60/hr × 24 = 7,200 Ore/day. The "120,000+/day" figure in the search snippet must include event/rewards; needs deconvolution.

  8. Resource chest tier sizes by HQ level. Community says "green +360 per even level / +720 per odd, blue = 10× green" but no source publishes the complete HQ-level → chest-yield table.
  9. World-map gathered resources — protected or exposed on delivery? All guides imply exposed but this is inference.

  13. Gold Bricks farming rate for F2P beyond the 2K one-time drops. Are there recurring event drops that meaningfully add up, or is Gold Brick pure paid currency?

  15. VIP per-level stat table (mirrors Chief open question #8) — full 1–18 delta on resource-production % / construction % / research % per level.
