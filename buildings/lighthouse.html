Last War: Survival runs on an 8-week seasonal treadmill: every server rotates through a fresh themed map with new resources, buildings, PvP events, and hero/weapon rollouts. Since Season 2 (Polar Storm) ended, First Fun has shipped four full seasons — S3 Golden Kingdom (Egyptian desert / Spice Wars / Curse Resistance), S4 Evernight Isle (Sakura island / Lighthouse / Blood Night), S5 Wild West (Golden Wasteland / Banks / Trade Train / merged 8-warzone map), and S6 Shadow Rainforest (faction warfare / permanent city destruction / Hero Awakening). Season 7 is confirmed by community creators as launching approximately August 2026 but no official theme/mechanic details have leaked publicly at the time of this research (2026-07-14). Each season introduces a new seasonal resource loop, a new PvP conflict pattern, and a designated hero for UR ascension or Awakening — and the trend from S5→S6 is toward fewer, longer war windows and permanent, high-stakes territorial outcomes rather than continuous grind (documented in 13-meta-competitive).

Signature building: The Lighthouse. Central power system storing electricity and pushing back darkness. Requires 4 zombie-powered generators (Optoelectronic Lab levels 1, 3, 10, 15), each operated by an Electrician. Features:

  • 4 brightness levels (L1–L4)
  • Power management system
  • Auto-adjustment toggle for conservation

Lighthouse — official brightness bonuses per level [lastwartutorial, 2026-07-15]:

  • L1: +50 Virus Resistance
  • L2: +150 Virus Resistance
  • L3: +250 Virus Resistance, +3% Morale
  • L4: +250 Virus Resistance, +5% Morale
  • L4 max brightness requires Optoelectronic Lab L20+.

  • 02-heroes — Every season promotes one SSR to UR. Since S6, three heroes per season also gain Hero Awakening. Named vs Universal Awakening Shards are the new currency; hero tier-list volatility now depends on which heroes are in the Awakening rotation.
  • 03-chief — Chief profession cap extended from 60 → 100 in S3, retained through S6. Diplomat unavailable in S3/S4 (unconfirmed if unlocked later). New seasonal skills (Sandworm Traps, Trap Landmines, Nature's Touch, etc.) tie to season-specific PvE.
  • 04-squads-combat — Tactics Cards debuted in S4; card meta shifted hard in S6 with the removal of the 4+1 hybrid squad strategy from Global Expedition, forcing pure Tank/Missile/Aircraft formations. Curse Resistance / Virus Resistance stats are new gate mechanics for PvE damage that stack over standard stats.
  • 05-season-1-crimson-plague — S1 Crimson Plague introduced T10 troops. Season structure since S3 has become more elaborate but retains the S1 template of themed map + seasonal resources + Capitol/faction endgame.
  • 06-season-2-frozen — S2 Polar Storm was the first true themed environmental hazard (cold/heat). S3+ retained the "seasonal building trinity" model (resource-producer + shop-currency-producer + resistance-lab). Alliance Furnace pattern from S2 evolves into Blessing Fountain (S3) / Lighthouse (S4) / Bank Stronghold (S5) / Altars (S6). See STYLE_GUIDE for how the LWS_Alliance_Ops_Center suite handles seasonal building overlays.
  • 08-alliance-systems — Alliance Pacts (S6), Alliance Safe Time (S5+), and Faction Technology (S6) reshape how alliances coordinate at the meta level. Alliance city/stronghold caps evolve per season (S3 = 8/8; S4 = 6/6; S5 Bank = 4 default up to 12; S6 factional).
  • 09-events — Blood Night Descend (S4, 02:30/10:30/18:30 server), Sandworm Crisis (S3, 14 days), Spice Wars (S3, biweekly), Copper Wars (S4, Wed/Sat), Trade Train (S5), Altar Tuesdays (S6), Faction Duel (season-ending). Each season adds 2–3 new recurring events.
  • 10-economy — Season Battle Pass (~48 days, ~€11.99 Advanced / ~€23.99 Luxury) is the shipped pricing anchor since S3. Weekly Pass adds a temporary seasonal building + resources. Three exclusive weapon passes per season (Weeks 1/3/6). Hero Awakening shards are now a paid axis on top of exclusive weapons.
  • 14-ui-reference — Season transitions require map re-render, seasonal building rendering, and faction color coding (S6 Deepwood green / Wetland blue / Great River neutral). Alliance Safe Time UI added in S5.
  • 15-glossary — Every season has full KR terminology; namu.wiki maintains a running Korean-language reference. Faction names (Kage no Sato, Koubutai in S4; Deepwood/Wetland in S6) are transliterated from EN in KR sources rather than re-localized.
