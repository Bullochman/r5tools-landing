  • Avatar frames — Cosmetic frames on the player avatar. Sources include Season events, Console Contest (S6 champion frames), Faction War ranks, monthly pass, and the Gold Brick Radiance event. Frames are largely time-limited (often 7-day expiries on competition rewards).
  • Titles / Chief tags — Awarded through event rankings (e.g. Alliance Duel VS top rankers), faction leadership positions, and top-100 Kill Event rankers on a server. No published "collector list."
  • Valor Badges — Not a title. This is a tech-research currency for the Tech Center's Alliance Duel, Special Forces (T10 path), Intercity Truck, Siege Warfare, and Defense Fortification research trees. Sources: Alliance Duel VS event chests (50→400 badges), Warzone VS victories (~200), Glittering Market. Costs range from 50 badges (early nodes) to 20,000+ (late Special Forces). This is what most EN "chief badge" search results actually surface.
  • Skill Medals — Not chief-owned; hero-owned currency used to level hero skills. Sourced primarily from Arms Race (up to 8,000/day at gold-chest ceiling). Skill medals refund fully when a hero is UR-promoted (SSR → UR), enabling free re-allocation.
